## Important Formulas and Theorems

- **Mean and Median**
  - Mean: μ = (Σx) / n
  - Median: m = Median({x})
- **Standard Deviation**
  - σ = √((Σ(x-μ)^2) / n)
- **Correlation Coefficient**
  - ρ = Cov(X, Y) / (σ_X \* σ_Y)

## Key Definitions

- **Data Science**: Interdisciplinary field that extracts insights and knowledge from structured and unstructured data.
- **Machine Learning**: Subfield of Data Science that involves training algorithms to make predictions or decisions on new, unseen data.
- **Python**: Popular programming language used for Data Science, Machine Learning, and Data Analysis.
