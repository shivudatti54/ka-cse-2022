**Why Process Management Matters:**
Learning process management is crucial in operating systems as it enables the creation, execution, and synchronization of multiple tasks or programs running concurrently on a computer. Understanding process management is essential for designing efficient and scalable operating systems that can handle various workloads and applications. It also ensures that resources are utilized optimally, reducing the risk of system crashes and improving overall system performance. Effective process management is critical in today's fast-paced computing environments.

**Real-World Applications:**
Process management has numerous real-world applications in various industries, including computer networks, distributed systems, and mobile devices. In these environments, process management enables the creation of concurrent programs, improves resource utilization, and ensures system reliability. For instance, in a web server, process management is used to handle multiple requests simultaneously, ensuring fast and responsive service to users.

**Connection to Other Concepts:**
Process management is closely related to other operating system concepts, such as memory management, synchronization, and resource allocation. Understanding process management is essential for comprehending how these concepts work together to create a stable and efficient operating system. Additionally, process management is interconnected with programming concepts, such as concurrency and parallel processing, which are used to develop efficient and scalable software applications.
