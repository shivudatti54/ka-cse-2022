Establishing the rules for building trustworthy AI is crucial because it provides a framework for developers to create AI systems that are transparent, accountable, and respectful of human values. This topic matters because it addresses the growing concerns about AI's potential impact on society, including issues of bias, privacy, and safety. In real-world applications, such as healthcare, finance, and education, AI systems must adhere to these rules to ensure they are reliable and trustworthy. This concept is connected to other ideas in AI ethics, such as fairness, accountability, and transparency.
