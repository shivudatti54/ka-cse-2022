Learning "Chapter 9" on Ethics and Public Policy for AI is crucial as it addresses the pressing issues of AI's impact on society, particularly its potential to exacerbate existing social inequalities and create new risks. This topic matters because it highlights the importance of considering the broader societal implications of AI development and deployment, rather than solely focusing on technical aspects. In real-world applications, understanding these ethics is essential for developing AI systems that are fair, transparent, and accountable. This chapter connects to other concepts, such as the responsible use of AI, data governance, and human-centered design.
