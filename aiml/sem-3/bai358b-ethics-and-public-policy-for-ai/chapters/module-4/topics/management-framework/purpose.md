# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Explain the concept of AI governance and its significance in financial services operations

2. Describe the five-pillar risk management framework for AI systems in banking and finance

3. Identify and differentiate between various algorithmic fairness metrics used in financial applications

4. Analyze potential sources of bias in credit scoring, fraud detection, and insurance pricing models

5. Apply regulatory compliance requirements from RBI, SEBI, and international frameworks to AI deployments

6. Design a basic bias detection and mitigation strategy for a given financial AI scenario

7. Evaluate the effectiveness of different model monitoring techniques for deployed AI systems

8. Synthesize governance policies that ensure accountability and transparency in AI-driven financial decisions
