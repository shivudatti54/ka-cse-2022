### Learning Purpose: Test Components for AI Ethics & Policy

**1. Why is this topic important?**
Testing is a critical safeguard in the development of ethical AI. Without rigorous evaluation, AI systems can deploy with hidden biases, security flaws, and unforeseen harmful behaviours, posing significant risks to individuals and society. This topic ensures that theoretical ethical principles are translated into verifiable, practical outcomes.

**2. What will students learn?**
Students will learn the frameworks and methodologies for constructing effective test components. This includes designing audits for fairness and bias, creating red-team scenarios for security and alignment, developing real-world simulation environments, and establishing metrics for transparency and accountability.

**3. How does it connect to other concepts?**
This topic directly operationalizes concepts from across the module. It applies ethical principles (Module 1), implements governance and compliance requirements (Module 3), and utilizes technical knowledge of AI systems (Module 2) to build concrete evaluation procedures that ensure policies are effective and enforceable.

**4. Real-world applications**
These skills are essential for roles in AI auditing, compliance, and risk management. Practitioners use these methods to evaluate loan approval algorithms for bias, stress-test autonomous vehicle decision-making, and ensure public-facing chatbots operate within ethical guidelines before deployment.
