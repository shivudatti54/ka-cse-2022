# **Text Book 1: 1.9 - Digital Design and Computer Organization**

## **Key Points:**

- Introduction to logical gates and their applications
- Types of logical gates:
  - AND gate
  - OR gate
  - NOT gate (Inverter)
  - NAND gate
  - NOR gate
- Digital logic circuits:
  - Sequential logic
  - Combinational logic
- Digital design principles:
  - Binary arithmetic
  - Boolean algebra

## **Important Formulas and Definitions:**

- Boolean algebra:
  - A \* B = AB (AND gate)
  - A \+ B = AB (OR gate)
  - \~A = A' (NOT gate)
  - A \* \~A = 0 (Complement law)
- Digital arithmetic:
  - Binary addition and subtraction
  - Binary multiplication

## **Important Theorems:**

- De Morgan's law:
  - \~(A \* B) = \~A + \~B
  - \~(A \+ B) = \~A \* \~B
- Karnaugh map:
  - Used for simplifying Boolean expressions

## **Quick Revision Tips:**

- Understand the types of logical gates and their applications
- Learn the digital logic circuits and principles
- Practice solving digital arithmetic problems
