### Learning Purpose: Continuous Internal Evaluation (CIE)

1.  **Why is this topic important?**
    CIE shifts the focus from a single high-stakes exam to a continuous, holistic assessment of a student's understanding. It reduces exam stress, provides regular feedback for improvement, and ensures that learning is an ongoing process rather than a last-minute effort.

2.  **What will students learn?**
    Students will learn the philosophy and mechanisms behind CIE, including various assessment methods (quizzes, assignments, presentations, projects). They will understand how to track their own progress, interpret feedback, and develop consistent study habits to achieve long-term mastery of Data Structures.

3.  **How does it connect to other concepts?**
    CIE is intrinsically linked to every module in the course. It provides the framework for assessing understanding of core concepts (like arrays, linked lists, trees, and graphs) and their algorithms. It ensures that foundational topics are firmly grasped before moving on to more complex, interconnected data structures.

4.  **Real-world applications**
    The CIE model mirrors modern professional environments where performance is continuously evaluated through projects, code reviews, and sprint deliverables. It prepares students for a career where consistent quality, meeting deadlines, and iterative improvement are valued over a one-time test.
