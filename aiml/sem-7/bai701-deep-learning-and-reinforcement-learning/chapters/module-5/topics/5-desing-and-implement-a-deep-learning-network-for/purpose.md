**Why this topic matters:**
Learning to classify textual documents is crucial in today's data-driven world, as it enables applications such as sentiment analysis, spam detection, and text classification for customer service. This skill is essential for organizations to make informed decisions based on large amounts of unstructured data. By mastering this topic, individuals can unlock the value of unstructured text data and drive business insights.

**Real-world applications:**
This topic has numerous real-world applications, including:

- Sentiment analysis for customer service chatbots
- Spam detection in emails and social media
- Text classification for medical diagnosis

**How it connects to other concepts:**
Deep learning and natural language processing (NLP) are closely tied to reinforcement learning, as both topics involve learning from data and making predictions based on that data. This topic also connects to multi-armed bandits, as both involve making decisions based on uncertainty and exploring the most promising options.
