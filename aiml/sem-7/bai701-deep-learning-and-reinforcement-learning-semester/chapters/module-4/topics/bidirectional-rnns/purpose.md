# Learning Objectives
After studying this topic, you should be able to:
1. Explain the fundamental concept and architecture of Bidirectional RNNs
2. Differentiate between unidirectional and bidirectional RNNs in terms of capabilities and limitations
3. Describe the mathematical formulation of forward and backward passes in Bi-RNNs
4. Identify appropriate applications for Bidirectional RNNs based on their characteristics
5. Compare different strategies for combining forward and backward hidden states
6. Implement a basic Bidirectional RNN using deep learning frameworks
