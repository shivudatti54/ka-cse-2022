Here are the explanations:

**Why this topic matters:**
Learning key management fundamentals is crucial in today's data-driven world, as it enables individuals and organizations to protect sensitive information from unauthorized access. Effective key management ensures the confidentiality, integrity, and authenticity of data, making it a vital aspect of data security and privacy.

**Real-world applications:**
Key management is essential in various real-world applications, such as online banking, e-commerce, cloud computing, and cybersecurity. It is also critical in industries like healthcare, finance, and government, where sensitive information is handled. Moreover, key management is used in IoT devices, smart home systems, and other connected devices.

**How it connects to other concepts:**
Key management is closely related to other concepts in data security and privacy, such as encryption, authentication, and access control. It is also linked to concepts like risk management, security policies, and compliance with regulations like GDPR and HIPAA. Effective key management can be achieved by combining it with other security measures, such as multi-factor authentication and encryption, to ensure comprehensive data protection.
