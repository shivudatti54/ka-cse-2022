# Chapter 11: Data Security and Privacy

### Important Definitions

- **Confidentiality**: The principle of protecting sensitive information from unauthorized access.
- **Data Security**: The process of protecting data from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, disruption, modification, or destruction.
- **Data Privacy**: The right to control one's personal information and have it protected from unauthorized disclosure or use.

### Key Concepts

- **Data Protection Laws**: Laws regulating the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data, such as GDPR, HIPAA, and CCPA.
- **Encryption**: The process of converting plaintext into unreadable ciphertext to protect data from unauthorized access.
- **Access Control**: The process of controlling who has access to data, systems, or applications.
- **Authentication**: The process of verifying the identity of users, devices, or systems.
- **Authorization**: The process of determining what actions a user, device, or system can perform.

### Important Formulas and Theorems

- **Data Integrity**: The theorem stating that data should be modified only by authorized personnel and that data should be kept consistent across different systems.
- **Data Confidentiality**: The theorem stating that data should be protected from unauthorized access and disclosure.

### Key Security Measures

- **Firewalls**: Network security systems that monitor and control incoming and outgoing network traffic.
- **Intrusion Detection Systems**: Systems that monitor network traffic for signs of unauthorized access or malicious activity.
- **Virus Scanners**: Software that scans data for malware and viruses.

### Important Privacy Concepts

- **Data Minimization**: The principle of collecting and processing only the minimum amount of personal data necessary to achieve a specific purpose.
- **Data Retention**: The practice of storing personal data for a limited period of time before deleting it.
- **Right to Erasure**: The right of individuals to request the deletion of their personal data.

This summary provides key points for quick revision before exams, covering important definitions, concepts, formulas, theorems, and security measures related to data security and privacy.
