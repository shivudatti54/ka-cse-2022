The topic of Database Management System (DBMS) is crucial as it enables organizations to efficiently store, manage, and retrieve vast amounts of data, making it a vital skill for professionals in various industries. In the real world, DBMS is used in applications such as online banking, e-commerce, and healthcare, where accurate data management is essential. This topic connects to other concepts such as data modeling, data normalization, and data security, which are all integral to a comprehensive understanding of information systems.
