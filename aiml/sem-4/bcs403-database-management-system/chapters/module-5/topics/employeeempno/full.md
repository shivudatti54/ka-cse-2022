## **Attributes and Relationships**

An Employee (EMPNO) record typically contains the following attributes:

- **EMPNO (Employee Number)**: A unique identifier for the employee.
- **NAME**: The employee's full name.
- **TITLE**: The employee's job title.
- **DEPARTMENT**: The department the employee belongs to.
- **REPORTS_TO**: The manager or supervisor of the employee.
- **SALARY**: The employee's salary or compensation.
- **BENEFITS**: The employee's benefits, such as health insurance or retirement plans.
- **HOURS_WORKED**: The number of hours the employee has worked.

Employee (EMPNO) records are typically related to other entities, such as:

- **Departments**: Employee (EMPNO) records are often related to departments, which are used to store department-specific information.
