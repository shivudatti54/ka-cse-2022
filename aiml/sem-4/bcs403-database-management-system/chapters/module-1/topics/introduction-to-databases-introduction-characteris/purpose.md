**Introduction to Databases Topic Overview**

**Why this topic matters:** Learning the fundamentals of databases is crucial in today's data-driven world, as it enables individuals to design, implement, and manage complex data systems that support various applications and industries. Understanding databases is essential for careers in IT, business, and data science.

**Real-world applications:** Database management systems (DBMS) are used in various sectors, such as healthcare (patient records), finance (account management), e-commerce (order tracking), and social media (user data). Effective database management is critical for organizations to make informed decisions and optimize operations.

**Connection to other concepts:** The understanding of databases connects to other concepts in computer science, such as data modeling, data mining, and data visualization. Additionally, database management is closely related to programming languages, such as SQL and Python, which are used to interact with and manipulate database systems.

**Module Overview**

This 8-hour module provides a comprehensive introduction to databases, covering the following topics:

1. Introduction to Databases: Definition, types, and applications of databases.
2. Characteristics of Database Approach: Advantages of using a database approach, including data normalization and security.
3. Advantages of using the DBMS approach: Overview of DBMS features, such as data modeling, data storage, and data retrieval.
4. History of database applications: Evolution of database technology and notable milestones.

By completing this module, students will gain a solid understanding of databases and their importance in today's technology landscape.
