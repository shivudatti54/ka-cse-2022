# Teaching-Learning Process: Chalk and Talk Method / PowerPoint Presentation

### Overview

- The chalk and talk method involves lecturing in front of a class, writing on the board, and explaining concepts.
- PowerPoint presentations can enhance the chalk and talk method by providing visual aids and engaging multimedia content.

### Key Points

- **Chalk and Talk Method:**
  - Advantages: promotes student participation, clarifies complex concepts, and builds instructor-student rapport.
  - Disadvantages: instructor dominance, limited student engagement, and potential for misunderstandings.
- **PowerPoint Presentations:**
  - Advantages: engaging visuals, easy to modify, and accessible to a wider audience.
  - Disadvantages: distractions, info overload, and lack of student interaction.
- **Effective Strategies:**
  - Encourage student participation and questions.
  - Use visual aids to illustrate complex concepts.
  - Provide opportunities for students to practice and apply what they have learned.
