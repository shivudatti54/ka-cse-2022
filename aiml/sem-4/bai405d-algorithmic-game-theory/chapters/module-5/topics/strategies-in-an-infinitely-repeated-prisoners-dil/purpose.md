By analyzing strategies in an infinitely repeated Prisoner's dilemma, researchers can better comprehend the dynamics of cooperative behavior and strategic decision-making in various social and economic contexts.

The concepts developed from this topic have numerous real-world applications, such as in business, politics, and international relations.
