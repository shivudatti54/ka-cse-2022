**Topic Overview: (8 hours) Teaching-Learning Process Chalk and Talk Method / PowerPoint Presentation**

**1. Why this topic matters:**
The teaching-learning process is crucial in game theory, as it enables students to understand strategic interactions and develop critical thinking skills. Mastering the chalk and talk method and PowerPoint presentation skills is essential in conveying complex concepts to students and helping them grasp the intricacies of game theory.

**2. Real-world applications:**
This topic is relevant in various fields, including economics, politics, and social sciences, where strategic decision-making is critical. Effective communication and presentation skills are vital in these contexts, allowing individuals to present and analyze game-theoretic models, negotiate, and make informed decisions.

**3. Connection to other concepts:**
Understanding the teaching-learning process and presentation skills is closely linked to other game theory concepts, such as mixed strategy Nash equilibrium and game theory in strategic interactions. By mastering these skills, students can better comprehend and apply game-theoretic models to real-world scenarios, enhancing their analytical and problem-solving abilities.
