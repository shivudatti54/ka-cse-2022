# **Teaching-Learning Process: Chalk and Talk Method / PowerPoint Presentation**

### Overview

- The chalk and talk method involves a lecturer explaining a topic to a class using a lecture format.
- PowerPoint presentation is a commonly used tool for the chalk and talk method.

### Key Points

- **Chalk and Talk Method:**
  - Advantages:
    - Encourages active learning
    - Fosters discussion and Q&A
    - Can be used for large classes
  - Disadvantages:
    - May not engage students with visual aids
    - Can be time-consuming
- **PowerPoint Presentation:**
  - Advantages:
    - Enhances engagement with visual aids
    - Allows for easy organization and notes
    - Can be used for remote learning
  - Disadvantages:
    - Can be distracting if not used effectively
    - May not encourage active learning

### Important Formulas, Definitions, and Theorems

- **Bayesian Game:** A game where the outcome depends on the actions of multiple players, and the decision-making process involves updating beliefs based on new information.
- **Mixed Strategy:** A strategy that combines multiple actions to achieve a desired outcome, often used in game theory.
- **Expected Utility:** The average outcome of a decision, taking into account the probability of each outcome.

### Teaching-Learning Process

- **Initial Stage:** Lecture and introduction to the topic
- **Active Learning Stage:** Participants engage with the material through discussions, group work, and activities
- **Transfer Stage:** Participants apply what they have learned to new situations and challenges
