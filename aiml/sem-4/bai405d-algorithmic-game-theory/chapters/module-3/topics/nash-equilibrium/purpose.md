By analyzing Nash equilibrium, individuals can make informed decisions that maximize their payoffs while minimizing the payoffs of others.

It is also used in economics to analyze the behavior of firms and individuals in different market structures. Additionally, it is used in artificial intelligence to develop more sophisticated decision-making algorithms.
