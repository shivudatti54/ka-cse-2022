**Teaching-Learning Process: Chalk and Talk Method / PowerPoint Presentation**

**1. Why this topic matters:**
The topic of teaching-learning process in metric spaces is crucial as it enables students to understand the fundamental concepts and relationships within this mathematical framework, ultimately leading to better comprehension and application of mathematical ideas.

**2. Real-world applications:**
The concepts learned from metric spaces have numerous real-world applications, such as geometry, trigonometry, and computer science, where distances and spatial relationships are essential. For instance, in geography, understanding metric spaces helps predict distances and travel times between locations.

**3. Connection to other concepts:**
Metric spaces are closely related to other mathematical concepts, such as topology, analysis, and algebra. A strong grasp of metric spaces provides a solid foundation for exploring these interconnected areas, enabling students to build upon their knowledge and develop a deeper understanding of mathematical structures.
