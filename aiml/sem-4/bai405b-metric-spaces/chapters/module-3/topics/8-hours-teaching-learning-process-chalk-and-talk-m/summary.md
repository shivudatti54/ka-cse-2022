# **Teaching-Learning Process: Chalk and Talk Method / PowerPoint Presentation**

## **Key Points**

- **Chalk and Talk Method:**
  - Advantages:
    - Encourages active participation and engagement
    - Allows for immediate feedback and clarification
  - Disadvantages:
    - Time-consuming
    - May not be suitable for large classes
- **PowerPoint Presentation:**
  - Advantages:
    - Time-efficient
    - Visual aids help to illustrate complex concepts
  - Disadvantages:
    - May not encourage active participation
    - Requires technical expertise

## **Important Formulas and Definitions**

- **Complete Metric Space:** A metric space (X, d) is said to be complete if every Cauchy sequence in X converges to a point in X.
- **Cauchy Sequence:** A sequence {x_n} in a metric space (X, d) is said to be Cauchy if for every positive real number ε, there exists a positive integer N such that d(x_n, x_m) < ε for all n, m > N.

## **Theorems**

- **Heine-Borel Theorem:** A subset K of a metric space (X, d) is compact if and only if it is closed and bounded.
- **Bolzano-Weierstrass Theorem:** Every bounded sequence in a metric space (X, d) has a convergent subsequence.

## **Quick Revision Tips**

- Review key concepts and definitions
- Practice problems and exercises
- Review notes and textbook material
- Use flashcards to reinforce important formulas and theorems
