# **Teaching-Learning Process: Chalk and Talk Method/PowerPoint Presentation**

## **Key Points**

- **Chalk and Talk Method**
  - Lecture-style teaching
  - Instructor speaks while writing on the blackboard
  - Students listen and take notes
  - Limited opportunities for student participation
- **PowerPoint Presentation**
  - Visual aids used to support instruction
  - Often used in conjunction with lectures
  - Can be more engaging than traditional lectures
  - Can be used to present complex information in a clear and concise manner

## **Key Concepts**

- **Engagement**: The degree to which students are interested and motivated in the learning process
- **Interactivity**: The degree to which students are involved in the learning process
- **Relevance**: The degree to which the material is connected to students' lives and interests

## **Teaching-Learning Process**

- **Pre-Teaching**: Preparation for the lesson
- **Teaching**: The actual delivery of the lesson
- **Learning**: The process of students acquiring new knowledge and skills
- **Post-Teaching**: Evaluation and assessment of the lesson
