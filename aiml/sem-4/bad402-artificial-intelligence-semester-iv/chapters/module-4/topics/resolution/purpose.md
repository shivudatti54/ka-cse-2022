# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Explain the resolution rule and how it applies to both propositional and first-order logic
2. Convert logical expressions to conjunctive normal form (CNF) for resolution
3. Apply resolution refutation to prove logical entailments
4. Implement unification for first-order resolution problems
5. Identify appropriate resolution strategies for different scenarios
6. Compare resolution with other inference methods and understand its advantages and limitations
