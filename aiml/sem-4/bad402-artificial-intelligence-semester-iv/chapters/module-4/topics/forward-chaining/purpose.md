# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define forward chaining and explain its role in logical inference
2. Differentiate between forward chaining and backward chaining approaches
3. Apply forward chaining to derive conclusions from a set of facts and rules in First-Order Logic
4. Perform unification operations between logical expressions
5. Identify suitable applications for forward chaining in AI systems
6. Recognize the limitations and efficiency considerations of forward chaining
