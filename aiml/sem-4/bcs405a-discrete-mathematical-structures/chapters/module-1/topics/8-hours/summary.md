### Time and Scheduling

- Definition: Time is a measure of the duration of an event or activity.
- Important concept: 8 hours = 1 day
- Formula: 1 day = 8 hours

### Basic Concepts

- Definition: A set of discrete, distinct objects or events.
- Types of sets:
  - Finite sets
  - Infinite sets
  - Empty set (∅)
- Important concept: Set operations (union, intersection, difference)

### Set Theory

- Definition: A mathematical structure consisting of a set and its operations.
- Important formulas:
  - Union: A ∪ B = {a, b} (where a ∈ A and b ∈ B)
  - Intersection: A ∩ B = {a} (where a ∈ A and a ∈ B)
  - Difference: A \ B = {a} (where a ∈ A and a ∉ B)
- Important theorem: Axiom of Extensionality

### Boolean Algebra

- Definition: A mathematical structure consisting of binary operations.
- Important formulas:
  - Boolean operations: ∧ (AND), ∨ (OR), ¬ (NOT)
  - Laws of Boolean algebra: Distributive law, Commutative law, Associative law
- Important theorem: De Morgan's laws

### Logic

- Definition: A branch of mathematics that deals with reasoning and inference.
- Important concepts:
  - Propositions
  - Statements
  - Inferences
- Important theorem: Syllogism
