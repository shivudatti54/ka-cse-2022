# **Fundamental Principles of Counting: Revision Notes**

## **Introduction**

This summary covers the key concepts of counting principles, including the rules of sum and product, permutations, combinations, the binomial theorem, and combinations with repetition.

## **Rules of Sum and Product**

- **Rule of Sum**: The number of ways to perform two or more independent tasks is the product of the number of ways to perform each task.
  - Formula: n1 × n2 × ... × nk
- **Rule of Product**: The number of ways to perform one or more tasks is the sum of the number of ways to perform each task.
  - Formula: n1 + n2 + ... + nk

## **Permutations**

- **Permutation**: The number of ways to arrange objects in a specific order.
  - Formula: n! (n factorial)
- **Permutation Formula**: nPr = n! / (n-r)!

## **Combinations**

- **Combinations**: The number of ways to choose objects without regard to order.
  - Formula: nCr = n! / (r!(n-r)!)
- **Combinations Formula**: nCr = n! / (r!(n-r)!)

## **The Binomial Theorem**

- **The Binomial Theorem**: A fundamental theorem in algebra that describes the expansion of binomial expressions.
  - Formula: (a + b)^n = Σ (n choose k) a^(n-k) b^k
- **Binomial Theorem Formula**: (a + b)^n = Σ (n choose k) a^(n-k) b^k

## **Combinations with Repetition**

- **Combinations with Repetition**: The number of ways to choose objects with repetition allowed.
  - Formula: (n+r-1) choose r
- **Combinations with Repetition Formula**: (n+r-1) choose r = (n+r-1)! / (r!(n-1)!)

## **Important Formulas and Theorems**

- nPr = n! / (n-r)!
- nCr = n! / (r!(n-r)!)
- (a + b)^n = Σ (n choose k) a^(n-k) b^k
- (n+r-1) choose r = (n+r-1)! / (r!(n-1)!)
