# Learning Objectives
After studying this topic, you should be able to:
1. Understand the concept of the pumping lemma and its purpose.
2. Apply the pumping lemma to prove that a language is not regular.
3. Identify the relationship between the pumping lemma and the regularity of a language.
4. Provide examples of languages that are not regular.
5. Explain the consequence of the pumping lemma.
6. Use the pumping lemma to determine whether a language is regular or not.
