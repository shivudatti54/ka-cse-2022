# Learning Objectives
After studying this topic, you should be able to:
1. Define what a regular expression is and explain its primary use.
2. Write regular expressions to match different patterns.
3. Explain the purpose of the ^ character and the {8,} quantifier in regular expressions.
4. Compare and contrast different regular expression flavors.
5. Use regular expressions to solve real-world problems.
6. Explain the importance of regular expressions in computer science and other fields.
