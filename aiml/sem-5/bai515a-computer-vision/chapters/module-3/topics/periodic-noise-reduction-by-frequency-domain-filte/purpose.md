**1. Why this topic matters:**
Learning periodic noise reduction by frequency domain filtering is crucial in computer vision as it enables the effective removal of unwanted patterns and noise from images, resulting in improved image quality and accuracy. This technique is essential in various applications, including image restoration, de-noising, and super-resolution. By mastering this topic, researchers and practitioners can develop robust image processing algorithms that can handle complex degradation patterns.

**2. Real-world applications:**
Periodic noise reduction by frequency domain filtering has numerous real-world applications in computer vision, including:

- Image de-noising in medical imaging, such as MRI and CT scans
- Removing artifacts from digital cameras and surveillance systems
- Enhancing image quality in security and surveillance applications
- Improving image quality in digital forensics and forensic analysis
