# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1.  Define and differentiate between the terms "iterative" and "incremental" development.
2.  Explain the cyclical process of a single iteration, including its key phases.
3.  Identify the advantages and disadvantages of using an iterative/incremental approach compared to a linear model like Waterfall.
4.  Describe the structure and risk-driven nature of the Spiral Model.
5.  Explain how iterative development facilitates early risk mitigation and customer feedback.
6.  Determine the type of project scenarios where an iterative model is most and least appropriate.
