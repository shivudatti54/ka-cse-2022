# Learning Objectives
After studying this topic, you should be able to:
1. Explain the fundamental concepts and components of Information Retrieval systems
2. Describe and compare classical IR models including Boolean, Vector Space, and Probabilistic models
3. Understand and apply TF-IDF weighting and cosine similarity in Vector Space Model
4. Explain alternative IR models including Language Models, LSI, and Neural IR approaches
5. Analyze the advantages and limitations of different IR models for various applications
6. Calculate basic relevance scores using different IR models for simple examples
