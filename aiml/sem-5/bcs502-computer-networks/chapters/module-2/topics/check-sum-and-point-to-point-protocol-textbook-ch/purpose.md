**Why this topic matters:**
Learning about Check Sum and Point to Point Protocol is crucial as it enables devices to detect and correct errors in data transmission, ensuring the reliability and integrity of network communications.

**Real-world applications:**
This topic has numerous real-world applications in various industries, including telecommunications, networking devices, and data storage systems, where accurate data transmission is essential for business operations and data security.

**Connection to other concepts:**
Understanding Check Sum and Point to Point Protocol is closely related to other concepts in the Data Link Layer, such as Frame Error Detection (FED) and Frame Error Correction (FEC), which are used to detect and correct errors in data transmission, respectively.
