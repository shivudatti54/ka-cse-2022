The topic of Datagram Protocol (UDP) and Transmission Control Protocol (TCP) is crucial in computer networking as it explains the fundamental services, features, and mechanisms that enable reliable and efficient data transfer between devices. This knowledge matters because it lays the foundation for understanding how data is transmitted, received, and controlled in computer networks. In real-world applications, this topic is essential for designing and implementing network protocols, such as online gaming, video streaming, and file transfer services. This knowledge also connects to other concepts, such as routing protocols, network architecture, and network security.
