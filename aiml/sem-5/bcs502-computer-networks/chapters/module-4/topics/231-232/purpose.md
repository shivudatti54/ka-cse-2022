Learning topics 23.1-23.2, which typically cover the basics of transport-layer protocols such as Transport Layer Protocols (TCP, UDP) and connection establishment, is crucial in understanding computer networks. This topic matters because it forms the foundation for reliable data transfer and communication between devices on a network.

Real-world applications of this topic include email transmission, file sharing, and online gaming, all of which rely on the reliable and efficient transfer of data.

This topic connects to other concepts in the transport layer, such as congestion control, flow control, and error checking, which are essential for ensuring that data is transmitted accurately and efficiently.
