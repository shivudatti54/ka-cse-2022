# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define and explain the concept of structural representations in finite automata.
2. Identify and describe the different types of structural representations, including transition diagrams, transition tables, and state transition functions.
3. Analyze and compare the advantages and disadvantages of each type of structural representation.
4. Apply structural representations to solve problems in finite automata, such as constructing and analyzing automata.
5. Evaluate the complexity and scalability of different structural representations.
6. Use structural representations to prove the correctness and completeness of finite automata.
