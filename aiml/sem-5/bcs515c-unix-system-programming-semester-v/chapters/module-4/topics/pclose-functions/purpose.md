# Why Study Loss Functions?

## Big Picture Foundation
Loss functions define what the neural network optimizes. Understanding them is crucial for choosing the right objective for your task and understanding training behavior.

## Exam Relevance
| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Frequency | Core topic in every DL exam |
| Marks | 6-12 marks (formulas, derivations, comparisons) |
| Question Types | Derive gradients, compare losses, explain why cross-entropy for classification |

## Practical Value
- **Task Matching**: Choose appropriate loss for regression vs classification
- **Debugging**: Understand loss curves and training dynamics
- **Custom Losses**: Design losses for specific problems (imbalanced data, etc.)

## Learning Outcomes
After this topic, you should be able to:
1. Define MSE, MAE, and cross-entropy losses
2. Explain why cross-entropy is preferred for classification
3. Choose appropriate loss functions for different tasks
4. Understand the connection between loss and gradients
