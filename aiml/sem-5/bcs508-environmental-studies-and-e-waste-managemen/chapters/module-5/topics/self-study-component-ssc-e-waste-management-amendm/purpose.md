The Self-Study Component (SSC) on E-Waste (Management) Amendment Rules, 2023, 2024 is crucial as it highlights the importance of managing electronic waste sustainably, protecting the environment, and conserving natural resources. This topic matters because e-waste has become a significant environmental concern, posing threats to human health and the ecosystem. Real-world applications of this topic can be seen in the implementation of recycling programs, responsible manufacturing practices, and waste management policies. This component also connects to concepts of environmental science, pollution control, and sustainable development.
