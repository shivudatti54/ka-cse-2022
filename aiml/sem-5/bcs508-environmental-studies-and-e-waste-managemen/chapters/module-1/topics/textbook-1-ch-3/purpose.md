Here's an explanation of the purpose and importance of learning "Textbook 1: CH- 3" on Environmental Studies and E-Waste Management:

**1. Why this topic matters**: Understanding E-Waste Management is crucial as it addresses the growing concern of electronic waste, which poses significant environmental and health risks if not disposed of properly. This knowledge is essential for mitigating the impact of human activities on the ecosystem and promoting sustainable development.

**2. Real-world applications**: The principles learned from Textbook 1: CH- 3 have real-world applications in industries such as electronics, manufacturing, and waste management. Effective E-Waste Management strategies can help reduce pollution, conserve natural resources, and minimize the environmental and health impacts associated with e-waste disposal.

**3. Connection to other concepts**: The topic of E-Waste Management is interconnected with other Environmental Studies concepts, such as sustainable development, ecosystem services, and waste reduction. By understanding the importance of E-Waste Management, students can better appreciate the broader context of environmental sustainability and its relevance to other areas of study.
