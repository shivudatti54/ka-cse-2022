#### Important Formulas and Definitions

- **Mean Absolute Error (MAE)**: $\frac{1}{n} \sum_{i=1}^{n} |y_i - \hat{y}_i|$
- **Mean Squared Error (MSE)**: $\frac{1}{n} \sum_{i=1}^{n} (y_i - \hat{y}_i)^2$
- **Coefficient of Determination (R-squared)**: measures the proportion of variance in the dependent variable that is predictable from the independent variable(s)

#### Theorems and Concepts

- **Boole's Inequality**: states that for any two arrays $A$ and $B$, $\sum_{i=1}^{n} |A_iB_i| \leq \sum_{i=1}^{n} |A_i| + \sum_{i=1}^{n} |B_i|$
- **Kolmogorov-Smirnov Test**: used to test the goodness of fit of a distribution
