# **Software Engineering & Project Management**

## **Topic: Role of Project Planning and Quality Management in Software Development**

### Overview

- Project planning and quality management are crucial components of software development.
- Effective planning and quality management ensure the successful delivery of software projects.

### Key Points

#### Project Planning

- **Project Planning**: The process of defining, organizing, and controlling resources to achieve specific project goals.
- **Life Cycle Phases**:
  - Initiating
  - Planning
  - Executing
  - Monitoring and Controlling
  - Closing
- **Project Management Methodologies**:
  - Waterfall
  - Agile
  - Hybrid
- **Project Management Tools**:
  - Gantt charts
  - Kanban boards
  - Burn-down charts

#### Quality Management

- **Quality Management**: The process of planning, organizing, and controlling activities to ensure that products or services meet customer and organizational requirements.
- **Quality Models**:
  - ISO 9001 (Quality Management System)
  - CMMI (Capability Maturity Model Integration)
- **Quality Metrics**:
  - Defect density
  - Defect leakage rate
  - Cycle time
  - Lead time
- **Testing and Validation**:
  - Unit testing
  - Integration testing
  - System testing
  - User acceptance testing (UAT)

### Important Formulas and Definitions

- **Project Management Triangle**:
  - Scope
  - Time
  - Cost
- **The Three-Point Estimate Formula**:
  - Optimum cost = (Low estimate + High estimate) / 2
- **Quality Control (QC)**:
  - The process of monitoring and controlling activities to ensure that products or services meet customer and organizational requirements.

### Important Theorems and Principles

- **Project Management Maturity Model**:
  - The CMMI model assesses an organization's ability to plan, organize, and control projects.
- **The Law of Diminishing Returns**:
  - Increased quality and functionality can lead to decreased productivity and increased costs.

### Quick Revision Tips

- Focus on the key life cycle phases and project management methodologies.
- Understand the importance of quality metrics and testing.
- Review the project management triangle and the three-point estimate formula.
- Familiarize yourself with quality control and the CMMI model.
