For instance, a requirement engineer's skills are needed to gather and document user requirements for a new mobile payment system.

**Connection to other concepts:** Requirements engineering and analysis are closely linked to other concepts in software engineering, such as software design, testing, and validation. Understanding the importance of requirements engineering also informs the development of project management skills, such as risk management, stakeholder management, and project scheduling.
