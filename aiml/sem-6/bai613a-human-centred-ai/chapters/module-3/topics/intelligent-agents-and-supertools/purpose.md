### **Learning Purpose: Intelligent Agents and Supertools**

**1. Why is this important?**
This topic is crucial because intelligent agents (IAs) are the fundamental building blocks of modern AI systems that interact with users and environments. Understanding them is key to designing AI that is effective, trustworthy, and truly human-centred. The concept of "supertools"—AI that amplifies human capability rather than replacing it—is central to ensuring technology serves humanity's best interests.

**2. What will you learn?**
You will learn to define and classify different types of intelligent agents (e.g., simple reflex, model-based, goal-based, learning agents). You will analyze their core components (percepts, actions, goals, environment) and explore the "supertools" paradigm, which focuses on creating collaborative AI partners that augment human intelligence and expertise.

**3. How does it connect?**
This module builds directly on prior knowledge of AI ethics (Module 2) by providing the technical framework for building responsible AI. It connects the theoretical principles of human-centred design to practical system architecture. It also serves as a foundation for subsequent topics like human-AI collaboration and multi-agent systems.

**4. Real-world applications**
This knowledge applies directly to the design of virtual assistants (Siri, Alexa), recommendation algorithms (Netflix, Spotify), autonomous systems (self-driving cars), and advanced decision-support tools in fields like healthcare diagnostics and scientific research, where AI acts as a powerful augmenting tool.
