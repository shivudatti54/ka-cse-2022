# **Systems, Two-Dimensional HCAI Framework, Design Guidelines and Examples**

### Chapter 6: Systems and HCAI

- **Definition of HCAI Systems:** A system that integrates human-centered design, artificial intelligence, and automation to improve human well-being.
- **HCAI Systems Design Principles:**
  - User-centered design
  - Transparency and explainability
  - Accountability and liability
  - Human-machine interface (HMI)
- **Types of HCAI Systems:**
  - Intelligent transportation systems (ITS)
  - Smart homes and cities
  - Healthcare and medical systems

### Chapter 7: Two-Dimensional HCAI Framework

- **Two-Dimensional HCAI Framework:**
  - Human-centered (H)
  - Responsible AI (RAI)
  - Intelligent systems (IS)
- **Key Components:**
  - Human values and principles
  - AI and machine learning
  - System design and development
  - Human-AI collaboration and interaction
- **Benefits of the Two-Dimensional HCAI Framework:**
  - Improved human well-being
  - Increased transparency and accountability
  - Enhanced system safety and reliability
