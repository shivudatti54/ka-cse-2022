### Learning Purpose: Course Project

**1. Why is this topic important?**
The project is the capstone application of Human-Centred AI (HCAI) principles. It moves theory into practice, challenging you to design and build an AI system that is not just technically proficient but also ethical, fair, and truly beneficial for its users. This hands-on experience is critical for understanding the real-world complexities and responsibilities of an HCAI practitioner.

**2. What will students learn?**
You will learn to integrate core HCAI concepts—like value-sensitive design, stakeholder analysis, and ethical auditing—into a complete project lifecycle. This includes scoping a real-world problem, designing an AI solution with human needs at its core, prototyping key interactions, and critically evaluating its potential impact and risks.

**3. How does it connect to other concepts?**
This project synthesizes all previous modules: ethical frameworks (Module 1), interaction design (Module 2), fairness and transparency (Module 3), and evaluation methods (Module 4). It requires you to weave these discrete concepts into a single, coherent, and responsible AI application.

**4. Real-world applications**
This mirrors the standard process for developing HCAI in industry, from healthcare diagnostics and educational tools to equitable financial systems. The skills honed here are directly applicable to roles in UX research, AI ethics auditing, product management, and responsible AI development.
