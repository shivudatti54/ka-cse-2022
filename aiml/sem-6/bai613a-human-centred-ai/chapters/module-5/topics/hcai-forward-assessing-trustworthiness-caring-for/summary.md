# **HCAI Forward, Assessing Trustworthiness, Caring for and Learning from Older Adults Textbook: Chapter 22, 24, 25, 26 Revision Notes**

### Chapter 22: Trustworthiness of AI Systems

- **Definition:** Trustworthiness of an AI system refers to its ability to deliver the expected results and behave as intended.
- **Factors affecting trustworthiness:**
  - Data quality and bias
  - Algorithmic transparency and explainability
  - Human oversight and accountability
  - Testing and validation procedures
- **Trustworthiness evaluation methods:**
  - Formal testing and validation
  - Human evaluation and feedback
  - Automated testing and monitoring

### Chapter 24: Caring for Older Adults in AI Systems

- **Definition:** Care for older adults in AI systems involves designing and developing AI systems that prioritize the well-being and dignity of older adults.
- **Key considerations:**
  - Accessibility and usability
  - Personalization and adaptability
  - Emotional support and empathy
  - Cultural sensitivity and awareness
- **Design principles:**
  - Human-centered design
  - Co-design and collaboration
  - Empathy and understanding

### Chapter 25: Learning from Older Adults in AI Systems

- **Definition:** Learning from older adults in AI systems involves incorporating the knowledge, experiences, and perspectives of older adults into AI systems.
- **Key benefits:**
  - Improved system performance and accuracy
  - Enhanced user experience and engagement
  - Increased inclusivity and accessibility
  - Better understanding of human behavior and needs
- **Methods for learning from older adults:**
  - User research and evaluation
  - Collaboration and co-design
  - Knowledge transfer and sharing

### Chapter 26: Regulatory Frameworks for HCAI

- **Definition:** Regulatory frameworks for HCAI (Human-Centred AI) involve laws, policies, and guidelines that govern the development and deployment of AI systems.
- **Key considerations:**
  - Data protection and privacy
  - Liability and accountability
  - Transparency and explainability
  - Human rights and dignity
- **Regulatory frameworks:**
  - General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  - Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA)
  - EU Artificial Intelligence White Paper
