To provide a comprehensive learning purpose for Module 5: Research and Applications, focusing on Human-Centred AI.

***

### **Learning Purpose**

**1. Why is this topic important?**
Understanding the cutting-edge research and practical applications of Human-Centred AI (HCAI) is crucial. It moves the discussion beyond theory into the tangible, real-world systems that impact society. This knowledge is vital for ensuring that the AI we build is not only powerful but also beneficial, ethical, and aligned with human needs and values.

**2. What will students learn?**
Students will analyze contemporary HCAI research from leading academic and industry sources. They will learn to critically evaluate how HCAI principles are implemented in various domains, identify successful case studies, and recognize ongoing challenges and limitations in current applications.

**3. How does it connect to other concepts?**
This module synthesizes all previous concepts—ethical frameworks, design methodologies, and evaluation techniques—by examining them in action. It connects theoretical principles of trust, fairness, and user autonomy to their practical execution within live AI systems, providing a holistic view of the HCAI lifecycle.

**4. Real-world applications**
Students will explore applications across diverse fields such as healthcare (diagnostic aids), education (adaptive learning platforms), and productivity (AI collaborators). This demonstrates the universal relevance of HCAI and prepares students to design and advocate for responsible AI solutions in their own future careers.
