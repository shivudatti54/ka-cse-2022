### Learning Purpose: Understanding Human-Centred AI with Sangwon Lee

**1. Why is this topic important?**
Understanding the work of experts like Sangwon Lee is crucial as it provides concrete examples of how to implement Human-Centred AI (HCAI) principles in practice. This moves the theory of designing AI for human benefit into tangible methodologies and ethical frameworks, ensuring technology augments human capabilities responsibly and equitably.

**2. What will students learn?**
Students will learn about specific HCAI design philosophies, methodologies, and evaluation techniques, as exemplified by Sangwon Lee's research. This includes how to design AI systems that are transparent, accountable, and user-friendly, focusing on concepts like value-sensitive design and ethical considerations in the AI development lifecycle.

**3. How does it connect to other concepts?**
This topic directly builds upon earlier modules on AI ethics, bias, and explainability (XAI). It connects these foundational principles to their practical application in user experience (UX) design, software development, and policy-making, creating a holistic view of the responsible AI ecosystem.

**4. Real-world applications**
The concepts discussed are applied in developing trustworthy recommendation systems, creating accessible AI tools, designing fair hiring algorithms, and building collaborative AI partners in fields like healthcare, finance, and education, ensuring technology serves human needs effectively.
