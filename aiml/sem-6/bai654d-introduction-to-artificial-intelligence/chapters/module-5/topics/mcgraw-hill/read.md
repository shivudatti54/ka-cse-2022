### Introduction to Artificial Intelligence
#### Module 5: McGraw Hill
Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a field of study that focuses on creating intelligent machines capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as visual perception, speech recognition, decision-making, and language translation. As  engineering students, understanding the fundamentals of AI is crucial in today's technology-driven world.

### Core Concepts of Artificial Intelligence
The core concepts of AI can be broadly categorized into several areas:

* **Machine Learning (ML)**: A subset of AI that involves training machines to learn from data and make predictions or decisions without being explicitly programmed. ML algorithms can be supervised, unsupervised, or semi-supervised.
* **Deep Learning (DL)**: A type of ML that uses neural networks with multiple layers to analyze data. DL is particularly useful for image and speech recognition tasks.
* **Natural Language Processing (NLP)**: A field of study that deals with the interaction between computers and humans in natural language. NLP is used in applications such as language translation, sentiment analysis, and text summarization.
* **Computer Vision**: A field of study that enables computers to interpret and understand visual data from the world. Computer vision is used in applications such as image recognition, object detection, and facial recognition.

### Examples of Artificial Intelligence
AI is already being used in various industries, including:

* **Virtual Assistants**: Virtual assistants such as Siri, Google Assistant, and Alexa use NLP to understand voice commands and perform tasks such as setting reminders, sending messages, and making calls.
* **Image Recognition**: Image recognition technology is used in applications such as Facebook, where it can identify and tag individuals in photos.
* **Self-Driving Cars**: Self-driving cars use a combination of computer vision, ML, and sensor data to navigate roads and avoid obstacles.
* **Chatbots**: Chatbots use NLP to understand and respond to customer inquiries, providing 24/7 support and helping to reduce the workload of human customer support agents.

### Key Techniques Used in Artificial Intelligence
Some key techniques used in AI include:

* **Backpropagation**: An algorithm used to train neural networks by minimizing the error between predicted and actual outputs.
* **Gradient Descent**: An optimization algorithm used to minimize the loss function in ML models.
* **Support Vector Machines (SVMs)**: A type of ML algorithm used for classification and regression tasks.
