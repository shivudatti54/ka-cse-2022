# **Cloud Security: Top Concerns and Risks**

### 11.1: Cloud Security Risks

- **Types of Cloud Security Risks:**
  - Data breaches and unauthorized access
  - Data loss and corruption
  - Denial of Service (DoS) and Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attacks
  - Insider threats and privilege escalation
  - Malware and ransomware attacks

### 11.2: Privacy Impact Assessment

- **Privacy Impact Assessment (PIA) Framework:**
  - Identify risks and threats to data privacy
  - Assess the likelihood and impact of risks
  - Determine the level of risk and implement controls
  - Continuously monitor and review controls

- **Key Considerations:**
  - Data minimization and limitation
  - Data anonymization and pseudonymization
  - Access controls and authentication
  - Data encryption and storage

### 11.3: Cloud Data Security

- **Cloud Data Security Models:**
  - Public Cloud: Third-party managed
  - Private Cloud: On-premises managed
  - Hybrid Cloud: Combination of public and private

- **Data Protection Regulations:**
  - General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  - Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA)
  - Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI-DSS)

- **Cloud Data Encryption:**
  - Full-disk encryption
  - File-level encryption
  - Data-at-rest encryption
  - Data-in-transit encryption
