### Key Points

### Definitions

- **Cloud Security**: The practice of protecting cloud computing systems and data from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, disruption, modification, or destruction.
- **Cloud Service**: A network of remote servers, accessed over the internet, that are used to store, manage, and process data.

### Theorems and Formulas

---

### Risks in Cloud Computing

- **Common Risks**: Data breaches, unauthorized access, data loss, and service disruptions.
- **Risk Assessment**: Evaluate the likelihood and potential impact of each risk.

### Important Concepts

---

- **Cloud Security Architecture**: A comprehensive security plan that includes access controls, encryption, and monitoring.
- **Data Privacy**: The protection of personal data in cloud computing.

### Privacy Impact Assessment

---

- **Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA)**: A process to identify and mitigate potential risks to personal data in cloud computing.
- **Data Protection Regulations**: Compliance with regulations such as GDPR and HIPAA.
