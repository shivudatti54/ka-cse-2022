# Learning Objectives
After studying this topic, you should be able to:
1. Differentiate between manual (human) and automatic methods for evaluating machine translation output.
2. Explain the mechanics, advantages, and limitations of key automated metrics like BLEU, TER, and METEOR.
3. Distinguish between intrinsic and extrinsic evaluation approaches and provide examples of each.
4. Identify and describe at least three major ethical concerns arising from the development and use of Machine Translation systems.
5. Analyze a given translation scenario and propose an appropriate evaluation strategy to assess its quality.
6. Discuss potential mitigation strategies for ethical issues such as bias, privacy, and misuse in MT.
