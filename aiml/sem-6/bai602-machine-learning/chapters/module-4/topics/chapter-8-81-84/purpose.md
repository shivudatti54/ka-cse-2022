Learning Chapter-8 (8.1-8.4) on Decision Tree Learning is crucial as it provides a foundational understanding of the decision tree learning model, enabling individuals to build and implement effective classification and regression models. This topic matters as it has significant real-world applications in areas such as customer segmentation, credit risk assessment, and recommendation systems. Decision Tree Learning is also closely related to other concepts in machine learning, such as supervised learning, feature selection, and tree-based models.
