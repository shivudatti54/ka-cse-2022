# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Design research presentations that comply with DU's academic standards and NEP 2024's interdisciplinary focus  
2. Apply viva voce defense strategies for technical, ethical, and methodological cross-examinations  
3. Develop accessible visualizations using tools like Matplotlib or Tableau aligned with WCAG 2.1  
4. Critically evaluate research limitations using DU's Research Integrity Framework  
5. Synthesize feedback from mock panels into iterative presentation improvements  
6. Demonstrate live technical systems with contingency planning for infrastructure failures  
7. Articulate research novelty using bibliometric indicators from Scopus/Web of Science  
8. Align ethical justifications with UGC's AI Ethics Guidelines and EU's GDPR standards
