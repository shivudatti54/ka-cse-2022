# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Explain transformer architecture and its mathematical foundations
2. Analyze the impact of scaling laws on model performance
3. Implement basic self-attention mechanisms from scratch
4. Evaluate ethical implications of LLM deployment
5. Design parameter-efficient fine-tuning strategies
6. Critique current research on emergent capabilities
7. Optimize prompt engineering for specific tasks
8. Calculate computational requirements for LLM training
