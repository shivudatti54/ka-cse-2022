# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Analyze ethical dilemmas in AI systems using deontological, utilitarian, and virtue ethics frameworks
2. Evaluate fairness metrics under India's socio-cultural constraints
3. Design audit pipelines compliant with DPDP Act 2023 requirements
4. Critique existing AI ethics guidelines using game-theoretic models
5. Develop value-alignment mechanisms for multilingual LLMs
6. Propose governance models addressing AI's environmental justice impacts
7. Formulate red teaming strategies for high-risk AI deployments
8. Synthesize ethical AI architectures balancing explainability and performance
