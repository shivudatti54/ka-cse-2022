# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Analyze ethical implications of AI systems using established frameworks
2. Evaluate compliance of AI architectures with major regulatory regimes
3. Design technical governance mechanisms for machine learning pipelines
4. Conduct risk assessments for AI applications in different domains
5. Critique existing regulations using case law and technical literature
6. Propose mitigation strategies for algorithmic bias in regulated systems
7. Integrate transparency requirements into AI system design
8. Engage with current research on AI governance challenges
