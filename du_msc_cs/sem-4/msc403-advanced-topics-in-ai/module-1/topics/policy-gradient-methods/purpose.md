# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Derive the policy gradient theorem and explain its significance in RL optimization.
2. Implement REINFORCE and Actor-Critic algorithms from scratch using PyTorch/TensorFlow.
3. Analyze variance-bias tradeoffs in different advantage estimation methods (MC vs TD vs GAE).
4. Design a PPO-clip objective function with trust region constraints.
5. Evaluate policy gradient methods against value-based approaches (DQN) in continuous control tasks.
6. Interpret recent research papers extending PG methods (e.g., IMPALA, SAC, or D4PG).
7. Troubleshoot common training failures in PG algorithms (e.g., high variance, policy collapse).
8. Apply policy gradients to real-world partially observable environments using RNN policies.
