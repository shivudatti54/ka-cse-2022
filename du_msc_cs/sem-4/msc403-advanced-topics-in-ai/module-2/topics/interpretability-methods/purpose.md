# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Explain the fundamental principles of model interpretability in AI systems
2. Compare and contrast different interpretation methods (LIME vs SHAP vs Grad-CAM)
3. Apply appropriate interpretability techniques to real-world AI applications
4. Evaluate the limitations of current explanation methods in terms of faithfulness and completeness
5. Design human-in-the-loop systems incorporating interpretability requirements
6. Critically analyze recent research papers on explainable AI (XAI)
7. Implement basic interpretation methods using Python libraries (SHAP, Captum, LIME)
8. Integrate interpretability considerations into the AI development lifecycle
