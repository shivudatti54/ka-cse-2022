# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Explain the mathematical formulation of self-attention and its computational complexity
2. Design transformer architectures for specific NLP/vision tasks
3. Implement multi-head attention with efficient matrix operations
4. Analyze the role of positional encodings in sequence modeling
5. Compare transformer variants (BERT, GPT, ViT) at architectural level
6. Debug gradient flow in deep transformer networks
7. Optimize transformer models for memory-constrained environments
8. Propose novel attention mechanisms for research problems
