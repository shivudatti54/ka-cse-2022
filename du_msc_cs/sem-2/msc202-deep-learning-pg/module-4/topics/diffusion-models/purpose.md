# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Derive the mathematical foundations of diffusion models from first principles
2. Implement both forward and reverse diffusion processes in PyTorch
3. Analyze the trade-offs between different noise scheduling approaches
4. Compare and contrast diffusion models with other generative architectures
5. Optimize sampling efficiency using advanced techniques like DDIM
6. Apply diffusion models to real-world problems in scientific computing
7. Critically evaluate recent research papers in diffusion models
8. Design novel variance schedules for domain-specific applications
