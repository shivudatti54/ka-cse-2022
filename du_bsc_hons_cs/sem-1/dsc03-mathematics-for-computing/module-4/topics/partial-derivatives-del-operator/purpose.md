## Purpose and Learning Objectives

### Purpose
Partial derivatives and the Del operator form the mathematical foundation for gradient-based optimization techniques essential in machine learning, computer graphics (lighting and shading calculations), and physics-based simulations. Understanding these concepts enables computer science professionals to develop algorithms for image processing, robotics, and data science applications where rate of change and directional derivatives are critical.

### Learning Objectives
- **Define** partial derivatives and explain their significance in multi-variable functions
- **Perform** partial differentiation with respect to multiple variables
- **Apply** the Del operator (∇) to compute gradient, divergence, and curl
- **Interpret** the gradient vector as a direction of maximum rate of change
- **Solve** problems involving directional derivatives and their applications
- **Analyze** the role of partial derivatives in optimization algorithms used in machine learning
- **Evaluate** the use of Del operator in computer graphics for surface normal calculations
