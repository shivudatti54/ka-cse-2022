# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define divergence and curl and explain their physical meaning.
2. Compute divergence and curl for different vector fields.
3. Verify the properties of divergence and curl for different vector fields.
4. Use the properties of divergence and curl to simplify computations.
5. Interpret the physical meaning of divergence and curl.
6. Use divergence and curl to solve problems in physics, engineering, and computer science.
7. Derive the formulas for divergence and curl from first principles.
8. Apply divergence and curl to real-world problems.
