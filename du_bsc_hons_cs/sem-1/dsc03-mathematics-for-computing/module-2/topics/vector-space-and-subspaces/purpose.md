## Purpose & Learning Objectives

### Purpose

Vector spaces provide the foundational mathematical framework for many computing disciplines, enabling the analysis and manipulation of high-dimensional data structures essential to machine learning, computer graphics, and data science. Understanding subspaces and linear transformations equips computer scientists with the tools to optimize algorithms, perform dimensionality reduction, and solve systems of linear equations that arise in network analysis, image processing, and scientific computing.

### Learning Objectives

- **Define** the key properties of a vector space and identify subspaces within given vector spaces.
- **Explain** the concepts of linear independence, basis, and dimension with appropriate examples.
- **Apply** linear transformations to solve problems in computer graphics and data representation.
- **Demonstrate** the ability to construct bases for given subspaces and determine their dimensions.
- **Analyze** the rank-nullity theorem and its implications for solving systems of linear equations.
- **Compute** eigenvalues and eigenvectors and relate them to practical applications in machine learning.
- **Solve** real-world problems by modeling them as vector space problems in areas such as network flow and error correction.
