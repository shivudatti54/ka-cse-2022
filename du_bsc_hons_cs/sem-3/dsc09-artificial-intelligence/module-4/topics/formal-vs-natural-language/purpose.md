# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define and distinguish between natural languages and formal languages with appropriate examples
2. Identify the key characteristics of natural languages including ambiguity, context-dependence, and evolution
3. Explain the precise nature of formal languages including syntax, grammar, and unambiguous interpretation
4. Describe the Chomsky hierarchy and classify formal grammars into Type 0, Type 1, Type 2, and Type 3
5. Compare the computational approaches required for processing natural vs. formal languages
6. Apply context-free grammars to generate and parse simple arithmetic expressions
7. Analyze the role of formal and natural languages in artificial intelligence applications
8. Evaluate why natural language processing presents unique challenges compared to formal language processing in AI systems
