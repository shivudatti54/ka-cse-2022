## Purpose

Understanding vanishing points is essential for creating realistic 3D-to-2D projections in computer graphics, directly impacting applications like gaming, virtual reality, architectural visualization, and computer vision. This concept forms the mathematical foundation for perspective rendering, enabling computers to simulate human visual perception and depth cues in digital environments.

## Learning Objectives

- Define vanishing points and explain their role in perspective projection systems
- Distinguish between one-point, two-point, and three-point perspective transformations
- Apply mathematical formulations to calculate vanishing points in 3D scenes
- Implement perspective projection algorithms in graphics rendering pipelines
- Analyze the relationship between camera parameters and vanishing point placement
- Evaluate the impact of vanishing points on depth perception and visual realism
- Design perspective-correct scenes for various computer graphics applications
