# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define bias and variance in the context of machine learning and explain their roles in model performance.

2. Explain the bias-variance tradeoff and describe how it relates to underfitting and overfitting.

3. Derive and interpret the bias-variance decomposition formula for regression problems.

4. Implement L1 (Lasso) and L2 (Ridge) regularization techniques and explain their differences.

5. Apply dropout regularization in neural networks and explain its mechanism during training and inference.

6. Describe other regularization techniques including early stopping, data augmentation, and batch normalization.

7. Select appropriate regularization strategies based on the symptoms of underfitting or overfitting observed in model performance.
