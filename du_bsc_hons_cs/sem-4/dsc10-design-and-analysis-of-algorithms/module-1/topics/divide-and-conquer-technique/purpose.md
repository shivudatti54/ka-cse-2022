# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Explain the fundamental principles and three-step process of the divide and conquer algorithm design technique
2. Identify and describe classic algorithms that use the divide and conquer paradigm, including Binary Search, Merge Sort, and Quick Sort
3. Analyze the time and space complexity of divide and conquer algorithms using recurrence relations
4. Apply the Master Theorem to solve recurrence relations of the form T(n) = aT(n/b) + f(n)
5. Implement Binary Search and Merge Sort algorithms with correct recursive structure
6. Compare and contrast different divide and conquer algorithms based on their time complexities and practical considerations
7. Understand the concept of recursion trees and be able to trace recursive algorithm executions
8. Apply divide and conquer thinking to solve novel algorithmic problems by identifying how to divide, conquer, and combine
