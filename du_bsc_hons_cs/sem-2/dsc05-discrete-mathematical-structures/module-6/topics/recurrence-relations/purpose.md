# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define recurrence relations and identify their key components including order, degree, and initial conditions
2. Classify recurrence relations as linear/non-linear, homogeneous/non-homogeneous, and with/without constant coefficients
3. Solve linear homogeneous recurrence relations with constant coefficients using the characteristic equation method
4. Solve linear non-homogeneous recurrence relations by combining homogeneous and particular solutions
5. Apply the iteration method to solve first-order recurrence relations
6. Use the Master Theorem to determine time complexity of divide-and-conquer recurrences
7. Analyze recursive algorithms and formulate appropriate recurrence relations for their time complexity
8. Verify the correctness of solutions by substituting back into the original recurrence relation
