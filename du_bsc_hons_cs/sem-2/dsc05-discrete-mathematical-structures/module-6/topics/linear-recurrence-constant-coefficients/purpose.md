# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define and distinguish between homogeneous and non-homogeneous linear recurrence relations with constant coefficients.

2. Determine the order of a linear recurrence relation and identify its constant coefficients.

3. Apply the characteristic equation method to solve homogeneous linear recurrence relations with distinct roots.

4. Solve homogeneous recurrence relations with repeated roots using the multiplicity-based solution formula.

5. Find particular solutions for non-homogeneous recurrence relations using the method of undetermined coefficients.

6. Construct the complete solution of a non-homogeneous recurrence as the sum of homogeneous and particular solutions.

7. Apply appropriate initial conditions to determine unique solutions for recurrence relations.

8. Analyze and solve recurrence relations arising from recursive algorithm time complexity problems.
