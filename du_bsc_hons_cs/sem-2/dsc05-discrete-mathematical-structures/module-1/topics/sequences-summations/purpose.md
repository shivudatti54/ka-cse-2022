**Purpose**  
Understanding sequences and summations is fundamental for analyzing the time‑ and space‑complexity of algorithms, especially recursive ones, and for modelling combinatorial problems that arise in data science, cryptography, and machine learning.

**Learning Objectives**  
- **Define** sequences, summations, and common types (arithmetic, geometric, recursive).  
- **Compute** closed‑form expressions for standard finite and infinite series.  
- **Solve** recurrence relations using iteration, substitution, and the master theorem.  
- **Apply** summation techniques to derive Big‑O, Ω, and Θ bounds for algorithm runtimes.  
- **Construct** generating functions for given sequences and use them to solve counting problems.  
- **Evaluate** the convergence of infinite series and determine when they represent valid probability distributions.  
- **Use** sequences and summations to model real‑world scenarios such as amortized analysis and network traffic patterns.
