# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Identify and explain at least five major application domains of machine learning in industry and society

2. Describe how machine learning is applied in healthcare for diagnostics, treatment personalization, and drug discovery

3. Analyze the role of ML in financial services, including fraud detection, credit scoring, and algorithmic trading

4. Understand the working of recommendation systems in e-commerce and entertainment platforms

5. Explain computer vision applications in healthcare imaging, autonomous vehicles, and quality control

6. Discuss natural language processing applications including virtual assistants, machine translation, and sentiment analysis

7. Evaluate the ethical considerations and societal implications of widespread ML adoption

8. Connect theoretical ML concepts to practical real-world problems and identify opportunities for ML implementation
