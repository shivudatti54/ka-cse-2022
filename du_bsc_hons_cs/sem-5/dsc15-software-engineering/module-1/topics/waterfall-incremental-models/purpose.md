## Purpose
Understanding the Waterfall and Incremental models is crucial for software engineers, as these lifecycle approaches dictate how projects are scoped, scheduled, and delivered. In the NEP 2024 curriculum for BSc (Hons) Computer Science at Delhi University, mastering these models equips students to analyse project requirements, choose appropriate development strategies, and adapt to evolving stakeholder expectations.

## Learning Objectives
- **Compare** the phases, strengths, and limitations of the Waterfall model versus Incremental models.  
- **Differentiate** between pure Waterfall, iterative Waterfall, and incremental delivery approaches.  
- **Apply** the Waterfall model to a given project scenario to produce a structured development plan.  
- **Design** an incremental development plan that delivers functionality in successive builds.  
- **Evaluate** trade‑offs between flexibility, risk, and customer feedback in each model.  
- **Select** an appropriate model based on project requirements, team size, and stakeholder needs.
