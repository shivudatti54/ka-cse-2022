**Purpose**  
In modern software engineering, uncertainties can jeopardize schedule, budget, and quality. Mastering risk identification, projection, and mitigation equips developers with systematic techniques to anticipate threats, allocate resources efficiently, and deliver dependable software systems.

**Learning Objectives**  
- **Identify** potential risks across requirements, design, implementation, and deployment phases.  
- **Analyze** and categorize risks by likelihood, impact, and criticality.  
- **Estimate** risk exposure using both quantitative (e.g., probability‑impact matrix) and qualitative methods.  
- **Prioritize** risks for mitigation based on severity and project constraints.  
- **Develop** risk mitigation strategies, contingency plans, and risk‑aware architectures.  
- **Implement** mitigation actions and integrate risk management into agile or DevOps workflows.  
- **Monitor** the effectiveness of mitigations and update the risk register continuously.  
- **Communicate** risk status and decisions clearly to stakeholders at all project levels.
