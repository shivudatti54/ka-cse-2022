# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Explain the fundamental concepts of data privacy including personal data, data processing, and the roles of data controller versus data processor in cloud environments.

2. Analyze the key provisions of major privacy regulations including GDPR, India's DPDP Act 2023, and CCPA, and identify which scenarios trigger their applicability.

3. Evaluate the jurisdictional challenges arising from cloud computing architecture, including data localization requirements and cross-border transfer mechanisms.

4. Apply appropriate data transfer mechanisms such as Standard Contractual Clauses, Binding Corporate Rules, and adequacy decisions when designing cloud solutions.

5. Determine the allocation of responsibilities between cloud service providers and customers across IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS deployment models.

6. Design compliant data processing strategies that satisfy multi-jurisdictional requirements for organizations operating in different legal frameworks.

7. Identify breach notification requirements and timelines under various privacy regulations.

8. Assess practical scenarios to recommend appropriate privacy compliance measures for cloud-based data processing activities.
