## Purpose and Learning Objectives

### Purpose

Understanding IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS service models is essential for modern computer science professionals, as these models form the backbone of contemporary cloud-based applications and infrastructure that power nearly every digital service used today. This knowledge enables students to make informed architectural decisions, evaluate cloud solutions against business requirements, and effectively contribute to technology teams deploying cloud strategies.

### Learning Objectives

Upon completion of this unit, students will be able to:

- **Define** the three fundamental cloud service models (IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS) and explain their core characteristics
- **Differentiate** between the levels of abstraction, management responsibility, and control offered by each service model
- **Identify** appropriate use cases and scenarios where each service model is most effectively applied
- **Analyze** the advantages and limitations of each model in terms of scalability, cost, and operational overhead
- **Evaluate** security responsibilities and compliance considerations across the different service models
- **Compare** major cloud service providers and their specific offerings within each category
- **Design** basic cloud architecture solutions by selecting appropriate service models for given business requirements
