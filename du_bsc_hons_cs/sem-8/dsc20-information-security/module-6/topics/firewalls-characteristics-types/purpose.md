## Purpose  
Understanding firewalls is essential for safeguarding computer networks against unauthorized access and ensuring compliance with security policies. This topic equips students with the knowledge to select, configure, and manage firewall solutions that form a critical layer of defense in modern information systems.

## Learning Objectives  
- **Identify** the core functions and characteristics of firewalls in network security.  
- **Distinguish** between hardware, software, and cloud‑based firewall implementations.  
- **Compare** packet‑filtering, stateful inspection, proxy, and next‑generation firewall architectures.  
- **Analyze** the role of firewalls within a defense‑in‑depth security strategy.  
- **Design** a basic firewall policy that enforces least‑privilege access controls.  
- **Configure** a firewall to filter traffic based on IP addresses, ports, and protocols.  
- **Evaluate** the effectiveness of firewall rules using logging and intrusion‑detection integration.  
- **Assess** emerging trends such as Firewall‑as‑a‑Service (FWaaS) and micro‑segmentation.
