# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Define and explain the concept of data models and their role in database management systems

2. Distinguish between the three levels of schema in the ANSI-SPARC three-schema architecture: internal, conceptual, and external

3. Explain the concept of a database instance and differentiate it from schema with appropriate examples

4. Understand and describe data independence, including both logical and physical data independence

5. Compare and contrast different types of data models: hierarchical, network, relational, and object-oriented

6. Explain the purpose of subschemas and their role in providing user-specific views and security

7. Apply the concepts of schema and instance to analyze real-world database scenarios

8. Understand the evolution of data models and their impact on database technology
