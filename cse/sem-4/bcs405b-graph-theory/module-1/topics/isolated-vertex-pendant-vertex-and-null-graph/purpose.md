# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Define and identify an isolated vertex in a graph with degree equal to zero.

2. Define and identify a pendant vertex (leaf vertex) in a graph with degree equal to one.

3. Define a null graph (empty graph) and distinguish between trivial and multi-vertex null graphs.

4. Apply degree calculations to classify vertices as isolated, pendant, or regular vertices.

5. Analyze adjacency matrix representations to identify special vertices.

6. Understand the relationship between complete graphs and null graphs as complements.

7. Use the Handshaking Lemma to verify degree sequences involving isolated and pendant vertices.
