# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Understand and state the Principle of Inclusion and Exclusion for two and three sets
2. Apply the general PIE formula to solve problems involving the union of n sets
3. Compute derangements using both the factorial formula and recurrence relation
4. Solve practical counting problems involving divisibility conditions using PIE
5. Calculate the number of elements satisfying "at least one" or "none of" conditions
6. Apply PIE to real-world scenarios like survey problems and arrangements
7. Verify solutions using alternative counting methods
8. Connect PIE to applications in number theory (Euler's phi function) and probability
