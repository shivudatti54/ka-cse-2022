# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Identify and distinguish second order linear homogeneous recurrence relations from other types of recurrence relations.

2. Formulate the characteristic equation corresponding to a given second order linear homogeneous recurrence relation.

3. Solve characteristic equations with distinct real roots and construct the general solution.

4. Solve characteristic equations with repeated real roots and apply the modified solution form with the n-factor.

5. Solve characteristic equations with complex conjugate roots and express solutions in trigonometric form.

6. Apply initial conditions to determine the particular solution of a recurrence relation.

7. Verify that a obtained solution satisfies both the recurrence relation and the given initial conditions.

8. Apply the methods learned to analyze and solve real-world problems modeled by second order linear homogeneous recurrence relations.
