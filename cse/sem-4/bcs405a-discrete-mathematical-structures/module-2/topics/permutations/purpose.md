# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Understand and apply the Fundamental Principle of Counting (Multiplication Rule) to solve counting problems.

2. Define permutations and distinguish them clearly from combinations based on the importance of order.

3. Calculate permutations of n distinct objects taken r at a time using the formula P(n,r) = n!/(n-r)!.

4. Solve problems involving permutations with repeated objects using the division formula.

5. Compute circular permutations for arrangements around a table and understand when to divide by 2.

6. Solve derangement problems where no element remains in its original position.

7. Apply permutation concepts to solve word arrangement problems and seating arrangement problems.
