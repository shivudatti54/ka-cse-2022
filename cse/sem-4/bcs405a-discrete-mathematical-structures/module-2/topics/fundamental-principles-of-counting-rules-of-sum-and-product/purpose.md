# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Understand and state the Fundamental Theorem of Counting (Rule of Sum and Rule of Product)

2. Apply the Rule of Sum to solve problems involving mutually exclusive choices

3. Apply the Rule of Product to solve problems involving sequential decisions

4. Distinguish between situations where sum rule applies versus product rule

5. Solve counting problems with restrictions (repetition allowed vs. not allowed)

6. Combine both rules appropriately to solve complex counting problems

7. Use complementary counting technique when direct counting is complex

8. Analyze real-world scenarios and formulate counting models for them
