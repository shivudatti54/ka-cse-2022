# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define and distinguish between universal quantifier (∀) and existential quantifier (∃) with appropriate examples

2. Determine the truth value of quantified statements for given domains of discourse

3. Apply De Morgan's Laws for quantifiers to negate statements involving ∀ and ∃

4. Translate English statements into precise symbolic logical notation using quantifiers

5. Analyze the meaning of statements with multiple quantifiers and understand why quantifier order matters

6. Identify bound and free variables in quantified expressions

7. Construct counterexamples to disprove universal statements and verify existential statements

8. Apply quantifier logic to solve real-world problems in computer science contexts
