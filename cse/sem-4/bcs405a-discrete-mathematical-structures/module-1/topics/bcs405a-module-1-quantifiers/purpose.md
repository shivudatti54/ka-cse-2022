# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Define and distinguish between universal (∀) and existential (∃) quantifiers with precise mathematical notation.

2. Identify predicates, determine their domains, and evaluate predicate statements for specific values.

3. Apply De Morgan's laws for quantifiers to negate statements involving ∀ and ∃ correctly.

4. Analyze statements with multiple quantifiers and recognize how the order of quantifiers affects meaning.

5. Distinguish between bound and free variables in quantified expressions.

6. Translate English statements into precise symbolic logical form using quantifiers.

7. Construct proofs and disproofs for statements involving quantifiers, including finding counterexamples.
