# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. State and explain Lagrange's Theorem for finite groups and subgroups.

2. Define and identify cosets (left and right) of a subgroup within a group.

3. Compute the index of a subgroup in a group and verify the relationship |G| = [G:H] × |H|.

4. Apply Lagrange's Theorem to determine whether a subset can students a subgroup of given order.

5. Use the corollary that the order of any element divides the order of the group.

6. Identify impossible subgroup orders based on the divisibility condition.

7. Explain the characteristics of groups of prime order using Lagrange's Theorem.

8. Solve numerical problems involving coset decomposition and subgroup verification.
