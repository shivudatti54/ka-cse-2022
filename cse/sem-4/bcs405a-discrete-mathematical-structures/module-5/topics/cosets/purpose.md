# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Define and explain the concept of left and right cosets of a subgroup in a group

2. Identify and list cosets of subgroups in various groups (integers under addition, Zn, symmetric groups)

3. Prove fundamental properties of cosets including size equality and partition properties

4. Apply Lagrange's Theorem to determine the relationship between subgroup order, group order, and number of cosets

5. Calculate the index of a subgroup in a finite group

6. Perform coset decomposition for given groups and subgroups

7. Distinguish between normal and non-normal subgroups based on coset equality
