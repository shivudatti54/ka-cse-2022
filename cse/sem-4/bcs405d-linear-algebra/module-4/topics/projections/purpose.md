# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Define and explain the concept of projection of a vector onto a subspace in linear algebra.

2. Derive and apply the formula for projecting a vector onto a line spanned by a given vector.

3. Construct the projection matrix onto the column space of a matrix and verify its properties.

4. Distinguish between orthogonal and oblique projections and explain when each is applicable.

5. Apply projection concepts to solve least squares problems and interpret the results.

6. Understand the relationship between projections, orthogonal complements, and the Gram-Schmidt process.

7. Compute projections in practical scenarios and verify results using properties of projection matrices.
