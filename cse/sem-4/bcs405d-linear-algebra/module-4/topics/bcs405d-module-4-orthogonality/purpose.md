# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Define and compute the inner product (dot product) of vectors in ℝⁿ and verify its properties.

2. Calculate the length (norm) of a vector and the distance between two vectors using the appropriate formulas.

3. Determine the angle between two vectors using the dot product formula and interpret orthogonal vectors.

4. Apply the Cauchy-Schwarz inequality and triangle inequality in problem-solving and proofs.

5. Find the orthogonal complement of a subspace and verify its properties.

6. Construct orthogonal and orthonormal sets of vectors from linearly independent vectors using the Gram-Schmidt process.

7. Verify whether a given set of vectors is orthogonal or orthonormal.

8. Apply the Pythagorean theorem for vectors to solve problems involving orthogonal vectors.
