# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Define and explain the concepts of linear combination, linear dependence, and linear independence of vectors in ℝⁿ.

2. Determine whether a given set of vectors is linearly dependent or independent using various methods including the definition, matrix method, and determinant test.

3. Identify the geometric interpretation of linear dependence and independence in ℝ² and ℝ³.

4. Apply the fundamental theorems related to linear dependence and independence, including the relationship with vector space dimension.

5. Find non-trivial linear dependence relationships among vectors when they exist.

6. Connect the concepts of linear independence with basis and dimension of vector spaces.

7. Solve examination problems involving verification of linear independence for vectors in ℝ², ℝ³, and ℝⁿ.
