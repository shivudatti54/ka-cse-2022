# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Define and distinguish between different types of matrices including row, column, square, diagonal, identity, zero, symmetric, and skew-symmetric matrices.

2. Perform matrix operations including addition, scalar multiplication, multiplication, and transposition while verifying dimension compatibility.

3. Compute and interpret determinants of 2×2 and 3×3 matrices using cofactor expansion and properties of determinants.

4. Solve systems of linear equations using Gaussian elimination, Gauss-Jordan elimination, and matrix inverse methods.

5. Determine the rank of a matrix and apply rank concepts to analyze the consistency and nature of solutions for linear systems.

6. Perform vector operations including addition, scalar multiplication, dot product, and compute vector magnitudes.

7. Apply theoretical concepts to solve students problems involving linear systems and matrix computations.
