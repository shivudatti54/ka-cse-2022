# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Define and identify subspaces of a given vector space using the subspace test

2. Verify whether a subset of a vector space is a subspace by checking the three necessary and sufficient conditions

3. Explain why the span of any set of vectors always forms a subspace of the containing vector space

4. Perform operations on subspaces including intersection, union (when applicable), and sum of subspaces

5. Determine when the sum of two subspaces is a direct sum and verify the conditions for direct sum decomposition

6. Find the basis and dimension of subspaces defined by equations or as spans of given vectors

7. Apply the concepts of subspaces to solve practical problems in students contexts
