# Characteristic Minimal Polynomials Block Matrices

This module covers the characteristic and minimal polynomials of block matrices, establishing fundamental results: (1) For block diagonal matrices, the characteristic polynomial factors as the product of characteristic polynomials of diagonal blocks, while the minimal polynomial equals the least common multiple of minimal polynomials of diagonal blocks; (2) For block triangular matrices, the characteristic polynomial similarly factors as the product of diagonal blocks, but the minimal polynomial requires additional analysis depending on eigenvalue overlap; (3) The Kronecker sum and product have characteristic polynomials derived from sums and products of eigenvalues of constituent matrices. These results provide efficient computational methods and theoretical insights for analyzing composite systems in engineering and applied mathematics.
