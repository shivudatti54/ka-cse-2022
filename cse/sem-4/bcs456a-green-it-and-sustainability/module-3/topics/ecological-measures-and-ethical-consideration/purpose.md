# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Explain the concept of ecological measures in Green IT and their significance in reducing environmental impact of technology operations.

2. Analyze data center energy efficiency using metrics like Power Usage Effectiveness (PUE) and recommend improvement strategies.

3. Describe electronic waste management practices including the 3R principle and Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR).

4. Evaluate software applications for energy efficiency and apply green software students principles.

5. Identify and explain ethical considerations in Green IT including digital divide, data ethics, and corporate social responsibility.

6. Apply ethical decision-making frameworks to IT procurement and sustainability initiatives.

7. Compare the ecological benefits of cloud computing and virtualization over traditional IT infrastructure.

8. Understand the concept of intergenerational equity and its relevance to sustainable technology development.
