# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Understand the concept of Green IT considerations and their importance in modern organizations

2. Analyze energy efficiency metrics such as PUE (Power Usage Effectiveness) and calculate data center energy performance

3. Evaluate hardware lifecycle stages and identify green considerations at each stage: procurement, deployment, usage, and disposal

4. Apply the Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) approach to compare green and conventional IT solutions

5. Design basic e-waste management policies following environmental compliance requirements

6. Explain the role of virtualization, cloud computing, and software optimization in Green IT strategies

7. Assess organizational readiness for Green IT implementation considering policy, cost, and human factors

8. Make informed procurement decisions by evaluating vendor sustainability credentials and product environmental ratings
