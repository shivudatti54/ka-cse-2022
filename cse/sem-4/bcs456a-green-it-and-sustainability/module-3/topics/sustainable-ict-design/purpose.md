# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Explain the fundamental principles of systems students and how they apply to sustainable ICT design.

2. Describe the key green ICT design principles including energy efficiency, resource conservation, and pollution prevention.

3. Analyze the sustainable systems students process and its integration throughout the ICT lifecycle phases.

4. Apply Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) methodology to evaluate environmental impacts of ICT products and services.

5. Calculate and interpret green metrics such as Power Usage Effectiveness (PUE) and Carbon Usage Effectiveness (CUE).

6. Evaluate the role of virtualization and cloud computing in achieving sustainable ICT infrastructure.

7. Design green IT solutions by applying Design for Environment (DfE) principles.

8. Explain the importance of proper e-waste management and implement sustainable IT asset disposition processes.

9. Compare different approaches to sustainable systems design through trade-off analysis.

10. Apply systems students methodology to develop ICT solutions that meet both functional and environmental requirements.
