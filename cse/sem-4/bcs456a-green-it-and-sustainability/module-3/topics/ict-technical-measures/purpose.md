# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Explain the concept of ICT technical measures and their role in achieving Green IT sustainability goals.

2. Analyze energy-efficient hardware design principles including processor power management, storage technologies, and display systems.

3. Evaluate the benefits of virtualization technology for server consolidation and resource optimization in enterprise environments.

4. Calculate and interpret Power Usage Effectiveness (PUE) as a metric for data center energy efficiency.

5. Design power management strategies for computing systems including sleep modes, dynamic scaling, and operating system configurations.

6. Apply green coding principles to develop energy-efficient software applications.

7. Assess network energy management techniques including Energy-Efficient Ethernet and virtual LAN optimization.

8. Evaluate end-of-life strategies for IT equipment including e-waste management, recycling, and lifecycle assessment approaches.
