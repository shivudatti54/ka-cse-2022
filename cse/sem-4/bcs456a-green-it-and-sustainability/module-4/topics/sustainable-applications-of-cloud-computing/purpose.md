# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Define sustainable cloud computing and explain its importance in modern IT infrastructure

2. Describe energy-efficient data center design principles and their role in reducing cloud computing's environmental impact

3. Analyze how virtualization and resource consolidation contribute to energy savings in cloud environments

4. Evaluate different workload scheduling strategies for optimizing energy consumption and carbon emissions

5. Interpret green cloud metrics including PUE, Carbon Efficiency, and Virtual Machine Efficiency Ratio (VMER)

6. Explain the benefits of serverless computing in achieving sustainability goals in cloud deployments

7. Assess the environmental impact of cloud service models (IaaS, PaaS, SaaS) and identify optimization opportunities

8. Design basic strategies for implementing sustainable cloud solutions considering workload characteristics and energy source availability
