# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define green data centers and explain their environmental and economic significance in modern IT infrastructure.

2. Understand and calculate Power Usage Effectiveness (PUE) and interpret what different PUE values indicate about data center efficiency.

3. Explain how virtualization technology contributes to energy efficiency and resource optimization in data centers.

4. Describe various free cooling techniques and advanced cooling technologies used in green data centers.

5. Analyze the role of renewable energy integration in achieving carbon-neutral data center operations.

6. Identify key strategies for implementing energy-efficient computing practices across IT operations.

7. Evaluate the trade-offs between different green computing technologies and their applicability in various organizational contexts.

8. Apply knowledge of green data center principles to propose practical sustainability improvements for existing IT infrastructure.
