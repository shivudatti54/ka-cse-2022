# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define Green IT and Green Computing, and explain their significance in modern technology environments

2. Identify and describe the three pillars of sustainability and their relevance to Information Technology operations

3. Analyze the environmental impact of IT operations including energy consumption, e-waste generation, and carbon emissions

4. Explain the role of virtualization and cloud computing in achieving Green IT objectives

5. Describe key Green IT frameworks and strategies such as Green Grid, ENERGY STAR, and ISO 14001

6. Apply the concept of Power Usage Effectiveness (PUE) to evaluate data center energy efficiency

7. Discuss the principles of sustainable software development and its contribution to energy conservation

8. Evaluate the economic and environmental benefits of implementing Green IT practices in organizations
