# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Explain the concept of Green IT and its importance in modern computing environments

2. Identify and describe the key components of the Green IT agenda, including energy efficiency, virtualization, and sustainable manufacturing

3. Analyze the environmental impact of IT operations, including carbon footprint and e-waste generation

4. Evaluate the challenges organizations face in implementing Green IT initiatives, including economic, technical, and organizational barriers

5. Understand regulatory frameworks such as RoHS, WEEE, and Energy Star that guide Green IT implementation

6. Apply Green IT principles to design sustainable solutions for real-world IT scenarios

7. Demonstrate knowledge of e-waste management practices and the importance of responsible electronics disposal

8. Connect Green IT concepts to broader sustainability goals and environmental conservation efforts
