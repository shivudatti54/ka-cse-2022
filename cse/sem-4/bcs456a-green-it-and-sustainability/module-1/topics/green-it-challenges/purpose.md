# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Analyze the key challenges facing organizations in implementing Green IT practices, including energy consumption, e-waste management, and supply chain sustainability.

2. Evaluate the trade-offs between environmental sustainability and other business priorities such as cost, performance, and security in IT operations.

3. Apply quantitative methods to assess energy consumption, carbon footprint, and cost implications of IT infrastructure decisions.

4. Compare different approaches to e-waste management and circular economy implementation in IT organizations.

5. Assess the role of organizational culture, leadership, and governance in overcoming barriers to sustainable IT practices.

6. Interpret relevant environmental regulations and standards applicable to IT operations in the Indian context.

7. Design strategies for addressing Green IT challenges that balance short-term business needs with long-term sustainability goals.

8. Critically analyze emerging challenges in sustainable computing, including the environmental impact of emerging technologies like AI and blockchain.
