# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Explain the concept of connected devices and the Internet of Things (IoT) in the context of Green IT and Sustainability.

2. Analyze the categories of connected devices including consumer, industrial, commercial, and infrastructure IoT devices.

3. Evaluate the energy consumption patterns of connected devices including active power, standby power, and network energy requirements.

4. Describe the environmental impact of data centers that support IoT ecosystems, including cooling requirements and carbon emissions.

5. Assess the electronic waste (e-waste) challenges associated with the proliferation of connected devices and their disposal.

6. Calculate the carbon footprint of connected devices considering manufacturing, usage, and end-of-life phases.

7. Propose sustainable strategies for IoT deployment including edge computing, energy-efficient protocols, and green data center practices.

8. Apply green IT principles to minimize the environmental impact of connected device ecosystems in various applications.
