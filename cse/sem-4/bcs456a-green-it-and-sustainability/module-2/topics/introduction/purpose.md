# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define Green IT and Green Computing, explaining their significance in modern technology environments

2. Identify and describe the major environmental impacts of IT operations, including energy consumption and e-waste generation

3. Explain the four fundamental principles of sustainable computing: energy efficiency, resource conservation, waste reduction, and carbon footprint reduction

4. Analyze the economic, environmental, and social benefits of adopting Green IT practices in organizations

5. Evaluate practical examples of Green IT implementation, including virtualization, green data centers, and e-waste management

6. Understand the historical evolution of Green IT and recognize key milestones in sustainable computing development

7. Apply Green IT principles to propose sustainable solutions for typical IT infrastructure challenges
