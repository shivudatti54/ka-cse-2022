# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Understand the concept of Green IT and its importance in modern computing environments.

2. Explain various energy-efficient hardware technologies including DVFS, SSDs, and Energy Star certification.

3. Analyze power management techniques and their implementation in IT infrastructure.

4. Evaluate the benefits of virtualization technology for reducing energy consumption and improving resource utilization.

5. Describe how cloud computing contributes to Green IT through resource pooling and on-demand scaling.

6. Calculate Power Usage Effectiveness (PUE) and interpret its significance for data center efficiency.

7. Apply green coding principles to develop energy-efficient software applications.

8. Propose strategies for electronic waste management and sustainable IT practices in organizations.
