# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Explain the relationship between computing speed, system reliability, and energy consumption in modern IT systems.

2. Analyze the environmental impact of increased demand for performance and describe how Green IT practices address these concerns.

3. Evaluate different technologies and strategies for achieving optimal performance-per-watt ratios in computing systems.

4. Design sustainable data center solutions that balance speed, reliability, and environmental responsibility.

5. Apply reliability students principles to minimize electronic waste while maintaining system availability.

6. Assess the trade-offs involved in balancing performance requirements with sustainability objectives in real-world scenarios.

7. Recommend appropriate technologies and practices for achieving sustainable high-performance computing in enterprise environments.

8. Compare traditional IT approaches with Green IT methodologies for addressing speed and reliability demands.
