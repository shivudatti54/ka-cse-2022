# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Explain the concept of Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS) and its role in sustainable traffic management.

2. Describe various green routing and eco-navigation strategies used to minimize fuel consumption and emissions.

3. Analyze Traffic Demand Management (TDM) measures and their effectiveness in reducing traffic congestion and environmental impact.

4. Evaluate the integration of public transportation with traffic control systems for sustainable urban mobility.

5. Understand the principles of traffic flow optimization and its contribution to emission reduction.

6. Apply mathematical calculations to estimate fuel savings and emission reductions from traffic control improvements.

7. Compare different congestion pricing strategies and their environmental benefits.

8. Design basic solutions for eco-friendly traffic management in smart city applications.
