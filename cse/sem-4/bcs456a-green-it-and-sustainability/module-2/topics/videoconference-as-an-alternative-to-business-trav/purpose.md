# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Explain the environmental impact of business travel, including carbon emissions from air travel and associated activities.

2. Describe video conferencing technology, including its components, types of systems, and operational mechanisms.

3. Analyze the carbon footprint reduction achieved by replacing business travel with video conferencing using comparative calculations.

4. Evaluate the economic benefits of video conferencing implementation, including cost savings and return on investment analysis.

5. Identify the technical infrastructure requirements for effective video conferencing deployment in organizations.

6. Develop strategies for implementing video conferencing as a sustainable alternative to business travel, considering organizational policies and best practices.

7. Recognize the limitations and challenges of video conferencing and propose appropriate hybrid solutions for different business scenarios.

8. Apply Green IT principles to recommend video conferencing solutions that minimize environmental impact while maintaining business effectiveness.
