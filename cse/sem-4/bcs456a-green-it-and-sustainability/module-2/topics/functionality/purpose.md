# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Explain the concept of functionality in Green IT and its importance in achieving sustainable computing solutions.

2. Describe how energy-efficient computing techniques, including dynamic voltage scaling and power management features, contribute to sustainability while maintaining system performance.

3. Analyze the role of virtualization and containerization in improving resource utilization and reducing environmental impact of IT infrastructure.

4. Evaluate the benefits of cloud computing in achieving sustainability goals through scalable resources and energy-efficient data center operations.

5. Apply green software development principles to create applications that minimize energy consumption and resource requirements.

6. Design sustainable data center operations incorporating cooling optimization, monitoring systems, and energy-efficient infrastructure management.

7. Assess the environmental impact of IT operations using quantitative methods and propose optimization strategies for improved sustainability.
