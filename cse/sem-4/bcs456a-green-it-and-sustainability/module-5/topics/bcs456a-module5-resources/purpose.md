# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Identify and utilize appropriate textbooks and reference books for studying Green IT concepts.

2. Access and benefit from online learning platforms that complement the the syllabus.

3. Locate and interpret relevant research papers and journal articles from academic databases.

4. Use practical software tools for measuring and improving IT energy efficiency.

5. Analyze case studies from industry to understand real-world implementations of Green IT strategies.

6. Critically evaluate different Green IT resources and select appropriate materials for specific learning needs.

7. Apply knowledge from various resources to solve numerical problems related to energy efficiency calculations.

8. Develop a comprehensive understanding of Green IT that integrates theoretical knowledge with practical applications.

9. Prepare effectively for university examinations using the suggested learning resources and study materials.

10. Create a personal learning plan using multiple resources to achieve academic excellence in Green IT and Sustainability.
