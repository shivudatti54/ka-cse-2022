# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Explain the concept of energy consumption in software applications and differentiate between direct and indirect energy usage

2. Describe various energy profiling and measurement techniques used in application analysis

3. Identify energy-intensive code patterns and explain how they impact overall power consumption

4. Apply green software students principles to design energy-efficient applications

5. Analyze code-level optimizations for reducing energy consumption in applications

6. Evaluate different energy optimization strategies and their trade-offs

7. Use appropriate tools and methodologies for measuring application energy consumption

8. Understand the role of dynamic resource management in minimizing application energy footprint
