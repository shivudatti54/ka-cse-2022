# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Define Green IT and explain its importance in modern computing environments

2. Identify and describe the three pillars of Green IT: environmental sustainability, economic viability, and social responsibility

3. Explain the various dimensions of Green IT including green data centers, green hardware, and green software

4. Discuss the strategies for implementing Green IT such as virtualisation, cloud computing, and power management

5. Analyze the environmental impact of IT operations including energy consumption and e-waste generation

6. Evaluate the benefits of Green IT initiatives from economic and environmental perspectives

7. Apply the concept of sustainable IT lifecycle management to real-world scenarios

8. Compare different green computing certifications and standards such as Energy Star and EPEAT
