# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Define Green Information Technology and explain its significance in modern computing environments
2. Describe the key principles and framework of Green IT implementation in organizations
3. Analyze data center energy efficiency metrics and calculate Power Usage Effectiveness (PUE)
4. Evaluate the benefits of virtualization and cloud computing for sustainable IT operations
5. Explain electronic waste management strategies and the importance of proper e-waste disposal
6. Apply green software students principles to develop energy-efficient applications
7. Compare different Green IT strategies and recommend appropriate solutions for given scenarios
8. Assess the environmental and economic benefits of implementing Green IT practices in organizations
