# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Explain the concept of Product Life Cycle and its relevance to sustainable software design in the context of Green IT.

2. Identify and describe each phase of the software product life cycle and the environmental considerations associated with each phase.

3. Analyze how design decisions made during early phases of software development impact environmental sustainability throughout the product's lifespan.

4. Compare linear and circular life cycle models and explain how circular economy principles apply to software products.

5. Evaluate the carbon footprint of software applications across different life cycle phases and identify opportunities for reduction.

6. Apply life cycle thinking to make informed decisions about software architecture, development practices, and maintenance strategies.

7. Recommend sustainable practices for each phase of the product life cycle in software students contexts.
