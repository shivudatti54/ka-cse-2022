# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Explain the concept of evaluating sustainability effects and its significance in modern IT environments

2. Describe the three pillars of sustainability (environmental, economic, and social) and their relevance to IT operations

3. Apply key sustainability metrics including PUE, CUE, and carbon footprint calculation methodologies

4. Compare and contrast different sustainability evaluation frameworks such as LCA, ISO 14001, and GRI standards

5. Analyze the environmental impact of IT infrastructure through quantitative assessment of energy consumption and emissions

6. Evaluate e-waste management options and recommend sustainable disposal strategies

7. Interpret sustainability performance indicators and their application in organizational decision-making

8. Design a basic sustainability evaluation plan for an IT department or data center operation
