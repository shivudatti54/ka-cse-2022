This study material covers Heaps and Heapsort as part of the Transform-and-Conquer algorithmic paradigm. It provides comprehensive coverage of binary heap data structures, their array representations, and operations including Heapify, Build-Heap, Insert, and Extract. The material includes formal proofs for time complexity, detailed worked examples, and comparative analysis with other sorting algorithms. This content is designed for B.Tech/MSc/MCA students to develop understanding of in-place sorting algorithms, priority queue implementations, and rigorous algorithm analysis.
