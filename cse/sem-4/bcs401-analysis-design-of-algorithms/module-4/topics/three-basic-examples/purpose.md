# Three Basic Examples

This chapter introduces three fundamental dynamic programming problems: Fibonacci sequence calculation, Binomial Coefficient computation, and Minimum Coin Change. It demonstrates both top-down (memoization) and bottom-up (tabulation) approaches, proving optimal substructure and overlapping subproblems for each problem. The content establishes rigorous mathematical foundations with complexity analysis and space optimization techniques, preparing students to apply DP to more complex algorithmic challenges.
