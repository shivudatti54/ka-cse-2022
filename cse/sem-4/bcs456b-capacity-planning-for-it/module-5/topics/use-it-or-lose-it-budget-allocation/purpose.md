# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Explain the "Use It or Lose It" principle and its significance in IT budget management and capacity planning.

2. Distinguish between Capital Expenditure (CapEx) and Operational Expenditure (OpEx) and their relationship to budget utilization policies.

3. Analyze how the budget cycle and utilization metrics influence IT capacity planning decisions.

4. Evaluate the consequences of year-end spending behavior on organizational resource allocation and technical efficiency.

5. Assess the impact of cloud computing models on traditional "Use It or Lose It" dynamics in IT environments.

6. Apply the "wallet" metaphor to real-world scenarios involving IT budget optimization and capacity planning.

7. Propose strategies to balance budget utilization requirements with efficient technical resource management.

8. Critically examine case scenarios involving budget underutilization or overcommitment in IT infrastructure planning.
