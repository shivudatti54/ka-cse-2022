# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Define virtualization and explain its role in modern IT infrastructure and capacity planning
2. Identify and describe the five main types of virtualization: server, desktop, network, storage, and application virtualization
3. Explain the architecture and functioning of hypervisors, distinguishing between Type 1 (bare-metal) and Type 2 (hosted) hypervisors
4. Analyze how virtualization improves hardware utilization rates and contributes to cost optimization in IT capacity planning
5. Apply virtualization concepts to solve capacity planning scenarios involving server consolidation and workload scaling
6. Evaluate the benefits and limitations of virtualization in enterprise IT environments
7. Understand the relationship between virtualization and cloud computing in the context of IT service delivery
