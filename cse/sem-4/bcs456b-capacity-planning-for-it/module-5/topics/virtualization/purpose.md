# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Explain the concept of virtualization and its role in modern IT infrastructure, including the different types of virtualization (server, desktop, network, storage).

2. Describe hypervisor architecture and distinguish between Type 1 (bare-metal) and Type 2 (hosted) hypervisors, including their appropriate use cases.

3. Identify and explain the three main cloud computing service models: Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS), Platform as a Service (PaaS), and Software as a Service (SaaS).

4. Compare and contrast cloud deployment models (public, private, hybrid) and recommend appropriate models based on organizational requirements.

5. Apply capacity planning principles to virtualized environments, including calculating server consolidation ratios and resource utilization metrics.

6. Design auto-scaling configurations for cloud-based applications, including threshold setting and scaling policy definition.

7. Evaluate cost optimization strategies in cloud computing, including on-demand, reserved, and spot instance pricing models.

8. Analyze real-world scenarios to recommend appropriate virtualization and cloud computing solutions for enterprise capacity planning challenges.
