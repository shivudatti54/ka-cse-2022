# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Identify and explain the major issues encountered in IT capacity planning, including forecasting, cost management, and scalability challenges.

2. Analyze the root causes of capacity planning failures and understand how organizational, technical, and strategic factors contribute to these issues.

3. Compare and contrast cloud-based versus on-premise capacity solutions, evaluating the advantages and disadvantages of each approach in different business contexts.

4. Evaluate the impact of technology obsolescence and migration challenges on long-term capacity planning strategies.

5. Recommend appropriate mitigation strategies for common capacity planning issues based on organizational requirements and constraints.

6. Understand the relationship between capacity planning and other IT service management processes, including performance management and service level management.

7. Apply knowledge of capacity planning issues to analyze real-world case studies and propose practical solutions.
