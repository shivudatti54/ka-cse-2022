# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Define procurement in the context of IT capacity planning and explain its strategic importance for organizational operations.

2. Describe the complete procurement process lifecycle, including all ten phases from need identification through ongoing vendor management.

3. Differentiate between various types of IT procurement including hardware, software, cloud, and services procurement.

4. Apply the procurement process to practical scenarios involving server procurement and cloud service acquisition.

5. Evaluate IT procurement options using criteria such as total cost of ownership, vendor capabilities, compliance, and scalability.

6. Analyze the importance of Service Level Agreements (SLAs) and contractual terms in IT procurement decisions.

7. Recognize common risks in IT procurement including vendor lock-in, hidden costs, and compliance gaps.

8. Understand the role of strategic sourcing in achieving cost efficiency and optimal resource utilization in IT capacity planning.
