# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Define capacity planning and explain its significance in IT infrastructure management
2. Identify and explain the three main types of capacity planning: resource, business, and service capacity planning
3. Describe the four-phase capacity planning process and explain each phase in detail
4. Analyze key capacity planning metrics including utilization, throughput, response time, and saturation point
5. Apply capacity planning models to predict future resource requirements for given scenarios
6. Distinguish between capacity planning and performance tuning, understanding their complementary roles
7. Evaluate workload characteristics and their impact on capacity requirements
8. Make informed capacity planning decisions considering cost, performance, and business requirements
