# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Define capacity, utilization, and threshold in the context of IT systems and explain their interrelationships

2. Identify and explain the three types of thresholds (warning, critical, and failure) and their significance in preventing system failures

3. Apply different growth prediction models (linear, exponential, and CAGR) to forecast when systems will reach capacity limits

4. Perform trend analysis using moving averages and linear regression techniques on historical utilization data

5. Calculate the time remaining before a system reaches its capacity threshold given current utilization and growth rate

6. Determine appropriate planning horizons based on organizational needs and procurement lead times

7. Analyze workload characteristics to understand what drives resource consumption in IT systems

8. Make informed capacity planning decisions that include appropriate safety margins and lead times
