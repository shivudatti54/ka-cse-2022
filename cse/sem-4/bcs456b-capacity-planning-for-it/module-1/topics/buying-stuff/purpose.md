# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Explain the make vs. buy decision framework and identify factors that influence this critical choice in IT procurement.

2. Calculate and interpret Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) for different procurement options, considering both visible and hidden costs.

3. Apply vendor evaluation criteria and use weighted scoring methods to select appropriate IT vendors and solutions.

4. Differentiate between traditional procurement models (CapEx) and cloud-based procurement models (OpEx), understanding their respective advantages and disadvantages.

5. Analyze the three cloud service models—IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS—and determine appropriate scenarios for their use in capacity planning.

6. Interpret Service Level Agreements (SLAs) and understand their implications for IT service delivery and capacity management.

7. Align procurement decisions with capacity planning requirements to ensure optimal resource utilization and cost efficiency.
