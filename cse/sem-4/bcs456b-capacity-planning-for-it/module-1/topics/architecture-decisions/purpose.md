# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Explain the fundamental concepts of architecture decisions and their role in IT capacity planning

2. Compare and contrast different architectural styles (monolithic, microservices, client-server) and analyze their implications for capacity planning

3. Evaluate deployment models (on-premises, cloud, hybrid) based on scalability, cost, and operational characteristics

4. Apply scalability patterns (vertical scaling, horizontal scaling, auto-scaling) to appropriate scenarios

5. Analyze performance versus cost trade-offs in architecture decision-making

6. Select appropriate technology solutions based on workload characteristics and capacity requirements

7. Perform basic TCO comparisons between different architectural options

8. Justify architecture decisions for specific capacity planning scenarios commonly encountered in enterprise IT environments
