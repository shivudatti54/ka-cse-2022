# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Define measurement and explain its role in capacity planning for IT infrastructure
2. Distinguish between quantitative and qualitative measurement techniques
3. Identify and explain the key metrics used for CPU, memory, disk, and network resources
4. Apply measurement techniques including baseline measurement, trend analysis, and benchmarking
5. Calculate CPU utilization, memory utilization, and storage growth projections
6. Analyze measurement data to identify performance bottlenecks and resource constraints
7. Recommend appropriate capacity planning actions based on measurement results
8. Evaluate the appropriateness of different measurement tools and techniques for various scenarios
