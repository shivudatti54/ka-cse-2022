# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Explain the concept of long-term trends in IT capacity planning and their significance in infrastructure management decisions.

2. Analyze exponential data growth patterns and their implications for storage, processing, and network capacity requirements.

3. Evaluate the evolution of cloud computing and the emergence of hybrid multi-cloud architectures in capacity planning strategies.

4. Assess the impact of artificial intelligence and machine learning workloads on computational resource requirements and hardware selection.

5. Design distributed computing strategies incorporating edge computing to address latency, bandwidth, and data sovereignty challenges.

6. Incorporate sustainability and energy efficiency considerations into capacity planning decisions using metrics like Power Usage Effectiveness.

7. Apply trend analysis techniques to project future capacity requirements based on historical data and market predictions.

8. Compare short-term versus long-term capacity planning approaches and determine appropriate strategies for different organizational contexts.
