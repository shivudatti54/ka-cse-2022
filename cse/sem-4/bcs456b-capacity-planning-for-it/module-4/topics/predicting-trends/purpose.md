# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Explain the importance of trend prediction in IT capacity planning and its role in proactive resource management.

2. Apply time series analysis techniques to identify patterns, trends, and seasonal variations in historical capacity data.

3. Calculate forecasts using moving averages and exponential smoothing methods, and interpret the results for capacity planning decisions.

4. Perform linear regression analysis to establish relationships between business drivers and resource requirements.

5. Select appropriate forecasting methods based on data characteristics, business context, and accuracy requirements.

6. Evaluate forecast accuracy using metrics such as MAE, MAPE, and MSE to improve prediction models.

7. Integrate both quantitative and qualitative forecasting approaches to develop comprehensive capacity predictions.
