# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Define procurement in the context of IT capacity planning and explain its strategic importance in IT service management.

2. Describe the complete procurement lifecycle and explain each phase from requirements identification to asset management.

3. Calculate and interpret Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) for IT procurement decisions, considering all direct and indirect costs.

4. Apply vendor selection criteria to evaluate and compare different suppliers based on technical, financial, and operational factors.

5. Differentiate between various procurement models including traditional, leasing, cloud, and managed services with their respective advantages and disadvantages.

6. Explain the components and importance of Service Level Agreements (SLAs) in IT procurement contracts.

7. Analyze procurement decisions by comparing alternatives such as on-premise versus cloud infrastructure using TCO and other relevant metrics.

8. Understand the relationship between capacity planning analysis and procurement execution in IT infrastructure management.
