# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Explain the concept of deployment in IT infrastructure and its significance in capacity planning.

2. Compare and contrast different deployment strategies including blue-green, canary, rolling deployment, and feature flags.

3. Analyze how deployment strategies impact infrastructure capacity requirements and resource utilization.

4. Describe the deployment pipeline and its role in automated software delivery.

5. Understand the role of containerization and orchestration in modern deployment approaches.

6. Evaluate deployment scenarios and recommend appropriate strategies based on capacity constraints and business requirements.

7. Calculate infrastructure requirements for different deployment strategies during migration and updates.

8. Design rollback mechanisms that minimize downtime and maintain service availability.
