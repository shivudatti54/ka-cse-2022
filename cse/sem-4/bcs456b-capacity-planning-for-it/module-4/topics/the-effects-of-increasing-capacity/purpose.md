# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Analyze the direct performance effects of capacity increases, including impacts on throughput, latency, and resource utilization metrics.

2. Evaluate the economic implications of capacity expansion, including capital expenditure, operational expenditure, and total cost of ownership considerations.

3. Explain the bottleneck shifting phenomenon and its implications for effective capacity planning decisions.

4. Compare vertical scaling (scale-up) versus horizontal scaling (scale-out) approaches and their respective advantages and limitations.

5. Assess the operational complexity effects associated with capacity increases, including management, monitoring, and maintenance requirements.

6. Apply the concept of diminishing returns to determine optimal capacity expansion points in IT infrastructure.

7. Design capacity increase strategies that account for both immediate performance benefits and long-term organizational needs.

8. Critically evaluate capacity planning decisions using total cost of ownership analysis and return on investment considerations.
