# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Explain the concept of demand patterns in IT systems, including seasonal, trend, cyclical, and random variations, and provide examples of each in IT infrastructure contexts.

2. Apply time series analysis techniques, including moving averages and exponential smoothing, to forecast future IT resource demands from historical data.

3. Perform linear regression analysis to identify long-term trends in demand and predict future capacity requirements.

4. Analyze the concept of "capacity planning waves" and explain how organizations can prepare for different phases of demand cycles.

5. Compare and contrast qualitative and quantitative forecasting methods, and determine appropriate methods for different capacity planning scenarios.

6. Evaluate the accuracy and limitations of different trend prediction techniques in real-world IT environments.

7. Apply trend prediction concepts to practical capacity planning scenarios including server provisioning, network bandwidth planning, and cloud resource allocation.

8. Interpret forecasting results and make informed capacity planning decisions based on predicted trends.
