# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Explain the purpose and importance of design guidelines in UI/UX design practice.

2. Identify and describe the key principles of major design guideline systems including Material Design, iOS Human Interface Guidelines, and WCAG accessibility standards.

3. Interpret design guidelines appropriately for different platforms, screen sizes, and device types while maintaining design consistency.

4. Apply design tokens and component libraries to ensure consistency across digital products.

5. Implement accessibility requirements from design guidelines to create inclusive user experiences.

6. Adapt design guidelines for responsive web applications across desktop, tablet, and mobile viewports.

7. Evaluate design decisions against established guidelines to ensure compliance and best practices.
