# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Explain the role and importance of sketching in the UI/UX design process

2. Distinguish between different types of sketches: thumbnail sketches, wireframes, and high-fidelity sketches

3. Apply the sketching process to generate and evaluate multiple design solutions

4. Select appropriate sketching tools and materials for different design contexts

5. Create effective wireframe sketches that communicate layout and functionality

6. Understand how sketching facilitates collaboration in design teams and stakeholder meetings

7. Apply sketching principles to create clear, communicative design visualizations

8. Integrate sketching into the broader UX design workflow, understanding its relationship with prototyping and testing
