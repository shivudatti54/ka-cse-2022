# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Define Design Thinking and explain its significance in UI/UX design contexts
2. Describe and apply the five stages of Design Thinking: Empathize, Define, Ideate, Prototype, and Test
3. Analyze the Double Diamond model and understand the concepts of divergent and convergent thinking
4. Create and interpret empathy maps for understanding user needs and motivations
5. Compare Design Thinking with traditional problem-solving approaches
6. Apply Design Thinking methodology to solve real-world UI/UX design problems
7. Explain why the Design Thinking process is iterative and human-centered
8. Identify appropriate techniques for each stage of the Design Thinking process
