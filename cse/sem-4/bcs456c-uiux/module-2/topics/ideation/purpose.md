# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Define ideation and explain its role in the UX design process
2. Identify and describe various ideation techniques including brainstorming, SCAMPER, mind mapping, and worst possible idea
3. Apply the ideation process to generate solutions for given user problems
4. Evaluate generated ideas using appropriate criteria such as feasibility, desirability, and viability
5. Differentiate between idea generation and idea selection phases in design thinking
6. Understand how ideation connects user research to prototyping in the Double Diamond model
7. Select appropriate ideation techniques based on project constraints and team dynamics
8. Recognize the importance of deferring judgment during the ideation phase
