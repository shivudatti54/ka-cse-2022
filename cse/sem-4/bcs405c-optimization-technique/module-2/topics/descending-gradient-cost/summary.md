# Gradient Descent - Summary

## Algorithm

w = w - α × ∂J/∂w

## Learning Rate (α)

- Small: slow convergence
- Large: may diverge
- Adaptive: self-adjusting

## Types

- Batch: entire dataset
- Stochastic: single sample
- Mini-batch: small batches

## Exam Tips

1. Remember: subtract gradient (go downhill)
2. Learning rate must be tuned
3. Local minima can trap optimization
