# Bcs402 M5 Allocation Policy

This content provides comprehensive coverage of cache miss allocation policies for students/students level students studying microcontrollers and computer architecture. It establishes theoretical foundations through formal definitions and mathematical proofs (AMAT derivations), integrates ARM Coprocessor 15 context for embedded systems relevance, and develops analytical skills through numerical examples and performance comparisons. The content prepares students to design memory systems for ARM-based embedded applications by understanding policy trade-offs.
