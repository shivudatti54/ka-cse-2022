This study material provides comprehensive theoretical and practical coverage of Raspberry Pi hardware interfaces for IoT applications at the B.Tech/MSc/MCA level. It establishes a rigorous foundation in GPIO operations, serial communication protocols (I²C, SPI, UART), and peripheral interfaces. The mathematical derivations, protocol specifications, and electrical characteristics enable students to analyze, design, and troubleshoot embedded IoT systems. The assessment section evaluates both conceptual understanding and quantitative problem-solving abilities essential for engineering practice.
