This module provides comprehensive coverage of the logical design of IoT systems, enabling students to understand the abstract architectural components, communication protocols, and data processing pipelines without hardware dependencies. Students will learn to analyze IoT systems from a functional perspective, select appropriate communication protocols based on system requirements, design data flow architectures, and evaluate processing tier placements (edge, fog, cloud). The content prepares students for IoT system design, protocol selection decisions, and architectural trade-off analysis at the professional level.
