This module introduces the fundamental concepts, architectures, and design principles of the Internet of Things (IoT). It establishes a comprehensive foundation covering IoT definition, evolutionary context, layered architecture, physical and logical design components, communication protocols, enabling technologies, and deployment challenges. The content progresses from theoretical foundations to practical implementation considerations, preparing B.Tech/MSc/MCA students for advanced IoT system design and analysis. The module develops competencies in understanding IoT hardware components, selecting appropriate communication protocols, evaluating architectural trade-offs, and addressing real-world deployment challenges.
