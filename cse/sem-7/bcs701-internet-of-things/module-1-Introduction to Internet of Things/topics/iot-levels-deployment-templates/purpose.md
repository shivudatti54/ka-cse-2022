This content establishes a comprehensive understanding of IoT system classification and deployment strategies, essential for designing scalable, efficient, and cost-effective IoT solutions. Students learn to analyze trade-offs between edge and cloud computing, select appropriate architectural patterns based on latency, bandwidth, and computational requirements, and make informed decisions for enterprise-grade IoT deployments. The formal classification framework and comparative analysis enable systematic evaluation of IoT architectures, preparing students for real-world system design challenges in industries ranging from smart cities to industrial automation.
