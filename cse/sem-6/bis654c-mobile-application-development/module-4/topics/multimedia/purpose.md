### Learning Purpose: Android Multimedia

**1. Why is this topic important?**
Multimedia capabilities -- audio playback, video playback, and media recording -- are core features of modern mobile applications. Understanding the MediaPlayer class, its state machine, lifecycle management, and audio focus handling is essential for building apps that correctly play, pause, and release media resources.

**2. Real-world applications:**
These skills are used in music streaming apps, video players, podcast applications, educational platforms with audio/video content, social media apps with media playback, and any application that needs to play sound effects, background music, or video clips. Proper media handling ensures a smooth user experience.

**3. Connection to other topics:**
Multimedia builds on the Activity lifecycle (media must be paused/stopped when Activities are backgrounded), Services (background audio playback requires a foreground Service), and the Android system architecture (understanding the media framework layer). It also connects to the extended multimedia topic covering system architecture, audio/video playback, and text-to-speech.
