# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Understand the fundamental concept, core principles, and characteristics of Decentralized Autonomous Organizations (DAOs)

2. Explain how DAOs leverage blockchain technology and smart contracts to achieve decentralized governance

3. Compare DAOs with traditional hierarchical organizations in terms of structure, decision-making, and operational efficiency

4. Analyze the various governance mechanisms used in DAOs, including token-based voting, quadratic voting, and conviction voting

5. Apply the principles of DAO design to create a basic organizational structure with defined rules and procedures

6. Evaluate the advantages, limitations, and challenges associated with DAOs, including security risks and regulatory concerns

7. Design a simple DAO framework for a real-world use case, specifying tokenomics, voting mechanisms, and execution protocols
