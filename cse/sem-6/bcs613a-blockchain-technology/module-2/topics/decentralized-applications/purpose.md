# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define decentralized applications and their characteristics.
2. Explain the benefits of decentralized applications.
3. Identify popular platforms for building decentralized applications.
4. Provide examples of decentralized applications.
5. Understand the development process of decentralized applications.
6. Analyze the strengths and weaknesses of decentralized applications.
7. Evaluate the potential use cases of decentralized applications.
