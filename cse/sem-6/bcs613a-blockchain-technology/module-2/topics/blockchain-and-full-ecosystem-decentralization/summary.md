# Blockchain and Full Ecosystem Decentralization

Summary

- Blockchain decentralization refers to the distribution of control and decision-making power across a network of nodes.
- Full ecosystem decentralization refers to the decentralization of all aspects of a system, including infrastructure, applications, and governance.
- The benefits of blockchain decentralization include security, transparency, and autonomy.
- The benefits of full ecosystem decentralization include increased security, improved transparency, and greater autonomy.

- Full ecosystem decentralization offers many benefits, including increased security, improved transparency, and greater autonomy.
