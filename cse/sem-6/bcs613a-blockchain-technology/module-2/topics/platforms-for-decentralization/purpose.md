# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define decentralization and its importance in blockchain technology.
2. Identify the different types of decentralization platforms, including blockchain-based and non-blockchain-based platforms.
3. Describe the characteristics of decentralization platforms, including decentralized networks, open-source, autonomy, and security.
4. Explain the use cases for decentralization platforms, including DeFi, social media, and gaming.
5. Analyze the benefits and limitations of decentralization platforms.
6. Evaluate the role of decentralization platforms in enabling decentralization in blockchain technology.
7. Design a decentralized application (dApp) using a decentralization platform.
