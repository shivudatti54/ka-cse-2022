# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Understand the fundamental concept, characteristics, and components of Decentralized Autonomous Corporations (DACs)

2. Explain the key differences between Decentralized Autonomous Corporations (DACs), Decentralized Autonomous Organizations (DAOs), and traditional corporate structures

3. Analyze the architectural framework of a DAC including its governance mechanisms, tokenomics, and decision-making processes

4. Apply smart contract principles to design autonomous corporate governance systems for DACs

5. Evaluate the advantages, limitations, and real-world challenges of implementing DACs in business environments

6. Design basic DAC structures with appropriate incentive mechanisms for specific business use cases

7. Compare existing DAC platforms, frameworks, and tools available in the blockchain ecosystem
