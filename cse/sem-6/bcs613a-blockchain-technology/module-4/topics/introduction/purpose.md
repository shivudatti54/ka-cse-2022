# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define Ethereum and explain how it extends blockchain technology beyond simple value transfers.
2. Describe the key differences between Ethereum and Bitcoin at an architectural level.
3. Explain the role of the Ethereum Virtual Machine (EVM) in enabling smart contract execution.
4. Identify the two types of Ethereum accounts (EOA and Contract) and their characteristics.
5. Describe the concept of gas and its importance in the Ethereum ecosystem.
6. Outline the history and evolution of Ethereum from its whitepaper to the current proof-of-stake system.
