# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define gas and its significance in the Ethereum blockchain.
2. Explain how gas is used to prevent denial-of-service (DoS) attacks.
3. Describe how gas works, including gas price and gas limit.
4. Understand how gas is used in smart contracts.
5. Calculate gas costs for transactions and contract executions.
6. Identify the units of measurement for gas, including Gwei.
7. Apply gas concepts to real-world scenarios in the Ethereum ecosystem.
