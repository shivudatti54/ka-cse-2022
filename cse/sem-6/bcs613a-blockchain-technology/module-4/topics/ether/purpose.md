# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define Ether and its role in the Ethereum ecosystem.
2. Explain the characteristics of Ether, including its decentralized nature, digital form, limited supply, and divisibility.
3. Identify the uses of Ether, including transaction fees, computational services, and storage.
4. Compare and contrast Ether and Ethereum Classic.
5. Describe the relationship between gas and Ether.
6. Explain how smart contracts use Ether to execute transactions and access services on the Ethereum network.
7. Understand the importance of Ether in the Ethereum ecosystem.
