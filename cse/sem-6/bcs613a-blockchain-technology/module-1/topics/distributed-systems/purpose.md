# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define distributed systems and explain their key characteristics in the context of blockchain technology.
2. Explain why blockchain is fundamentally a distributed system and how it achieves decentralization.
3. Describe the challenges of distributed systems (consistency, fault tolerance, network partitions) and how blockchain addresses them.
4. Differentiate between centralized, decentralized, and distributed architectures with examples.
5. Explain the role of peer-to-peer networking in blockchain and how nodes communicate.
6. Analyze how blockchain overcomes the trust problem in distributed systems without a central authority.
