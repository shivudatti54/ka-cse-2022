# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Explain the three components of the CAP theorem and the trade-offs between them
2. Describe the Byzantine Generals problem and its significance for distributed systems
3. Differentiate between Byzantine and non-Byzantine failures in distributed systems
4. Analyze how blockchain systems address the challenges posed by CAP theorem and Byzantine faults
5. Compare and contrast different consensus mechanisms (PoW, PoS, PBFT) in terms of their fault tolerance
6. Apply the concepts of CAP theorem to classify real-world distributed systems and blockchains
