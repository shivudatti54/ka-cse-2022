# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Identify and describe at least five major application domains of blockchain technology beyond cryptocurrency.
2. Explain how blockchain properties like immutability, transparency, and decentralization enable specific use cases in supply chain, healthcare, and finance.
3. Analyze the suitability of blockchain for a given real-world problem by evaluating the need for trust, transparency, and decentralization.
4. Differentiate between blockchain applications that use public versus private/consortium blockchains.
5. Describe how smart contracts enable automated, trustless execution in domains such as insurance, real estate, and voting.
6. Evaluate the limitations and challenges of adopting blockchain in enterprise and government settings.
