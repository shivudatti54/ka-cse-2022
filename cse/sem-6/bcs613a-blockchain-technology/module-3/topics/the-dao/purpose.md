# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define what a DAO is and its structure.
2. Explain the events that led to the DAO hack.
3. Identify the vulnerability that caused the DAO hack.
4. Discuss the consequences of the DAO hack.
5. Highlight the lessons learned from the DAO hack.
6. Explain the importance of secure smart contract development.
7. Analyze the risks associated with decentralized autonomous organizations.
