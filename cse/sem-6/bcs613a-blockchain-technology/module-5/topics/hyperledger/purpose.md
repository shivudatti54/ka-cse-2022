# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Explain the concept of Hyperledger and its significance in enterprise blockchain solutions.

2. Describe the key features of Hyperledger including modular architecture, interoperability, scalability, and security.

3. Identify and differentiate between the various Hyperledger projects such as Fabric, Sawtooth, and Corda.

4. Analyze the architectural components of Hyperledger and their roles in building blockchain applications.

5. Evaluate real-world use cases of Hyperledger across industries like finance, supply chain, and healthcare.

6. Understand the importance of permissioned blockchains and how Hyperledger differs from public blockchains like Bitcoin and Ethereum.
