# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Explain the architecture of Corda, including nodes, ledger, transactions, and smart contracts.
2. Describe the features of Corda, including interoperability, scalability, security, and private transactions.
3. Identify the applications of Corda in the financial services industry and other regulated markets.
4. Compare and contrast Corda with other blockchain platforms.
5. Explain the role of smart contracts in Corda and how they are used to validate transactions.
6. Discuss the advantages and disadvantages of using Corda for building blockchain applications.
7. Design a simple Corda application using Kotlin programming language.
