# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1.  Explain the concept of model-free methods in reinforcement learning.
2.  Describe the Q-learning and SARSA algorithms.
3.  List the advantages and disadvantages of model-free methods.
4.  Identify the applications of model-free methods.
5.  Implement a simple Q-learning or SARSA algorithm.
6.  Explain the exploration-exploitation trade-off in model-free methods.
7.  Compare model-based and model-free methods.
