# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. **Identify** the key domains where reinforcement learning is currently applied, including robotics, game playing, autonomous vehicles, recommendation systems, and resource management.

2. **Explain** how reinforcement learning enables robots to acquire complex skills through experience rather than explicit programming, with examples of manipulation and locomotion tasks.

3. **Analyze** the role of RL in game-playing applications, understanding how self-play and policy optimization lead to superhuman performance in strategic games.

4. **Evaluate** how autonomous vehicles utilize RL for decision-making, including motion planning, lane changing, and traffic navigation in dynamic environments.

5. **Compare** traditional recommendation approaches with RL-based systems, understanding how sequential decision-making and long-term optimization improve user engagement.

6. **Assess** the broader scope of RL applications in resource management, including energy systems, cloud computing, and healthcare optimization.
