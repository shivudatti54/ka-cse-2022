# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Explain the biological inspiration and basic structure of an artificial neuron
2. Describe the mathematical formulation and working principle of a perceptron
3. Implement the perceptron learning algorithm including weight updates
4. Identify linearly separable problems and understand the limitations of perceptrons
5. Compare perceptrons with other classification models like logistic regression
6. Understand the perceptron convergence theorem and its implications
