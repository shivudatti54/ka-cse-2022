# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Explain the challenges of ANN, including data quality issues, overfitting, underfitting, computational complexity, and interpretability.
2. Identify the causes and effects of noisy data, missing values, and imbalanced data.
3. Describe the techniques to address overfitting and underfitting, such as regularization, early stopping, and dropout.
4. Analyze the factors that affect the computational complexity of ANNs, such as model size, data size, and algorithm complexity.
5. Discuss the importance of interpretability in ANNs and the challenges associated with it.
6. Apply the knowledge of ANN challenges to real-world problems and case studies.
7. Evaluate the performance of ANNs in various applications and identify areas for improvement.
