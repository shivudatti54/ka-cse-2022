# Popular Applications of Artificial Neural Networks

## Overview

Artificial Neural Networks have revolutionized numerous fields through their ability to learn complex patterns from data. From computer vision to natural language processing, ANNs power modern AI applications, achieving human-level or superhuman performance in many domains.

## Key Points

- **Computer Vision**: Image classification (AlexNet, ResNet), Object detection (YOLO, R-CNN), Face recognition, Medical image analysis, Autonomous driving
- **Natural Language Processing**: Machine translation (Google Translate), Sentiment analysis, Text generation (GPT), Speech recognition (Siri, Alexa), Question answering
- **Healthcare**: Disease diagnosis from medical images, Drug discovery, Personalized treatment recommendations, Patient risk prediction, Radiology assistance
- **Finance**: Fraud detection, Algorithmic trading, Credit scoring, Risk assessment, Market prediction
- **Recommendation Systems**: Netflix movie recommendations, YouTube video suggestions, E-commerce product recommendations, Music recommendations (Spotify)
- **Gaming**: AlphaGo (defeated world champion), Game playing AI, NPC behavior, Procedural content generation

## Important Concepts

- CNN dominance in vision: ImageNet competition breakthrough (AlexNet 2012) started deep learning revolution
- Transformer architecture: replaced RNNs in NLP (BERT, GPT); attention mechanism enables parallel processing
- Transfer learning: pre-trained networks (ResNet, BERT) fine-tuned for specific tasks; reduces training data needs
- Real-time applications: object detection in autonomous vehicles, voice assistants, real-time translation

## Notes

- Know CNN applications: image classification, object detection, face recognition
- NLP applications: translation, sentiment analysis, text generation (GPT models)
- Healthcare: medical image analysis, disease diagnosis, drug discovery
- Be able to match ANN type to application: CNN→vision, RNN/Transformer→NLP, DNN→finance/recommendations
