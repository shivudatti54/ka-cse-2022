# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Identify major application domains of artificial neural networks across industries
2. Explain how ANNs are applied in computer vision tasks including image recognition and object detection
3. Describe NLP applications of neural networks such as machine translation and text generation
4. Discuss ANN applications in healthcare, finance, autonomous systems, and gaming
5. Match specific neural network architectures to their most suitable application domains
6. Analyze the impact and future potential of ANN-based systems in real-world applications
