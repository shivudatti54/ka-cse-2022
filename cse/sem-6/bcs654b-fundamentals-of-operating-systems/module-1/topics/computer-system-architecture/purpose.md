# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Describe the architecture of single-processor systems and their limitations
2. Explain multiprocessor systems, including symmetric and asymmetric multiprocessing
3. Compare SMP and AMP architectures with their advantages and disadvantages
4. Understand clustered systems and their role in high availability and high-performance computing
5. Differentiate between tightly coupled and loosely coupled multiprocessor systems
6. Explain how the operating system manages multiple processors and clustered configurations
