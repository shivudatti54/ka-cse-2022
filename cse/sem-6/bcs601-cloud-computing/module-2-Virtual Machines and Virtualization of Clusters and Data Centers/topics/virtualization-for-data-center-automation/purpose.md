This study material provides comprehensive coverage of virtualization as the enabling technology for data center automation, targeting B.Tech/MSc/MCA students at the Standard level. The content establishes theoretical foundations including formal proofs of VM isolation, performance overhead analysis, and resource scheduling algorithms. It covers hypervisor architecture, containerization, VM lifecycle management, and the integration of these technologies with automation frameworks (Kubernetes, OpenStack, Ansible, Terraform). The material includes practical implementation examples (IaC definitions, autoscaling configurations) and quantitative analysis of automation benefits, preparing students to design and implement automated data center solutions.
