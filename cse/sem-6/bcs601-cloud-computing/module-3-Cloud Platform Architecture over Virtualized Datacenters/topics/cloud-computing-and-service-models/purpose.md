This module provides comprehensive coverage of cloud computing fundamentals and service models for B.Tech/MSc/MCA students at Standard level. The content establishes rigorous theoretical foundations including NIST definitions, essential characteristics, multi-tenancy architecture, and service level agreements. Students will develop analytical capabilities to compare IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS models, evaluate deployment strategies (public, private, hybrid, community), and assess cloud economics for enterprise decision-making.
