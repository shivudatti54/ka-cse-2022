# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Describe the sequential stages of the digital image formation pipeline, from scene radiance to a stored digital image.
2. Explain the purpose and function of key components: the lens, image sensor (CCD/CMOS), Bayer filter, and Analog-to-Digital Converter (ADC).
3. Compare and contrast the characteristics of CCD and CMOS sensor technologies.
4. Articulate the purpose of the Bayer filter mosaic and the subsequent need for the demosaicing process.
5. Define and differentiate between key concepts such as resolution, bit depth, dynamic range, sampling, and quantization.
6. Analyze how camera sensor properties (e.g., noise, resolution, bit depth) can impact downstream computer vision tasks.
