# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic on Introduction to Computer Vision, you should be able to:

1. Define computer vision and explain what distinguishes it from related fields like image processing
2. Understand the fundamental challenges that make computer vision difficult
3. Trace the evolution of computer vision from the 1960s to the deep learning era
4. Identify and describe major milestones in computer vision history
5. List and explain diverse real-world applications of computer vision across different industries
6. Describe the typical computer vision pipeline from image acquisition to decision making
7. Recognize common computer vision tasks (detection, recognition, segmentation)
8. Explain current trends and future directions in computer vision research
