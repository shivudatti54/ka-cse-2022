# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1.  Define the opening and closing operations mathematically in terms of erosion and dilation.
2.  Predict the visual outcome of applying opening or closing to a binary image given a specific structuring element.
3.  Choose the appropriate operation (opening or closing) to solve a specific image processing problem, such as noise removal or gap filling.
4.  Explain the duality relationship that exists between opening and closing.
5.  Describe the step-by-step process (erosion then dilation vs. dilation then erosion) for each operation.
6.  Differentiate between the effects of opening and closing on foreground objects and background regions.
