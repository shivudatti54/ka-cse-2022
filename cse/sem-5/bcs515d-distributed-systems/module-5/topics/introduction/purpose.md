# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define a distributed system and explain its fundamental characteristics

2. Describe the key goals of distributed systems including transparency, openness, and scalability

3. Compare and contrast different distributed system architectures (client-server, peer-to-peer, three-tier)

4. Explain the role of middleware in distributed systems

5. Identify and analyze the challenges inherent in distributed systems including network latency, heterogeneity, and security

6. Understand the implications of the CAP theorem for distributed system design

7. Apply knowledge of distributed systems concepts to analyze real-world examples

8. Connect the foundational concepts of distributed systems to subsequent topics on distributed transactions and recovery mechanisms
