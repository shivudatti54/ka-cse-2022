To provide B.Tech/MSc/MCA students with comprehensive understanding of Requirements Engineering concepts, processes, and techniques. This content enables students to analyze stakeholder needs, classify requirements into functional and non-functional categories, apply various elicitation techniques, create proper Software Requirements Specifications (SRS), validate requirements for correctness and testability, and manage requirement changes throughout the project lifecycle. The rigorous MCQ at the end assesses application-level understanding through scenario-based problem solving.
