This content serves as a comprehensive module on UML models that supplement use case diagrams within the Software Engineering Project Management course. It addresses the gap between high-level use case specifications and implementation-ready designs by introducing formal UML behavioral and structural modeling constructs. The content targets B.Tech, MSc, and MCA students who require both theoretical foundations and practical modeling skills. It supports learning outcomes related to requirements elaboration, behavioral modeling, and model-driven software development approaches aligned with IEEE and OMG standards.
