This content provides comprehensive theoretical and practical treatment of requirements negotiation for B.Tech/MSc/MCA students in software engineering. It establishes the formal foundations of negotiation theory while developing practical skills in conflict resolution, prioritization, and stakeholder management. Students will acquire competencies necessary to navigate complex stakeholder environments, make defensible prioritization decisions under resource constraints, and negotiate agreements that balance competing interests while maintaining technical feasibility. The mathematical treatment of prioritization methods prepares students for quantitative decision-making in professional practice.
