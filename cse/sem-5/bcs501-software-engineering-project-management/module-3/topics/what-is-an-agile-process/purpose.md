This module provides comprehensive theoretical grounding in Agile Process methodology for software engineering students at the B.Tech/MSc/MCA level. It establishes the historical context of Agile emergence, analyzes the philosophical foundations of the Agile Manifesto, examines prominent frameworks (Scrum, XP, Kanban), and provides rigorous comparative analysis with traditional waterfall methodology. The content integrates concepts from complexity theory, empirical process control, and socio-technical systems to establish academic credibility while maintaining practical relevance for industry application.
