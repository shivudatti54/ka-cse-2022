This study material provides B.Tech/ MSc/MCA students with a comprehensive understanding of Agile methodology, emphasising theoretical foundations and practical application. It establishes rigorous conceptual frameworks including the cost of change curve, contrasts Agile with plan-driven approaches, and develops analytical skills through scenario-based assessment. The content prepares students for professional practice by covering essential terminology, sprint mechanics with quantitative reasoning, and framework implementation. Assessment components target application and analysis levels, moving beyond trivial recall to develop problem-solving capabilities essential for software engineering roles.
