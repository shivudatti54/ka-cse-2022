This study material provides comprehensive coverage of Extreme Programming (XP) for B.Tech/MSc/MCA students at Standard level. The content delivers thorough theoretical foundations, detailed analysis of all twelve XP practices with implementation rationales, comparative framework analysis (XP vs. Scrum vs. Kanban), and industry applications. The material is structured to support formal academic instruction requiring analytical understanding rather than mere recall, preparing students for application-level assessment in software engineering project management courses.
