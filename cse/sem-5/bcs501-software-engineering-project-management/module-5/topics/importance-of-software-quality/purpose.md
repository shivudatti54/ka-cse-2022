# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Explain the concept of software quality and its importance in software engineering
2. Identify and describe the eight quality characteristics defined in ISO 25010 standard
3. Analyze the cost of quality and explain the economic benefits of investing in prevention
4. Distinguish between Software Quality Assurance and Software Testing with appropriate examples
5. Apply quality metrics such as defect density to evaluate software quality
6. Compare different quality models including McCall and CMMI frameworks
7. Discuss modern quality practices in agile and DevOps methodologies
8. Justify the importance of software quality from business and stakeholder perspectives
