# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Explain the concept of software quality and its importance in software project management
2. Differentiate between Quality Assurance (QA) and Quality Control (QC) with appropriate examples
3. Describe the cost of quality and the exponential cost curve for defect correction
4. Analyze the relationship between software quality and the triple constraint (scope, time, cost)
5. Apply quality planning processes to define quality goals, attributes, and metrics for software projects
6. Evaluate quality risks and propose appropriate mitigation strategies
7. Interpret quality standards including ISO 25010 and CMMI maturity levels
8. Make informed decisions about quality trade-offs in project planning scenarios
