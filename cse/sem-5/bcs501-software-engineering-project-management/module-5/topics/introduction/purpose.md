# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define software quality and explain its dual perspective of conformance to specifications and fitness for use

2. Distinguish between internal quality and external quality attributes in software systems

3. Differentiate between Quality Assurance (QA) and Quality Control (QC) activities within project management

4. Explain the historical evolution of quality management from manufacturing to software engineering

5. Analyze the cost of quality model and its implications for project planning and budget allocation

6. Evaluate the importance of proactive quality management versus reactive defect detection

7. Apply quality concepts to real-world software project scenarios

8. Recognize the relationship between software quality and overall project success metrics
