# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define software quality and explain its importance in software engineering
2. Describe McCall's Quality Model and identify its eleven quality factors across three categories
3. Explain the ISO/IEC 25010 standard and its eight quality characteristics
4. Distinguish between software quality assurance (SQA) and quality control (QC)
5. Identify and describe various software quality attributes from different stakeholder perspectives
6. Apply quality models to analyze and define quality requirements for software projects
7. Calculate and interpret defect density as a quantitative quality metric
8. Understand how quality is context-dependent and varies based on application domain
