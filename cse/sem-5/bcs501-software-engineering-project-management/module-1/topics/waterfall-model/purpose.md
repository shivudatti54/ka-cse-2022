This comprehensive study material on the Waterfall Model is designed for B.Tech, MSc, and MCA students within the Software Engineering curriculum. The content addresses the Software Engineering Project Management module, specifically the Software Process Models topic. It provides thorough theoretical coverage with formal definitions, theoretical assumptions, and detailed phase descriptions as required for Standard-level academic preparation. The material supports examination preparation by including complete lifecycle description, comparative analysis, and practical applicability scenarios that enable students to make informed model selection decisions in professional contexts.
