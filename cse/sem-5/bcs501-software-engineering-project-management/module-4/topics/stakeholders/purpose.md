# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define the term "stakeholder" and explain its significance in software engineering projects.

2. Classify stakeholders into internal and external categories with appropriate examples for each type.

3. Apply the Power/Interest Grid to categorize and prioritize stakeholders based on their influence and interest levels.

4. Conduct a systematic stakeholder analysis by following the five-step identification and analysis process.

5. Develop appropriate engagement strategies for different stakeholder categories based on their classification.

6. Create and maintain a stakeholder register with relevant identification and assessment information.

7. Evaluate stakeholder engagement levels and recommend strategies to move stakeholders toward active support.

8. Analyze given project scenarios and identify all relevant stakeholders with their roles and potential impacts.
