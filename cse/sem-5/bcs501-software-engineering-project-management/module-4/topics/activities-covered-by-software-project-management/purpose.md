This module aims to develop comprehensive understanding of all activities constituting software project management within the software engineering domain. Students will learn to apply formal estimation techniques (Function Point Analysis, COCOMO), network analysis methods (CPM, PERT), risk management processes, and quality assurance frameworks. The purpose is to equip B.Tech/MSc/MCA students with the analytical and practical skills required to plan, execute, monitor, and close software projects successfully, distinguishing software-specific SPM from generic project management through emphasis on intangible products, evolving requirements, and technical complexities.
