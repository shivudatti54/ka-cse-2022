# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Explain the concept of clipping in computer graphics and its necessity in the rendering pipeline.

2. Describe the different types of clipping: point clipping, line clipping, polygon clipping, and text clipping.

3. Apply the Cohen-Sutherland algorithm to clip line segments against a rectangular window.

4. Implement the Liang-Barsky parametric line clipping algorithm and compare its efficiency with Cohen-Sutherland.

5. Use the Sutherland-Hodgman algorithm to clip polygons against convex clipping regions.

6. Analyze the computational complexity and practical applications of different clipping algorithms.

7. Understand the role of clipping in the overall graphics pipeline and viewport transformations.
