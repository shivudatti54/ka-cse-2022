# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define e-waste and explain its classification with relevant examples from the electronics industry.

2. Identify and describe the hazardous substances present in electronic waste and their environmental and health impacts.

3. Explain the hierarchy of e-waste management principles and their application in sustainable electronic waste handling.

4. Analyse the regulatory framework governing e-waste management in India, including the E-Waste (Management) Rules, 2022.

5. Describe the various methods and processes involved in e-waste collection, transportation, and recycling.

6. Evaluate the roles and responsibilities of different stakeholders, including producers, consumers, and recyclers, in effective e-waste management.

7. Apply the principles of e-waste management to develop sustainable solutions for handling electronic equipment at institutional and organisational levels.

8. Assess the environmental and economic benefits of proper e-waste management, including material recovery and resource conservation.
