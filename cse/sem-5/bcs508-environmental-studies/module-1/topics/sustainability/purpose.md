# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define sustainability and explain its importance in modern business and engineering contexts.

2. Identify and describe the three pillars of sustainability: Economic, Environmental, and Social.

3. Explain the concept of sustainable development and its relevance to engineering professionals.

4. Understand the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and their implications for technology companies.

5. Apply sustainability principles to green computing practices, including energy-efficient software and hardware solutions.

6. Analyze the triple bottom line framework and its application in organizational decision-making.

7. Evaluate the environmental impact of technology operations and recommend sustainable alternatives.

8. Integrate sustainability considerations into technology design, development, and deployment decisions.
