# Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs): Targets and Possible Actions - Summary

## Key Definitions and Concepts

- **Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)**: A set of 17 universal goals adopted by UN member states in 2015 as part of the 2030 Agenda for sustainable development
- **MDGs vs SDGs**: Millennium Development Goals (MDGs) focused primarily on developing countries; SDGs are universal and apply to all nations
- **SDG Integration**: All 17 goals are interconnected and indivisible - actions in one area affect outcomes in others
- **169 Targets**: Each SDG has specific measurable targets totaling 169 across all goals

## Important Formulas and Concepts

There are no specific formulas for this topic, but key concepts include:

- Universal applicability of SDGs to all countries regardless of economic status
- 2030 deadline for achieving all goals
- Three dimensions of sustainable development: Economic, Social, and Environmental

## Key Points

- SDGs were adopted in 2015 with a target completion year of 2030
- All 17 goals must be achieved simultaneously as they are interconnected
- Goal 9 (Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure) is most relevant to engineering students
- Engineers can contribute through sustainable design, renewable energy, green technologies
- India has aligned its national policies with SDGs through various initiatives
- SDG 17 focuses on partnerships for implementation and monitoring
- Climate action (Goal 13) requires urgent and immediate measures

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Confusing SDGs with MDGs - they are different frameworks
- Memorizing goals without understanding their interconnections
- Ignoring the role of technology in achieving SDGs
- Overlooking the role of individual actions in contributing to global goals

## Revision Tips

1. Create a table of all 17 SDGs with their numbers and short descriptions
2. For each goal, note at least one engineering application or possible action
3. Understand how different goals relate to each other
4. Practice answering questions that require connecting multiple SDGs
5. Review India's initiatives and schemes related to SDGs
