# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Explain the concept of Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and their significance in global development

2. Identify and describe all 17 SDGs with their specific objectives

3. Understand the 169 targets associated with the SDGs and their measurable outcomes

4. Analyze the interconnections between different SDGs and how they are indivisible

5. Evaluate possible actions that engineers and technologists can take to contribute towards achieving the SDGs

6. Apply SDG concepts to engineering projects and professional practices

7. Relate the SDGs to India's national development priorities and initiatives

8. Critically assess the role of technology and innovation in achieving sustainable development goals
