# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define wetlands and explain their essential characteristics according to the Ramsar Convention
2. Classify wetlands into different types (inland, coastal, and human-made) with relevant examples
3. Describe the ecological functions of wetlands, including water purification, flood control, and biodiversity support
4. Analyze the economic values of wetlands in terms of fishery resources, forest products, and tourism
5. Identify the major threats to wetlands and explain conservation measures in India
6. Apply calculations for pollutant removal efficiency and flood peak attenuation in wetland systems
7. Discuss the role of Indian legislation and international conventions in wetland conservation
8. Evaluate the importance of wetlands in climate change mitigation through carbon sequestration
