# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Explain the nature and characteristics of solar radiation, including the solar constant and the solar spectrum.

2. Describe the working principles of solar thermal systems, including flat-plate collectors and concentrating collectors.

3. Analyze the photovoltaic effect and explain the operation of solar cells, including I-V characteristics and key performance parameters.

4. Differentiate between grid-connected and off-grid solar PV systems and their components.

5. Evaluate energy storage options for solar energy systems, including thermal storage and battery storage.

6. Apply solar energy calculation methods for sizing collectors, PV modules, and battery systems.

7. Understand the principles of solar passive architecture and its role in building energy efficiency.

8. Assess the environmental and economic benefits of solar energy utilization in the context of sustainable development.
