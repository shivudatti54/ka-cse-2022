This module examines water resources availability, distribution, and quality from an engineering perspective. Key topics include: (1) Hydrological cycle components with quantitative relationships; (2) Surface water resources including major river basins and reservoirs; (3) Groundwater systems with aquifer classification and water balance; (4) Spatial-temporal water availability analysis with per capita metrics; (5) Water quality parameters—physical, chemical, and biological—with BIS standards and calculation methods; (6) Water treatment processes from coagulation to advanced technologies; (7) Sustainable management strategies including IWRM and climate adaptation. The module integrates theoretical principles with practical engineering applications relevant to India's water resource challenges.
