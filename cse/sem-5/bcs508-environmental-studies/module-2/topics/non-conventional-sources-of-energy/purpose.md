# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Explain the concept of non-conventional energy sources and their importance in the context of sustainable development.

2. Describe the working principles of solar energy systems, including photovoltaic cells and solar thermal power generation.

3. Analyze wind energy conversion systems and apply the wind power equation to solve numerical problems.

4. Explain various biomass energy conversion technologies and their applications in power generation.

5. Understand geothermal energy resources and their utilization for heating and electricity generation.

6. Describe ocean energy resources including tidal, wave, and ocean thermal energy conversion (OTEC) systems.

7. Explain hydrogen production methods and fuel cell technology for clean energy applications.

8. Compare the advantages and limitations of different non-conventional energy sources.

9. Apply the concepts to evaluate the suitability of renewable energy systems for specific applications.

10. Connect the knowledge to real-world applications including green computing and sustainable IT infrastructure.
