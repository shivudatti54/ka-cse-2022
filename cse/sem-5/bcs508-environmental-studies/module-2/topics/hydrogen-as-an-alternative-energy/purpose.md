# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Explain the concept of hydrogen as an energy carrier and its role in the emerging hydrogen economy.

2. Describe the physical and chemical properties of hydrogen that make it suitable as a fuel.

3. Compare different hydrogen production methods (steam methane reforming, electrolysis, biomass gasification) with respect to environmental impact and efficiency.

4. Analyze the various hydrogen storage techniques including compressed gas, liquid hydrogen, and solid-state storage.

5. Explain the working principle of hydrogen fuel cells and differentiate between major fuel cell types.

6. Evaluate the advantages and challenges of hydrogen energy in the context of sustainable development.

7. Identify the applications of hydrogen energy in transportation, power generation, and industrial sectors.

8. Discuss the future prospects of hydrogen in India's energy mix and global sustainability efforts.
