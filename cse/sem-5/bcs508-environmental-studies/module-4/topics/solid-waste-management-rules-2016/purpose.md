# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Explain the significance and need for Solid Waste Management Rules, 2016 in India

2. Identify the various categories of solid waste as defined under the SWM Rules, 2016

3. Describe the responsibilities of waste generators, urban local bodies, and other stakeholders under the rules

4. Analyze the waste processing and treatment requirements mandated by the rules

5. Evaluate the criteria for selection and operation of sanitary landfill sites

6. Explain the provisions for user fees and revenue generation in solid waste management

7. Discuss the special provisions for rural areas under the 2016 rules

8. Apply the knowledge to design a basic solid waste management system for a municipality
