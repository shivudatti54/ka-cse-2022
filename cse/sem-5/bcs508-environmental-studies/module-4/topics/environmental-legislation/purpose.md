# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Explain the constitutional provisions and fundamental principles underlying environmental legislation in India.

2. Identify and describe the major Indian environmental laws including the Environment Protection Act, Water Act, and Air Act.

3. Analyze the provisions and requirements of the E-Waste Management Rules, 2022 and their implications for the IT and electronics industry.

4. Evaluate the role and functions of regulatory bodies such as CPCB, SPCB, and NGT in environmental governance.

5. Discuss India's commitments under international environmental agreements including the Paris Agreement and Montreal Protocol.

6. Apply environmental legislation concepts to real-world scenarios involving industrial compliance and e-waste management.

7. Assess the importance of Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) process for development projects.

8. Recognize the role of engineering professionals in ensuring environmental compliance and promoting sustainable practices.
