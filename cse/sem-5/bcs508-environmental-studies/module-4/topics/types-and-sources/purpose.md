This chapter provides comprehensive knowledge of biomedical waste classification and sources as required for environmental engineering and healthcare management curricula. Students will understand the regulatory framework governing biomedical waste in India, master the ten-category classification system, and identify various healthcare establishments generating biomedical waste. The content establishes foundational concepts necessary for studying treatment technologies and developing waste management plans. This knowledge is essential for B.Tech students specializing in environmental engineering and for MCA/MSc programs addressing healthcare informatics and hospital administration.
