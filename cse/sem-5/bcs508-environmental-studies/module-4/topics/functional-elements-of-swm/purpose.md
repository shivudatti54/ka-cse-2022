# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Identify and explain the six functional elements of solid waste management in their sequential order

2. Describe the factors affecting waste generation rates and characteristics in urban areas

3. Differentiate between various collection systems including curbside, door-to-door, on-demand, and fixed-time collection methods

4. Explain the purpose and types of transfer stations in solid waste management

5. Analyze different processing and recovery methods such as composting, recycling, and energy recovery

6. Evaluate the components and environmental considerations of sanitary and engineered landfills

7. Apply engineering principles to design basic collection systems and size processing facilities

8. Discuss the role of functional elements in achieving sustainable solid waste management objectives
