# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define biomedical waste and explain its significance in healthcare and environmental contexts.
2. Identify and categorize the four main categories of biomedical waste according to the BMW Rules, 2016.
3. Enumerate the primary and secondary sources of biomedical waste in healthcare establishments.
4. Explain the color-coded segregation system and its importance in waste management.
5. Calculate approximate waste generation rates for healthcare facilities of different sizes.
6. Describe the regulatory framework governing biomedical waste management in India.
7. Analyze the environmental and health impacts of improper biomedical waste disposal.
8. Apply the concepts of waste segregation to real-world healthcare scenarios.
