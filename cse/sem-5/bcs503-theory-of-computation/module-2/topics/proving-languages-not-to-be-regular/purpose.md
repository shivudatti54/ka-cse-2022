# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Explain why certain languages cannot be recognized by finite automata
2. State the Pumping Lemma for Regular Languages and explain its significance
3. Apply the Pumping Lemma to prove that given languages are not regular
4. Explain the Myhill-Nerode Theorem and its equivalence classes interpretation
5. Use Myhill-Nerode Theorem to prove non-regularity of languages
6. Apply closure properties to prove that languages are not regular
7. Distinguish between languages that are regular and non-regular in practical scenarios
8. Construct rigorous non-regularity proofs suitable for examination answers
