# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define backward chaining and explain its role in AI inference systems
2. Describe the goal-driven (top-down) reasoning approach used in backward chaining
3. Differentiate between forward chaining and backward chaining with respect to direction, strategy, and applications
4. Construct AND-OR graphs to represent the backward chaining process
5. Trace the backward chaining algorithm through a given knowledge base to prove or disprove a goal
6. Identify appropriate scenarios for applying backward chaining (diagnostic systems, query answering)
7. Explain the termination conditions in backward chaining (success, failure, unknown)
8. Apply backward chaining reasoning to solve simple diagnostic problems in expert systems
