# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define Artificial Intelligence and explain its significance in modern computing and technology.

2. Distinguish between the three types of AI: Narrow AI, General AI, and Super AI, with examples of each.

3. Compare and contrast the major approaches to AI: Symbolic AI, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning.

4. Explain the hierarchical relationship between AI, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning.

5. Describe the Turing Test and its role in measuring machine intelligence, along with its limitations.

6. Define and illustrate the concept of an intelligent agent and its role in AI system design.

7. Identify real-world applications of AI and categorize them based on the type of AI employed.

8. Appreciate the historical development of AI and understand the factors contributing to the current AI revolution.
