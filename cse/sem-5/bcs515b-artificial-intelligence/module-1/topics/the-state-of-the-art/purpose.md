# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define the term "state of the art" in the context of artificial intelligence and explain its significance.

2. Differentiate between narrow AI (weak AI) and artificial general intelligence (AGI), providing examples of each.

3. Identify the key technologies and approaches that define the current state of AI, including deep learning and neural networks.

4. Describe major achievements of modern AI systems in areas such as natural language processing, computer vision, and game playing.

5. Analyze the limitations of current AI systems, including their inability to reason, lack of common sense, and dependence on large datasets.

6. Evaluate the ethical challenges and practical limitations that constrain AI deployment in real-world applications.

7. Explain the concept of the "black box" problem in deep learning and its implications for AI trustworthiness.
