# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Explain the concept of forward chaining as a data-driven inference mechanism in rule-based systems

2. Describe the components of a rule-based system including working memory, fact base, and production rules

3. Apply the forward chaining algorithm step-by-step to derive conclusions from given facts and rules

4. Compare forward chaining with backward chaining and identify appropriate use cases for each

5. Analyze conflict resolution strategies and explain their role in forward chaining systems

6. Evaluate the advantages and limitations of forward chaining in expert system applications

7. Design simple expert system solutions using forward chaining for diagnostic and classification problems
