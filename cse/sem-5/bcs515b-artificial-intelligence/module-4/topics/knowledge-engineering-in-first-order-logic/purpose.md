# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Explain the systematic methodology of knowledge engineering in first-order logic and describe each stage of the process.

2. Design appropriate vocabularies for FOL knowledge bases, including identifying constants, functions, and predicates for a given domain.

3. Construct ontologies that define concepts, their hierarchies, and relationships within a specific domain.

4. Distinguish between axioms and facts in FOL knowledge representation and encode them correctly using quantifiers.

5. Apply knowledge engineering principles to build knowledge bases for practical domains such as electronics, genealogy, and university systems.

6. Perform logical inference to answer queries given a set of FOL axioms and facts.

7. Analyze and solve the fundamental challenges in knowledge representation including the frame problem, qualification problem, and ramification problem.

8. Evaluate the quality and completeness of a knowledge base and identify potential gaps or inconsistencies.
