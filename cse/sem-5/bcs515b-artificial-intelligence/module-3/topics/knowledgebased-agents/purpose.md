# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Explain the architecture of a knowledge-based agent and describe the roles of knowledge base, inference engine, and agent program.

2. Analyze the Wumpus World environment and identify all components including agent, Wumpus, pits, gold, and their interactions.

3. Interpret percept information (Stench, Breeze, Glitter, Bump, Scream) and relate them to environmental conditions.

4. Construct knowledge base sentences using propositional logic to represent Wumpus World rules and observations.

5. Apply logical inference rules including Modus Ponens and Universal Instantiation to derive new facts from existing knowledge.

6. Determine safe cells in the Wumpus World using logical reasoning and negation of percept information.

7. Differentiate between entailment (semantic) and inference (syntactic) in knowledge representation.

8. Design simple reasoning strategies for knowledge-based agents operating in partially observable environments.
