# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define unguided media and explain its role in computer network communications
2. Describe the electromagnetic spectrum and its relevance to wireless transmission
3. Compare and contrast different types of unguided media including radio waves, microwaves, infrared, and satellite communications
4. Explain the various propagation modes: ground wave, sky wave, and line-of-sight propagation
5. Analyze the advantages and disadvantages of wireless media over guided media
6. Identify appropriate applications for each type of unguided media based on frequency, range, and bandwidth requirements
7. Understand the concept of satellite orbits and their characteristics for communication purposes
8. Describe the security challenges and mitigation techniques associated with wireless communications
