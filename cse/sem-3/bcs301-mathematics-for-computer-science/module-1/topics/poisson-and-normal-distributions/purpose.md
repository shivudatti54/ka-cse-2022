# Why Study Poisson and Normal Distributions?

## Importance

The Poisson and Normal distributions are two of the most fundamental probability distributions in mathematics and computer science. The Poisson distribution models discrete event counts (network packets, server requests, defects), while the Normal distribution models continuous measurements and underpins much of statistical inference and machine learning.
