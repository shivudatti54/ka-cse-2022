# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Define Analysis of Covariance (ANCOVA) and explain its purpose in statistical analysis

2. Describe the general linear model for ANCOVA and identify all its components

3. List and explain the six key assumptions underlying ANCOVA

4. Perform one-way ANCOVA and interpret the results

5. Perform two-way ANCOVA and understand the interpretation of main effects and interactions

6. Calculate and interpret adjusted (least squares) means

7. Test for homogeneity of regression slopes and understand its implications

8. Apply ANCOVA to solve practical problems in computer science research
