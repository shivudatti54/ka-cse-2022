This study material covers Direct Memory Access (DMA) in computer systems, a critical topic in the I/O Organization and Memory Systems module of Digital Design and Computer Organization. The content progresses from basic I/O transfer concepts through DMA architecture, transfer modes, bus arbitration protocols, and quantitative performance analysis. It is designed for B.Tech/MSc/MCA students at the Standard level, requiring formal academic treatment with thorough theoretical explanations and numerical problem-solving capabilities. The assessment questions test application-level understanding through calculations involving transfer times, bus utilization, and CPU overhead comparisons.
