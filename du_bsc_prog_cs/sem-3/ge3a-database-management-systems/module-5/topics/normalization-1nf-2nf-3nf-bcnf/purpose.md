# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Understand the concept of normalization and its importance in eliminating database anomalies
2. Define and identify functional dependencies in a given relation
3. Apply the criteria for First Normal Form (1NF) to ensure atomic values
4. Identify partial dependencies and convert relations to Second Normal Form (2NF)
5. Detect transitive dependencies and normalize relations to Third Normal Form (3NF)
6. Comprehend the stricter requirements of Boyce-Codd Normal Form (BCNF)
7. Determine the highest normal form for a given relation with specified functional dependencies
8. Perform lossless-join decompositions to achieve higher normal forms while preserving data integrity
