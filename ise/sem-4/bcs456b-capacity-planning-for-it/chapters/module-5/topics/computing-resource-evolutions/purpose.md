### Learning Purpose: Module 5 - Computing Resource Evolutions

**1. Why is this topic important?**
Understanding the evolution of computing resources—from mainframes to cloud and edge computing—is crucial. It provides the historical context and forward-looking perspective needed to anticipate future trends, avoid technological obsolescence, and make strategic, cost-effective capacity planning decisions.

**2. What will students learn?**
Students will learn the key technological shifts in processing, storage, and networking. They will analyze the drivers behind these evolutions (e.g., Moore's Law, virtualization) and evaluate the impact of emerging paradigms like hyper-converged infrastructure (HCI), serverless computing, and containers on capacity planning strategies.

**3. How does it connect to other concepts?**
This topic directly builds on previous modules covering forecasting and performance metrics. It provides the "why" behind changing resource types and scales, informing how you select appropriate solutions and create agile plans that can adapt to rapid technological change.

**4. Real-world applications**
This knowledge enables professionals to architect scalable systems, plan efficient cloud migrations, justify infrastructure modernization budgets, and ensure IT capacity can leverage—rather than be disrupted by—the next wave of innovation.
