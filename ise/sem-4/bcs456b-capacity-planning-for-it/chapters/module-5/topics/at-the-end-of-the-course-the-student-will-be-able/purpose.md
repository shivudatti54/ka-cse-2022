### Learning Purpose: Capacity Planning for IT

**1. Why is this topic important?**
Capacity planning is the strategic cornerstone of a resilient, cost-effective, and high-performing IT infrastructure. Neglecting it leads to system failures during peak demand, wasted resources during lulls, and an inability to support business growth, directly impacting revenue and user satisfaction.

**2. What will students learn?**
Students will learn to analyze current IT resource utilization (CPU, memory, storage, network), forecast future demands based on business drivers and trends, and model different scaling strategies (vertical/horizontal). They will develop the ability to create data-driven capacity plans that ensure performance, availability, and financial efficiency.

**3. How does it connect to other concepts?**
This module integrates knowledge from previous modules on **performance monitoring** (using metrics), **cloud computing** (elastic scaling models), **IT financial management** (cost-benefit analysis of CapEx vs. OpEx), and **business alignment** (translating business objectives into technical requirements).

**4. Real-world applications**
This skill is directly applied to right-sizing cloud deployments, planning hardware refresh cycles, ensuring e-commerce platforms handle holiday traffic, supporting new application rollouts, and building a scalable foundation for data analytics and AI workloads.
