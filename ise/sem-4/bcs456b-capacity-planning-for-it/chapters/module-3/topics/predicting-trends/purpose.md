### Learning Purpose: Predicting Trends in Capacity Planning

**1. Why is this topic important?**
Predicting trends is the cornerstone of proactive IT capacity planning. Without accurate trend analysis, organizations risk either costly over-provisioning of resources or severe under-provisioning, leading to performance degradation and system outages. This topic equips future IT professionals with the foresight needed to make data-driven investment decisions and ensure business continuity.

**2. What will students learn?**
Students will learn to identify and analyze historical usage patterns to forecast future IT resource demands. This includes applying key statistical methods like linear regression and moving averages to time-series data. They will also learn to differentiate between seasonal fluctuations and long-term growth trends, a critical skill for creating realistic capacity models.

**3. How does it connect to other concepts?**
This topic directly builds upon the foundational concepts covered in earlier modules, such as **performance baselining** (Module 1) and **metrics collection** (Module 2). The predicted trends generated here become the essential input for the subsequent module on **creating a capacity plan** (Module 4), where forecasts are translated into actionable procurement and deployment strategies.

**4. Real-world applications**
The ability to predict trends is applied across the industry to plan for cloud scaling (e.g., auto-scaling groups in AWS/Azure), budget for hardware refreshes, and ensure e-commerce platforms can handle anticipated holiday traffic spikes. It transforms capacity planning from a reactive firefighting exercise into a strategic business function.
