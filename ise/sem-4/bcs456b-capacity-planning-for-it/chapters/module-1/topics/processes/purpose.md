# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1.  Differentiate between SOC processes and workflows and explain their importance.
2.  Describe the key processes in a SOC, including monitoring, triage, incident response, threat hunting, and reporting.
3.  Map common SOC activities to the phases of the NIST Incident Response Lifecycle.
4.  Explain the steps involved in core SOC workflows such as alert triage and phishing investigation.
5.  Define key SOC metrics (MTTD, MTTR) and explain how processes impact them.
6.  Identify how the SOC integrates with other organizational teams during incident response.
