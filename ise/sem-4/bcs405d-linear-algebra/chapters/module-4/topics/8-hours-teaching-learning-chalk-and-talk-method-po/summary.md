### Linear Algebra Revision Notes

#### Inner Product Spaces (8 hours)

##### Teaching-Learning Method/PowerPoint Presentation Process

- **Key Concepts:**
  - Definition of Inner Product Space
  - Properties of Inner Product ( positivity, definiteness, homogeneity, additivity)
  - Orthogonality and Orthonormality
  - Gram-Schmidt Process
- **Important Formulas:**
  - Inner Product: $\langle u, v \rangle = u^T v$
  - Inner Product Space: $V = \{v: \langle v, v \rangle \geq 0\}$
  - Orthogonal Basis: $\{u_i\}$ is orthonormal if $\langle u_i, u_j \rangle = 0$ and $\langle u_i, u_i \rangle = 1$
- **Key Theorems:**
  - Pythagorean Theorem for Inner Product Spaces: $\langle u + v, u + v \rangle = \langle u, u \rangle + \langle v, v \rangle$
  - Orthogonality Theorem: If $\langle u, v \rangle = 0$, then $u$ and $v$ are orthogonal
- **PowerPoint Presentation Process:**
  1.  Introduce key concepts and definitions
  2.  Use visual aids (graphs, diagrams) to illustrate properties and theorems
  3.  Provide example problems and solutions
  4.  Encourage class discussion and Q&A
