# Teaching-Learning Chalk and Talk Method / PowerPoint Presentation Process

=====================================

### Overview

The chalk and talk method and PowerPoint presentation process are two effective teaching methods used to convey information to students.

### Key Points:

- **Chalk and Talk Method:**
  - An interactive method where the teacher writes on the blackboard or whiteboard.
  - Allows for real-time questioning and clarification.
  - Suitable for small to medium-sized classes.
  - Enhances active learning and engagement.
- **PowerPoint Presentation Process:**
  - A visual aid method using PowerPoint slides.
  - Slides can include text, images, diagrams, and charts.
  - Allows for easy organization and time management of content.
  - Facilitates a clear and concise delivery of information.

### Important Formulas, Definitions, and Theorems:

- **Graph Theory Basics:**
  - Definition: A graph is a non-linear data structure consisting of nodes or vertices connected by edges.
  - Types of graphs: Undirected, Directed, Weighted, Unweighted.
- **Graph Terminology:**
  - Vertex: A point or node in a graph.
  - Edge: A line or connection between two vertices.
  - Neighbor: A vertex connected to another vertex by an edge.
  - Degree: The number of edges connected to a vertex.
  - Path: A sequence of vertices and edges between two vertices.
  - Cycle: A path that starts and ends at the same vertex, passing through at least one edge.

### Quick Revision Tips:

- Review graph theory formulas and theorems.
