**Why this topic matters:**
Learning definitions and the proofs of theorems is crucial in discrete mathematical structures as it provides a solid foundation for understanding and reasoning about mathematical concepts. **Real-world applications:**
The concepts learned in this topic have numerous applications in computer science, programming languages, data structures, algorithms, and artificial intelligence. For instance, understanding the definitions of data structures like graphs, trees, and arrays enables developers to design efficient algorithms and implement them in real-world applications.

**Connection to other concepts:**
The proofs of theorems in discrete mathematical structures build upon and connect to other fundamental concepts, such as mathematical induction, combinatorics, graph theory, and coding theory.
