#### Key Concepts

- **Definition:** Commission refers to a payment made to a salesperson or agent for selling a product or service.
- **Types of Commissions:**
  - **Flat Rate Commission:** A fixed percentage of the sale price.
  - **Percentage-in-Percentage Commission:** A percentage of the percentage earned by the salesperson.
  - **Tiered Commission:** Additional commission paid for meeting or exceeding sales targets.

#### Important Formulas

- **Commission Formula:** `Commission = Total Sales x Commission Rate`
- **Flat Rate Commission Formula:** `Flat Rate Commission = Total Sales x Flat Rate`

#### Definitions

- **Salesperson:** An individual responsible for selling products or services.
- **Sales Agent:** An individual or company responsible for selling products or services on behalf of the manufacturer or supplier.

#### Theorems

- **Commission Law:** The law of commissions states that the total amount earned by the salesperson is equal to the sum of the sales price, taxes, and commission.
- **Salesperson's Net Earnings:** The salesperson's net earnings are equal to the total sales minus the cost of goods sold, taxes, and commission.

- Understand the importance of salesperson's net earnings and commission law.
