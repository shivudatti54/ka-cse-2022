**Why this topic matters:**
This topic is crucial in database management systems as it enables designers to translate conceptual database designs into logical designs, ensuring data consistency, integrity, and efficiency. By mastering ER-to-Relational mapping, individuals can create robust and scalable database systems that meet the needs of various applications. This skill is essential for software developers, database administrators, and data analysts. Effective mapping also helps prevent errors and reduces the complexity of database design.

**Real-world applications:**
The knowledge of ER-to-Relational mapping has numerous real-world applications in various industries, such as:

- E-commerce websites: to design databases for online shopping platforms
- Healthcare systems: to manage patient records and medical information
- Financial institutions: to develop databases for transactions and account management
