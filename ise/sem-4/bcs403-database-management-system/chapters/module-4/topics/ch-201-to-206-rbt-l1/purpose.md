Learning chapters 20.1 to 20.6 on Database Management Systems (DBMS) is crucial as it equips RBTs (Record-Related Data Technicians) with the skills to design, implement, and maintain databases that support efficient data management and retrieval. This topic matters because it enables RBTs to work effectively in various industries, such as healthcare, finance, and education, where accurate and secure data management is vital.

Real-world applications of DBMS include data analysis, data warehousing, and business intelligence, which are essential in decision-making processes.

This topic connects to other concepts, such as data modeling, normalization, and querying, which are fundamental to database management.
