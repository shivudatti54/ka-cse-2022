# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define and distinguish between SOC metrics and Key Performance Indicators (KPIs).
2. Identify and explain the four main categories of SOC metrics: Operational, Efficiency, Effectiveness, and Business Value.
3. Calculate fundamental efficiency metrics such as Mean Time to Detect (MTTD), Mean Time to Respond (MTTR), and Mean Time to Acknowledge (MTTA).
4. Evaluate the meaning behind common metric values, such as a high False Positive Rate or Alert-to-Incident Ratio.
5. Align specific metrics and KPIs with strategic business and security objectives.
6. Identify common challenges and pitfalls in implementing a SOC metrics program.
