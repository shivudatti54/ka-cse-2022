### Learning Purpose: Module 3 - Design Production

**1. Why is this topic important?**
Design Production is the critical phase where concepts are translated into tangible, functional assets. It ensures design consistency, efficiency for developers, and a seamless transition from prototype to final product. Mastering this phase is essential for creating scalable and maintainable digital experiences.

**2. What will students learn?**
Students will learn to create and hand off developer-ready design deliverables. This includes building comprehensive design systems, generating specs (measurements, colors, typography), exporting assets in multiple formats, and using collaborative handoff tools like Figma to ensure precise implementation.

**3. How does it connect to other concepts?**
This module builds directly on the principles of Visual Design (Module 1) and Prototyping (Module 2). It acts as the crucial bridge between the design team's work and the development phase, ensuring the visual and interactive integrity of the prototype is preserved in the final coded product.

**4. Real-world applications**
These skills are applied daily by UI/UX designers to hand off designs for websites, mobile apps, and software. Proficiency in design production reduces costly errors and rework, accelerates development timelines, and is a fundamental requirement for working effectively in a modern tech team.
