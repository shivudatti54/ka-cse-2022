# Mental Models and Conceptual Design: Storyboards

### UI/UX

#### Design Thinking, Ideation, and Sketching

**Key Concepts:**

- **Mental Models:** Assumptions and frameworks that influence how we perceive and understand the world.
- **Conceptual Design:** The process of creating designs based on mental models and conceptual thinking.
- **Storyboarding:** A visual representation of a narrative or a sequence of events.

**Important Formulas and Definitions:**

- **SWOT Analysis:** A framework for analyzing an idea or project's strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats.
  - SWOT = Strengths ∩ Weaknesses ∩ Opportunities ∩ Threats
- **User Personas:** Representative models of the target audience, created to guide design decisions.
  - Persona = Demographics ∩ Goals ∩ Pain Points ∩ Motivations
- **Service Blueprint:** A visual representation of a service or experience, highlighting touchpoints and interactions.
  - Service Blueprint = Customer ∩ Touchpoint ∩ Process ∩ Benefit

**Theoretical Background:**

- **Theories of Mental Models:** Cognitive biases, heuristics, and mental frameworks that influence design decisions.
  - e.g., Availability Heuristic, Representativeness Heuristic
- **Conceptual Design Theories:** Design principles that guide the conceptual design process.
  - e.g., Design Thinking, Human-Centered Design
