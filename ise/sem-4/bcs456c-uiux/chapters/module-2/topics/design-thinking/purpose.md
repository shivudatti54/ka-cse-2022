# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Identify and explain each of the five stages of the Design Thinking process
2. Differentiate Design Thinking from traditional problem-solving approaches
3. Apply appropriate techniques for each stage of the Design Thinking process
4. Create a Point of View (POV) statement based on user research insights
5. Develop low-fidelity prototypes to test design concepts
6. Explain the iterative nature of Design Thinking and how stages connect
