Learning "Methods for requirement extraction" is crucial as it enables designers and researchers to effectively gather and document user needs, leading to more effective and user-centered design solutions. This topic matters because it provides a structured approach to understanding user requirements, which is essential for creating products that meet user needs and expectations. In real-world applications, methods for requirement extraction are used in various industries such as IT, product design, and healthcare to inform design decisions and ensure that products meet user needs. This knowledge also connects to other concepts in UI/UX, such as user research, user testing, and design thinking.
