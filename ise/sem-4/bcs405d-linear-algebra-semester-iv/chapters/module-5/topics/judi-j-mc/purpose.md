### Learning Purpose: Optimization Techniques in Linear Algebra

**1. Why is this topic important?**
This topic is fundamental because optimization provides a powerful framework for making the best possible decisions under given constraints. It is the mathematical engine behind countless applications in data science, engineering, economics, and logistics, transforming real-world problems into solvable linear models.

**2. What will students learn?**
Students will learn to formulate real-world problems (e.g., maximizing profit, minimizing cost) as standard Linear Programming (LP) models. They will master the Simplex Method to solve these LP problems algebraically and interpret the results, including optimal solutions and sensitivity analysis.

**3. How does it connect to other concepts?**
This module directly builds upon core linear algebra concepts. Students will apply their knowledge of systems of linear equations, matrices, vector spaces, and convexity to set up and solve optimization problems. It also provides a crucial foundation for more advanced topics like integer programming and non-linear optimization.

**4. Real-world applications**
The techniques learned are used widely across industries. Key applications include optimizing supply chain logistics and transportation networks, managing financial portfolios, allocating resources in manufacturing, and enhancing efficiency in energy grids.
