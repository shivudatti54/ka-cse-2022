# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1.  Explain the limitation of Cartesian coordinates for representing translation as a matrix operation.
2.  Define homogeneous coordinates and convert points between homogeneous and Cartesian representations.
3.  Construct the 3x3 homogeneous transformation matrices for 2D translation, rotation, and scaling.
4.  Construct the 4x4 homogeneous transformation matrices for 3D translation, scaling, and rotation around the principal axes.
5.  Derive a single composite transformation matrix from a sequence of individual transformations, understanding the importance of order.
6.  Apply a composite transformation matrix to a point to find its final transformed position.
