### Learning Purpose: N. Parikh (Optimization Technique)

**1. Why is this topic important?**
This topic is crucial as it introduces advanced optimization algorithms developed by N. Parikh, which are highly efficient for solving large-scale, complex problems that are challenging for traditional methods. Understanding these techniques is vital for tackling real-world engineering and data science problems where computational efficiency and accuracy are paramount.

**2. What will students learn?**
Students will learn the fundamental principles behind N. Parikh's optimization algorithms, such as the Proximal Algorithm and its variants. They will understand how to model problems, apply these iterative methods to find optimal solutions, and analyze their convergence properties and computational complexity.

**3. How does it connect to other concepts?**
This topic builds directly upon foundational knowledge of calculus, linear algebra, and classical optimization techniques from earlier modules. It connects deeply with concepts like convex optimization, duality theory, and machine learning algorithms, providing a powerful toolkit for solving non-smooth and composite optimization problems.

**4. Real-world applications**
These algorithms are widely applied in various fields, including image processing (e.g., image deblurring), machine learning (training sparse models like LASSO), signal processing, and large-scale data analysis, making them indispensable for modern technological advancements.
