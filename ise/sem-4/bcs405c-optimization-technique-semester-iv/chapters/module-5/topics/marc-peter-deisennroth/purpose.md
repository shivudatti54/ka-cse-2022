### Learning Purpose: Advanced Optimization with Marc Peter Deisennroth

His work is crucial for moving beyond classical deterministic optimization, enabling solutions for complex, real-world problems characterized by uncertainty, incomplete information, and noisy data. Mastering these techniques is vital for tackling modern challenges in fields like finance, robotics, and data science.

Students will learn the foundational principles of probabilistic optimization, including key concepts like Bayesian optimization and likely focus areas such as Gaussian processes for modeling unknown functions. They will understand how to efficiently optimize expensive-to-evaluate functions, manage exploration vs. exploitation trade-offs, and solve problems where the objective function is not known in closed form.

It serves as a prerequisite for understanding advanced AI and automated decision-making systems.
