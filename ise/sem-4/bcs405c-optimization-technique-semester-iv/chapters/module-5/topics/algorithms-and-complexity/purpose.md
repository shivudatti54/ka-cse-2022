# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define a finite automaton and explain its components.
2. Describe the difference between a DFA and an NFA.
3. Explain the purpose of the transition function in a finite automaton.
4. Identify the recognition power of a DFA.
5. Construct a transition diagram and table for a DFA and NFA.
6. Prove that a language is regular or not.
