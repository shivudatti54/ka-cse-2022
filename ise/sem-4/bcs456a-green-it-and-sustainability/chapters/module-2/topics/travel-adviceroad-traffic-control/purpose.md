### Learning Purpose: Travel Advice & Road Traffic Control

**1. Why is this topic important?**
Transportation is a major contributor to global carbon emissions and energy consumption. This topic is crucial because inefficient road traffic directly impacts environmental sustainability through increased fuel usage and pollution. Green IT offers innovative solutions to optimize travel and traffic flow, making it a key area for reducing the environmental footprint of our mobility systems.

**2. What will students learn?**
Students will learn how Green IT principles and technologies—such as AI-powered traffic management systems, IoT sensors, and data analytics—are applied to create intelligent transportation networks. They will analyze how real-time travel advice apps and adaptive traffic control systems work to reduce congestion, idle time, and overall emissions.

**3. How does it connect to other concepts?**
This topic directly connects to broader Module 2 concepts like sustainable hardware (the sensors and infrastructure), energy-efficient data centers (processing the traffic data), and the role of big data in environmental decision-making. It also builds on the foundation of Green IT by demonstrating its practical application in a critical public sector.

**4. Real-world applications**
Students will examine real-world applications including smart traffic lights that adapt to real-time flow, navigation apps like Google Maps and Waze that suggest eco-routes, and city-wide initiatives like congestion charging schemes. This knowledge is directly applicable to careers in urban planning, logistics, software development, and environmental policy.
