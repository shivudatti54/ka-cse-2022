### Learning Purpose: Emerging Technologies and Their Environmental Impact

**1. Why is this topic important?**
Understanding the environmental impact of emerging technologies is crucial for developing sustainable IT strategies. While innovations like AI, IoT, and blockchain offer transformative potential, they also carry significant energy demands and e-waste implications. This topic equips future IT professionals to critically assess and mitigate the hidden environmental costs of technological progress.

**2. What will students learn?**
Students will learn to analyze the lifecycle environmental footprint of key emerging technologies. This includes evaluating the energy consumption of AI model training, data centers, and cryptocurrency mining, as well as the resource extraction and e-waste challenges associated with IoT devices. They will also explore "Green by Design" principles for developing more sustainable tech solutions.

**3. How does it connect to other concepts?**
This topic directly builds on the foundational principles of Green IT from Module 1. It connects the macro-level goals of sustainability to the micro-level design and implementation of specific technologies. It also provides a critical lens for evaluating the claims of future modules on policy, green software development, and circular economy models.

**4. Real-world applications**
This knowledge is applied when selecting cloud providers based on their energy mix, designing energy-efficient algorithms, advocating for corporate sustainability policies, and making informed decisions about technology adoption that balance innovation with ecological responsibility.
