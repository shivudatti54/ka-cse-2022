# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define distributed systems and explain their key characteristics
2. Identify and explain the challenges faced in designing distributed systems
3. Differentiate between the various types of transparency in distributed systems
4. Explain the request-reply protocol and its role in client-server communication
5. Describe the architecture and working mechanism of Remote Procedure Call (RPC)
6. Compare and contrast RPC with Remote Method Invocation (RMI)
7. Apply these concepts to analyze real-world distributed systems
