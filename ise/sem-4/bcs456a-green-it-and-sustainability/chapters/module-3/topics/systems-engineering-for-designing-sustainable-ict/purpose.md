Learning "Systems Engineering for Designing Sustainable ICT-Based Architectures" is crucial as it enables professionals to create sustainable and environmentally-friendly information and communication technologies (ICT) systems. This topic matters because it addresses the growing concern of climate change and the need for green IT practices to reduce carbon footprint and minimize e-waste. Real-world applications of this topic include designing energy-efficient data centers, developing sustainable supply chain management systems, and creating environmentally-friendly IoT devices. This topic also connects to other concepts such as ecological measures, ethical considerations, and the principles of sustainable development.
