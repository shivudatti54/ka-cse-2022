Learning about "Of Information and Communication Technologies" (ICT) is crucial because it enables individuals to understand the foundation of modern communication systems, network infrastructure, and data processing. This knowledge matters as it informs the development of sustainable and environmentally-friendly technologies, such as Green IT, which address the environmental and social impacts of ICT use. Real-world applications of ICT include e-government services, online education platforms, and telemedicine systems, which can improve efficiency, accessibility, and overall quality of life. This topic also connects to concepts like environmental sustainability, social responsibility, and information security, highlighting the need for a balanced approach to ICT adoption.
