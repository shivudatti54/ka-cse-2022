# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define and identify reflexive, symmetric, antisymmetric, and transitive properties in relations
2. Determine whether a given relation exhibits specific properties using formal definitions
3. Distinguish between symmetric and antisymmetric properties and understand that they are not opposites
4. Recognize equivalence relations based on the combination of reflexive, symmetric, and transitive properties
5. Identify partial orders based on the combination of reflexive, antisymmetric, and transitive properties
6. Construct examples and counterexamples of relations with specific properties
