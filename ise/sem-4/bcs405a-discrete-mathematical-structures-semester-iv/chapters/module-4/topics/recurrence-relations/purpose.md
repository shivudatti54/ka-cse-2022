# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define recurrence relations and explain their importance in algorithm analysis
2. Solve recurrence relations using substitution, recursion tree, and Master Theorem methods
3. Apply the Master Theorem to standard divide-and-conquer algorithm recurrences
4. Identify which case of the Master Theorem applies to a given recurrence
5. Recognize the limitations of the Master Theorem and when alternative methods are needed
6. Analyze the time complexity of divide-and-conquer algorithms using recurrence relations
