### E-Waste (Management) Amendment Rules, 2023, 2024 Revision Notes

#### **Definition and Theorems**

- E-Waste: Electronic waste generated from discarded electronics, including computers, phones, and televisions.
- The Electronic and Electrical Equipment (Waste) Management (E-Waste) Rules, 2011, was amended in 2023 and 2024 to improve e-waste management and recycling.

#### **Key Provisions**

- **Prohibition of e-waste generation and transportation:**
  - Prohibits the generation and transportation of e-waste.
  - Imposes penalties for non-compliance.
- **Registration and licensing:**
  - Requires manufacturers, importers, and retailers to register with the Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB).
  - Licensing of e-waste treatment facilities.
- **E-waste management:**
  - E-waste must be collected, transported, and disposed of in a responsible manner.
  - Requirements for e-waste recycling and reuse.
- **Penalties and fines:**
  - Imposes penalties and fines for non-compliance with the rules.
