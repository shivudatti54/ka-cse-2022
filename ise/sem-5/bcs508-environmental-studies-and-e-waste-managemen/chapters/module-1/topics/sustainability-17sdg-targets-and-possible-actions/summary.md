# **Sustainability: 17 SDG Targets and Possible Actions**

## **Introduction**

The United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) aim to create a better world by 2030. The 17 SDGs are interconnected and interdependent, requiring a holistic approach to achieve them. This summary highlights the 17 SDG targets related to sustainability and possible actions to achieve them.

## **SDG 1: No Poverty**

- Target 1.1: End extreme poverty
- Formula: GDP (Gross Domestic Product) per capita
- Action: Implement progressive taxation, social protection programs, and education

## **SDG 2: Zero Hunger**

- Target 2.1: End hunger
- Formula: Food availability and access
- Action: Promote sustainable agriculture, reduce food waste, and support nutrition programs

## **SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being**

- Target 3.1: Ensure universal access to healthcare
- Formula: Healthcare utilization and outcomes
- Action: Invest in healthcare infrastructure, improve healthcare workforce, and promote healthy behaviors

## **SDG 4: Quality Education**

- Target 4.1: Ensure inclusive and equitable quality education
- Formula: Education enrollment and outcomes
- Action: Invest in education infrastructure, improve teacher training, and promote digital literacy

## **SDG 5: Gender Equality**

- Target 5.1: Achieve gender equality in education and employment
- Formula: Gender disparities in education and employment
- Action: Implement policies promoting gender equality, increase representation of women in leadership positions

## **SDG 6: Clean Water and Sanitation**

- Target 6.1: Ensure universal access to safe drinking water
- Formula: Water availability and access
- Action: Invest in water infrastructure, promote water conservation, and improve sanitation facilities

## **SDG 7: Affordable and Clean Energy**

- Target 7.1: Ensure universal access to affordable and clean energy
- Formula: Energy availability and access
- Action: Invest in renewable energy sources, improve energy efficiency, and develop energy storage technologies

## **SDG 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth**

- Target 8.1: Promote sustainable and inclusive economic growth
- Formula: GDP growth and employment rates
- Action: Implement policies promoting sustainable agriculture and manufacturing, increase access to education and training

## **SDG 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure**

- Target 9.1: Build resilient infrastructure
- Formula: Infrastructure development and maintenance
- Action: Invest in renewable energy sources, improve transportation systems, and develop smart cities

## **SDG 10: Reduced Inequalities**

- Target 10.1: Reduce inequality within and between countries
- Formula: Income and social exclusion
- Action: Implement policies promoting social inclusion, increase access to education and healthcare, and reduce income inequality

## **SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities**

- Target 11.1: Make cities and human settlements inclusive, safe, and resilient
- Formula: Urbanization and infrastructure development
- Action: Invest in sustainable urban planning, improve waste management, and develop green spaces

## **SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production**

- Target 12.1: Ensure sustainable consumption and production patterns
- Formula: Carbon footprint and resource use
- Action: Implement policies promoting sustainable consumption, reduce plastic use, and increase recycling

## **SDG 13: Climate Action**

- Target 13.1: Take urgent action to combat climate change
- Formula: Greenhouse gas emissions
- Action: Invest in renewable energy sources, improve energy efficiency, and develop climate-resilient infrastructure

## **SDG 14: Life Below Water**

- Target 14.1: Conserve and sustainably use the world's oceans
- Formula: Ocean health and biodiversity
- Action: Implement policies promoting marine conservation, reduce plastic use, and increase sustainable fishing practices

## **SDG 15: Life on Land**

- Target 15.1: Protect, restore, and promote sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems
- Formula: Land degradation and biodiversity
- Action: Implement policies promoting sustainable land use, reduce deforestation, and increase afforestation

## **SDG 16: Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions**

- Target 16.1: Strengthen and reform institutions and promote inclusive and representative decision-making processes
- Formula: Governance and institutional capacity
- Action: Invest in institutional capacity building, promote transparency and accountability, and increase access to justice

## **SDG 17: Partnerships for the Goals**

- Target 17.1: Revitalize the global partnership for sustainable development
- Formula: International cooperation and partnerships
- Action: Foster international cooperation, increase funding for sustainable development, and promote knowledge sharing.

Note: This summary provides an overview of the 17 SDG targets and possible actions to achieve them.
