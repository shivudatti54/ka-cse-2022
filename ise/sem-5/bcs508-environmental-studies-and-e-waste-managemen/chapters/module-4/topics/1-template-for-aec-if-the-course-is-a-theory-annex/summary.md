# Environmental Studies and E-Waste Management - Waste Management

## Module: Waste Management

### Topic: 1 TEMPLATE for AEC (if the course is a theory) Annexure-IV Self-Study Component (SSC): Case studies on waste management options Textbook 1: CH- 6

#### Key Points:

- **Types of Waste:**
  - Solid waste
  - Liquid waste
  - Hazardous waste
  - Electronic waste (E-Waste)
- **Waste Management Hierarchy:**
  - Reduce
  - Reuse
  - Recycle
  - Dispose
- **Waste Management Techniques:**
  - Incineration
  - Composting
  - Landfilling
  - Recycling
- **Environmental Impact:**
  - Air pollution
  - Water pollution
  - Soil pollution
- **E-Waste Management:**
  - E-Waste generation
  - E-Waste recycling
  - E-Waste disposal

#### Important Formulas and Definitions:

- **Waste Management Index (WMI):**
  - WMI = (Reduced waste / Total waste) x 100
- **Solid Waste Management (SWM):**
  - SWM = SW generation - SW collection - SW treatment - SW disposal
- **Recycling Rate:**
  - Recycling rate = (Recycled waste / Total waste) x 100

#### Theorems and Concepts:

- **Zero-Waste Policy:**
  - Aims to minimize waste generation
- **Circular Economy:**
  - Aims to promote reuse and recycling of materials

#### Quick Revision Points:

- Understand the hierarchy of waste management techniques
- Identify the types of waste and their management
- Calculate the Waste Management Index (WMI)
- Apply the concepts of Zero-Waste Policy and Circular Economy
