# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1.  Define environmental ethics and explain its importance in the context of environmental studies.
2.  Differentiate between anthropocentric, biocentric, and ecocentric ethical frameworks.
3.  Explain the core principles of Deep Ecology, Ecofeminism, and Social Ecology.
4.  Describe Aldo Leopold's Land Ethic and its significance.
5.  Analyze the concept of environmental justice and provide real-world examples.
6.  Apply different ethical perspectives to contemporary environmental issues like climate change and conservation.
