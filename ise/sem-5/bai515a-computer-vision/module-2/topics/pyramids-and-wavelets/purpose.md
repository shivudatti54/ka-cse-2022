# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Explain the concept of multi-resolution analysis and its importance in image processing
2. Differentiate between Gaussian and Laplacian pyramids and describe their construction processes
3. Understand the fundamental principles of wavelet transform and its decomposition process
4. Identify the four subbands of wavelet decomposition and interpret their content
5. Compare and contrast pyramid and wavelet approaches for multi-resolution analysis
6. Apply pyramid and wavelet techniques to practical image processing problems
