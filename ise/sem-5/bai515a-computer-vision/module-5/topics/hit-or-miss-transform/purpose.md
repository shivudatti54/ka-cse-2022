# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Define the hit-or-miss transform and explain its mathematical formulation
2. Differentiate between the foreground (hit) and background (miss) structuring elements and their respective roles
3. Apply the hit-or-miss transform to detect specific patterns in binary images
4. Identify practical applications of the hit-or-miss transform in computer vision tasks
5. Compare and contrast the hit-or-miss transform with other morphological operations
6. Recognize the limitations and challenges associated with implementing hit-or-miss transform
