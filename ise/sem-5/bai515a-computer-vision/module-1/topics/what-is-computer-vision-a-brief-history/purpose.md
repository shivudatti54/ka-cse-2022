# Why Study What is Computer Vision?

## Foundation for All CV Topics

Understanding what computer vision is and its scope provides the **conceptual foundation** for all subsequent topics. Before diving into specific algorithms, you must understand the problems CV aims to solve and the challenges involved.

## Exam Relevance

| Aspect         | Details                                                           |
| -------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Frequency      | Appears in most CV exams as introductory question                 |
| Marks          | 5-10 marks (definitions, applications, challenges)                |
| Question Types | Short answers, essay on applications, compare with related fields |

## Industry Relevance

- **High Demand**: CV engineers are among the most sought-after AI professionals
- **Diverse Applications**: Healthcare, automotive, retail, security, and more
- **Research Active**: New architectures and methods published regularly
- **Foundation for AI Products**: Most AI-powered products include CV components

## Connection to Other Topics

After understanding CV fundamentals, you will study:

- **Image Representation**: How images are stored and processed
- **Feature Extraction**: SIFT, SURF, HOG for pattern detection
- **Object Detection**: R-CNN, YOLO for locating objects
- **Image Segmentation**: Pixel-level understanding

## Learning Outcomes

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Define computer vision and its objectives
2. List key tasks in computer vision
3. Explain the CV pipeline from acquisition to decision
4. Identify real-world applications of CV
5. Describe major challenges in the field
