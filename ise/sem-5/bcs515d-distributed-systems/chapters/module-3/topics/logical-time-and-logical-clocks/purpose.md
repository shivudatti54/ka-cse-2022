Logical time and logical clocks are crucial in distributed systems as they enable processes to coordinate and synchronize their actions, ensuring that all nodes in a system agree on the current time and the order of events. This is essential for maintaining consistency and predictability in distributed systems, where processes may operate in parallel or be subject to network delays. Understanding logical time and clocks helps developers design and implement reliable and efficient distributed systems.

Logical time and clocks have numerous real-world applications, including distributed databases, cloud computing, and parallel computing.
