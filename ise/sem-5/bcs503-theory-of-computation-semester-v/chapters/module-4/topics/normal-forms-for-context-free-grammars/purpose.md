# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define and identify Chomsky Normal Form and Greibach Normal Form
2. Convert any context-free grammar to Chomsky Normal Form following the proper sequence of steps
3. Explain the purpose and benefits of using normal forms for context-free grammars
4. Apply the CYK parsing algorithm to determine if a string is generated by a CNF grammar
5. Differentiate between the applications and characteristics of CNF and GNF
6. Identify and eliminate problematic productions (ε-productions, unit productions, useless symbols)
