# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Explain the concept of the halting problem and its significance
2. Describe the busy beaver problem and its implications
3. Understand the relationship between Turing machines and real computers
4. Analyze the limitations of computational power and their implications for computer science
5. Apply the concepts of the halting problem and the busy beaver problem to real-world scenarios
6. Evaluate the significance of the halting problem and the busy beaver problem for the development of computer science
