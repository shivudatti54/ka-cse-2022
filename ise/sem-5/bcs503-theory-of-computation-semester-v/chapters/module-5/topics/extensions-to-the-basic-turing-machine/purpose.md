# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Explain the concept of a multi-tape Turing machine and its advantages.
2. Describe the concept of a multi-head Turing machine and its advantages.
3. Define a non-deterministic Turing machine and its advantages and disadvantages.
4. Compare and contrast different extensions of the basic Turing machine.
5. Analyze the advantages and disadvantages of each extension.
6. Apply the concepts of Turing machine extensions to solve problems and puzzles.
