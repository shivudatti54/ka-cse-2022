# Why Study What is Artificial Intelligence?

## Big Picture Foundation

Understanding what AI is forms the conceptual foundation for the entire field. This topic establishes the vocabulary, frameworks, and paradigms that will be used throughout your AI studies.

## Exam Relevance

| Aspect         | Details                                           |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| Frequency      | Appears in almost every AI exam                   |
| Marks          | 6-10 marks (definitions, approaches, comparisons) |
| Question Types | Definitions, essay questions, comparisons         |

## Practical Value

- **Career Clarity:** Understanding AI scope helps identify career paths
- **Project Selection:** Choose appropriate AI techniques for problems
- **Communication:** Use correct terminology with colleagues and stakeholders
- **Expectation Management:** Know what AI can and cannot do

## Learning Outcomes

After this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define AI using multiple perspectives
2. Explain the four approaches to AI
3. Distinguish between types of AI systems
4. Identify core components of AI systems
5. Recognize AI applications in real-world scenarios
