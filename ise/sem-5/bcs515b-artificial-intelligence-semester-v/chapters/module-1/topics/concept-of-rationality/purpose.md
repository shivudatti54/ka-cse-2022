# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define the concept of rationality in the context of artificial intelligence
2. Distinguish between rational agents and omniscient agents
3. Explain the principle of rationality and its components
4. Identify different types of rationality (perfect vs. bounded)
5. Analyze how environmental properties affect rational behavior
6. Evaluate the rationality of agents in various scenarios based on performance measures
