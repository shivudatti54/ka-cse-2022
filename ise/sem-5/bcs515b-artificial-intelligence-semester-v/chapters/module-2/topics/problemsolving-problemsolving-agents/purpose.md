# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define what a problem-solving agent is and its main goal.
2. Explain the difference between informed and uninformed search.
3. Describe the different problem-solving techniques, including BFS, DFS, UCS, and A\* search.
4. Analyze the time and space complexity of different algorithms.
5. Apply problem-solving techniques to solve real-world problems.
6. Evaluate the advantages and disadvantages of different problem-solving techniques.
