**1. Why this topic matters:**
Learning about software and software engineering is crucial in today's technology-driven world, as it enables individuals to design, develop, and maintain software systems that meet business needs and solve real-world problems. The knowledge gained from this topic has numerous real-world applications, such as:

- Developing scalable web applications for e-commerce platforms, social media, and online services.
- Designing and implementing software solutions for healthcare, finance, and education sectors.
- Creating mobile apps for various industries, including gaming, navigation, and productivity.
- Collaborating with cross-functional teams to deliver software projects on time and within budget.

Software and software engineering concepts connect to other areas of computer science and related disciplines, such as:
