# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define guided and unguided media.
2. Explain the advantages and disadvantages of guided media.
3. Explain the advantages and disadvantages of unguided media.
4. Compare and contrast guided and unguided media.
5. Identify the different types of guided and unguided media.
6. Explain the primary advantage and disadvantage of guided and unguided media.
