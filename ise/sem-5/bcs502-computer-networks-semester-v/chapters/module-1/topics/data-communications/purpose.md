# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Understand the basics of data communications and networks.
2. Describe the different types of networks, transmission media, and packet switching techniques.
3. Explain the OSI model and TCP/IP model.
4. Understand the concept of packet switching and circuit switching.
5. Describe the difference between connectionless and connection-oriented packet switching.
6. Identify the primary function of the physical layer in the OSI model.
