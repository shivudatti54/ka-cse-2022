# Learning Objectives
After studying this topic, you should be able to:
1.  Define Natural Language Processing (NLP) and explain its significance in the field of Artificial Intelligence.
2.  Describe, in order, the key steps involved in a standard NLP processing pipeline (e.g., tokenization, stemming, POS tagging).
3.  Differentiate between text normalization techniques, specifically stemming and lemmatization, providing examples of each.
4.  Explain the purposes and outcomes of syntactic analysis (parsing) and semantic analysis (e.g., Word Sense Disambiguation, Named Entity Recognition).
5.  Identify and describe at least three real-world applications of NLP technology (e.g., machine translation, sentiment analysis).
6.  Compare and contrast simple word representation models (Bag-of-Words) with advanced models (Word Embeddings).
