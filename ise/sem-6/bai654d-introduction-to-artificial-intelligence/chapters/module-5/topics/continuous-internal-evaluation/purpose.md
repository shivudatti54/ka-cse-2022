### Learning Purpose: Continuous Internal Evaluation

**1. Importance**
This topic is crucial as it shifts the focus from a single high-stakes exam to a more holistic assessment of a student's understanding of AI. It ensures consistent engagement with the complex, cumulative material, allowing for early identification of knowledge gaps and providing ongoing feedback for improvement throughout the module.

**2. Learning Outcomes**
Students will learn to demonstrate their comprehension of core AI concepts through varied methods (e.g., quizzes, projects, presentations). They will develop the ability to apply theoretical knowledge to practical problems, articulate their findings, and receive constructive feedback, fostering a deeper and more resilient understanding of the subject matter.

**3. Connection to Other Concepts**
Continuous evaluation is intrinsically linked to all preceding and subsequent modules (e.g., Machine Learning, Neural Networks, Ethics in AI). It assesses not just isolated facts but a student's ability to synthesize and connect these concepts, ensuring that foundational knowledge is solid before advancing to more complex topics.

**4. Real-World Application**
This process mirrors the agile, iterative development cycles and continuous performance review systems used in the tech industry. It prepares students for a professional environment where ongoing learning, regular code/design reviews, and incremental project evaluation are standard practice for building robust AI systems.
