# **Text Book 1: Ch 1 Revision Notes**

## **What is Artificial Intelligence?**

- Artificial Intelligence (AI): The science and engineering of creating intelligent machines that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence.
- Definition: "Artificial intelligence is the science and engineering of making intelligent machines that can think and learn like humans."

## **Problems in AI**

- Problem Spaces: Areas of AI where problems need to be defined and solved.
- Types of Problems:
  - **Classification**: Assigning objects to predefined categories.
  - **Regression**: Predicting continuous values.
  - **Clustering**: Grouping similar objects.

## **Search Problems**

- **Problem Spaces Search**: Searching an exhaustive set of possible solutions.
- **Heuristics**: Using rules of thumb or experience to guide the search.
- **Greedy Search**: Choosing the best available solution at each step.

## **Important Formulas and Definitions**

- **Universal Turing Machine**: A theoretical model for computation.
- **Artificial Neural Networks**: Models inspired by the structure and function of the brain.
- **Markov Decision Process**: A mathematical framework for decision-making in uncertain environments.

## **Key Theorems**

- **P versus NP problem**: A fundamental question in computational complexity theory.
- **Chaitin's Incompressibility Theorem**: A result on the limits of compressing information.
