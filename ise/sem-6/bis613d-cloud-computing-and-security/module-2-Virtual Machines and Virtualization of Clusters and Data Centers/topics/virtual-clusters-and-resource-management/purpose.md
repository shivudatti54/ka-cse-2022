# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1.  Define a virtual cluster and differentiate it from a physical cluster.
2.  Explain the architectural components and layers of a virtual cluster.
3.  Describe the process and benefits of dynamic provisioning for virtual clusters.
4.  Identify key resource management tasks: allocation, load balancing, and consolidation.
5.  Detail the steps involved in live migration and its importance for resource management.
6.  Compare and contrast High Availability (HA) and Fault Tolerance (FT) features.
