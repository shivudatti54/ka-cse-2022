# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Analyze and evaluate the key performance metrics in distributed systems including throughput, latency, and scalability
2. Identify and explain the fundamental security principles and mechanisms used to protect distributed systems
3. Assess energy efficiency concerns and calculate relevant metrics like Power Usage Effectiveness (PUE)
4. Evaluate the trade-offs between performance, security, and energy efficiency in system design decisions
5. Apply appropriate optimization techniques to balance performance, security, and energy requirements
6. Compare real-world implementations of performance, security, and energy efficiency strategies in cloud environments
