### Learning Purpose: Module 5 - M. Vijayalakshmi

**1. Why is this topic important?**
This topic is important as it provides a practical, human-centric perspective on applying machine learning (ML) principles. By studying the work of a specific contributor like M. Vijayalakshmi, students move beyond abstract theory to understand how real-world problems are identified, approached, and solved using ML. This fosters an appreciation for the diverse applications and ethical considerations inherent in the field.

**2. What will students learn?**
Students will learn about the specific ML methodologies, algorithms, and research contributions associated with M. Vijayalakshmi. They will analyze case studies from her work, examining the problem formulation, data handling, model selection, and implementation strategies used to achieve results. This will develop their critical ability to evaluate and replicate applied ML research.

**3. How does it connect to other concepts?**
This module connects foundational concepts from previous modules—such as supervised learning, neural networks, and data preprocessing—to their practical application. It demonstrates how theoretical knowledge is integrated to form a complete ML pipeline, bridging the gap between algorithm-centric learning and solution-oriented deployment.

**4. Real-world applications**
The applications will be drawn directly from the case studies, which likely span impactful domains such as healthcare (e.g., medical image analysis, disease prediction), computational finance, or natural language processing. Students will see how ML creates tangible value and solves critical challenges in industry and society.
