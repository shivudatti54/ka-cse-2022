# Learning Objectives
After studying this topic, you should be able to:
1. Differentiate between biological and artificial neurons and explain the components of an artificial neuron
2. Describe the architecture and learning process of a perceptron and identify its limitations
3. Classify and compare different types of neural networks based on their architecture and learning mechanisms
4. Explain the working principle of backpropagation algorithm in training neural networks
5. Identify appropriate neural network architectures for specific problem domains and applications
6. Analyze the advantages and challenges associated with different neural network types
