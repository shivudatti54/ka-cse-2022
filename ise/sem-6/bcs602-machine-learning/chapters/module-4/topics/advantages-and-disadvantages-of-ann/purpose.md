### Learning Purpose: Module 4 - Advantages and Disadvantages of ANN

**1. Why is this topic important?**
Understanding the advantages and disadvantages of Artificial Neural Networks (ANNs) is critical for making informed decisions about their application. It prevents the misuse of a powerful tool and ensures that resources are allocated efficiently to problems where ANNs provide a clear benefit over simpler models.

**2. What will students learn?**
Students will learn to articulate the key strengths of ANNs, such as their ability to model complex, non-linear relationships and their robustness to noise in data. They will also identify their significant drawbacks, including the "black box" nature, high computational cost, substantial data requirements, and propensity for overfitting.

**3. How does it connect to other concepts?**
This analysis directly contrasts ANNs with other machine learning models (e.g., decision trees, linear regression) covered in previous modules. It provides the foundational knowledge needed to guide the selection of an appropriate model architecture, a key step before delving into more advanced deep learning topics.

**4. Real-world applications**
This knowledge is essential for real-world tasks like developing medical diagnosis systems (weighing accuracy against explainability), creating financial fraud detection algorithms (balancing performance with computational expense), and implementing natural language processing applications (requiring massive datasets).
