# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Explain the concept of LR(0) items and their role in SLR parsing
2. Construct the canonical collection of LR(0) items for a given grammar
3. Calculate FOLLOW sets for non-terminals in a context-free grammar
4. Build complete SLR parsing tables including ACTION and GOTO components
5. Identify and resolve shift-reduce and reduce-reduce conflicts in SLR parsing
6. Compare SLR parsing with other LR parsing techniques (LALR, canonical LR)
