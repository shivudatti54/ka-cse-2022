# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Explain the FIFO principle and why it makes queues suitable for various applications
2. Identify at least five real-world scenarios where queues are essential
3. Describe how operating systems use queues for process scheduling and resource management
4. Explain the role of queues in network routing and traffic management
5. Implement queue-based solutions for problems like BFS graph traversal
6. Compare and contrast different types of queues (linear, circular, priority) and their appropriate applications
