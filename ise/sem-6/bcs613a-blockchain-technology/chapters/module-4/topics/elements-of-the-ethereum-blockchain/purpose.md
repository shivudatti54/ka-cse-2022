### Learning Purpose: Elements of the Ethereum Blockchain

**1. Why is this topic important?**
Understanding the core elements of Ethereum is crucial because it is the leading platform for decentralized applications (dApps) and smart contracts. This knowledge is foundational for developers, entrepreneurs, and anyone looking to build or interact with Web3 technologies, NFTs, and DeFi protocols.

**2. What will students learn?**
Students will learn the fundamental components that constitute the Ethereum blockchain. This includes the structure of accounts (Externally Owned and Contract), the role of gas fees and the EVM (Ethereum Virtual Machine) in transaction execution, and how smart contracts are deployed and interact. They will also explore the purpose of Ether (ETH) as the native cryptocurrency.

**3. How does it connect to other concepts?**
This module directly builds upon previous knowledge of general blockchain principles (like decentralization and consensus). It provides the specific architectural understanding needed to grasp subsequent topics such as smart contract development (Solidity), dApp design, and the functionality of Layer-2 scaling solutions.

**4. Real-world applications**
This knowledge is directly applicable to creating and auditing smart contracts, developing dApps, participating in DeFi (e.g., lending, borrowing), trading NFTs, and understanding the transaction mechanics behind most of the current Web3 ecosystem.
