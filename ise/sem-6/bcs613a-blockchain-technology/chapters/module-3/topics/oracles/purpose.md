# Learning Objectives
After studying this topic, you should be able to:
1. Explain the concept and purpose of smart contracts in the Bitcoin ecosystem
2. Describe how Bitcoin Script works and its key opcodes for creating spending conditions
3. Identify different types of Bitcoin smart contracts (multisig, timelocked, HTLC)
4. Understand the role and challenges of oracles in blockchain systems
5. Compare different oracle design patterns (centralized, federated, decentralized)
6. Analyze real-world applications of Bitcoin smart contracts and oracles
