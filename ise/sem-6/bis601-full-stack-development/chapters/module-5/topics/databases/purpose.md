# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Explain the structure and organization of WordNet, including synsets and semantic relations
2. Identify and describe the major semantic relationships in WordNet (hypernymy, hyponymy, meronymy, etc.)
3. Demonstrate how to access and use WordNet programmatically for basic NLP tasks
4. Analyze how WordNet enhances information retrieval and other NLP applications
5. Compare WordNet with other lexical resources like FrameNet and PropBank
6. Evaluate the limitations of WordNet and discuss approaches to address them
