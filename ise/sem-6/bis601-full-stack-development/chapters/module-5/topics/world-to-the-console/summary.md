# **World! to the Console**

## **Key Points**

- **Understanding the World!**: A simulation of a simple world where the player's goal is to survive and thrive.
- **Key Features**:
  - Day/Night cycles and weather
  - Basic crafting and trading system
  - Simple AI for NPCs
- **Gameplay Mechanics**:
  - Resource gathering and management
  - Building and customizing base
  - Exploration and combat
