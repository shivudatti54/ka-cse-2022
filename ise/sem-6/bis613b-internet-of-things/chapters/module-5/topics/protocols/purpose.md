# Learning Objectives
After studying this topic, you should be able to:
1.  Define the core purpose of a VPN protocol and the concept of tunneling.
2.  Explain the architecture of IPSec, including its components (AH, ESP), modes (Transport, Tunnel), and key exchange process (IKE Phases 1 & 2).
3.  Describe the operation of OpenVPN, highlighting its use of SSL/TLS and its strengths in firewall traversal.
4.  Articulate the design philosophy behind WireGuard and its use of modern cryptography for simplicity and performance.
5.  Compare and contrast IPSec, OpenVPN, and WireGuard based on performance, security, ease of use, and typical deployment scenarios.
6.  Identify key security considerations when implementing any VPN solution, such as cryptographic strength and Perfect Forward Secrecy.
