Learning about Chapter-2, the Internet of Things (IoT), is crucial as it has revolutionized the way we live and work, making our devices and surroundings more interconnected and automated. This topic matters because it has far-reaching implications for industries such as healthcare, transportation, and energy, among others. Real-world applications of IoT include smart homes, wearables, and industrial automation, which can improve efficiency, productivity, and quality of life. This concept also converges with other areas, such as cyber-physical systems, data analytics, and artificial intelligence, making it a fundamental aspect of the digital transformation of industries.
