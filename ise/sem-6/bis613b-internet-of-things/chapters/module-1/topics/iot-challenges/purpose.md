# Learning Objectives
After studying this topic, you should be able to:
1.  Define a distributed system and contrast it with a centralized system.
2.  Identify and explain at least six fundamental challenges in designing and implementing distributed systems.
3.  Differentiate between partial failure and total failure and explain why partial failure is a significant challenge.
4.  Describe the concept of transparency and list its different forms with examples.
5.  Recall the eight fallacies of distributed computing and explain their implications for system design.
6.  Analyze a simple distributed scenario and identify the potential challenges related to concurrency, scalability, or failure handling.
