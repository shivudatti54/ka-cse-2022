# Learning Objectives
After studying this topic, you should be able to:
1. Identify and describe the six levels of IoT systems based on their complexity and components
2. Differentiate between various IoT deployment templates and their appropriate use cases
3. Analyze real-world IoT applications and classify them according to IoT levels and deployment templates
4. Evaluate the trade-offs between different IoT levels in terms of cost, complexity, scalability, and performance
5. Recommend appropriate IoT levels and deployment templates for given scenarios and requirements
6. Design basic IoT system architectures considering the appropriate level and deployment strategy
