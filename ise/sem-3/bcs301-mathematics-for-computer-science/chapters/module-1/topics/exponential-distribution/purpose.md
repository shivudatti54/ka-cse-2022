# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define the exponential distribution and identify its probability density function (PDF) and cumulative distribution function (CDF)

2. Calculate the mean, variance, and standard deviation of an exponential distribution given the rate parameter λ

3. Apply the memoryless property to solve conditional probability problems involving waiting times

4. Explain the relationship between the exponential distribution and the Poisson process

5. Compute probabilities involving exponential random variables using both the CDF and complement forms

6. Interpret the constant hazard function and its implications in reliability engineering contexts

7. Solve real-world problems involving waiting times, inter-arrival times, and component lifetimes using exponential distribution formulas

8. Distinguish the exponential distribution from other continuous distributions based on its unique properties
