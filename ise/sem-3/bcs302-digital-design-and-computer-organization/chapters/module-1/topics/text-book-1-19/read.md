# **Text Book 1: 1.9 - Digital Design and Computer Organization**

### 1.9 Digital Design: Introduction

---

In this topic, we will explore the basics of digital design and its importance in computer organization. Digital design involves the creation of digital circuits that can perform specific functions.

### 1.9.1 Definition of Digital Design

---

Digital design is the process of creating digital circuits that can process information in the form of binary digits (bits). These circuits are used to perform various functions such as arithmetic, logic, and control.

### 1.9.2 Importance of Digital Design

---

Digital design is crucial in computer organization as it forms the basis of all digital systems. Digital design enables the creation of digital circuits that can perform specific functions, which are then combined to form larger systems.

### Key Concepts

---

- **Binary System**: A number system that uses only two digits, 0 and 1.
- **Binary Code**: A sequence of binary digits that represent information.
- **Digital Circuit**: A circuit that processes digital information using binary digits.

### 1.9.3 Types of Digital Design

---

There are two types of digital design:

- **Sequential Design**: A type of digital design where the output of a circuit depends on the current input and the state of the circuit.
- **Combinational Design**: A type of digital design where the output of a circuit depends only on the current inputs.

### 1.9.4 Digital Design Techniques

---

There are several digital design techniques used to create digital circuits, including:

- **Combinatorial Logic**: A technique used to create combinational digital circuits using logic gates.
- **Sequential Logic**: A technique used to create sequential digital circuits using flip-flops and counters.

### 1.9.5 Digital Design Tools

---

Digital design tools are software programs used to design and simulate digital circuits. Some common digital design tools include:

- **Logic Simulators**: Software programs used to simulate the behavior of digital circuits.
- **Digital Design Software**: Software programs used to design digital circuits using graphical user interfaces.

### Practice Questions

---

1.  What is the definition of digital design?
2.  What are the two types of digital design?
3.  What are the key concepts in digital design?

### Answers

---

1.  Digital design is the process of creating digital circuits that can process information in the form of binary digits.

3.  The key concepts in digital design include binary system, binary code, digital circuit, combinatorial logic, sequential logic, and digital design tools.
