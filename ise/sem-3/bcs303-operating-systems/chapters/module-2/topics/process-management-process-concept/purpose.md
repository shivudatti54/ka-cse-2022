**Why this topic matters:**
Learning Process Management: Process concept is crucial in operating systems as it enables efficient resource allocation, prioritization, and execution of tasks, ultimately leading to improved system performance and responsiveness. Understanding process management is essential for designing and building effective operating systems. It also has a significant impact on the overall efficiency and productivity of computer systems.

**Real-world applications:**
Process management has numerous real-world applications in fields such as operating systems, embedded systems, and distributed systems. For instance, process scheduling and prioritization are essential in managing tasks in cloud computing, social media platforms, and virtual reality applications. Additionally, process management is critical in managing resources in large-scale networks and distributed systems.

**Connection to other concepts:**
Process management is closely related to other fundamental concepts in operating systems, including memory management, file systems, and synchronization. Understanding process management also helps to appreciate the importance of synchronization, deadlocks, and resource allocation in operating systems. Furthermore, process management is closely tied to the concept of multi-threading and concurrent processing, which are essential in modern computing applications.
