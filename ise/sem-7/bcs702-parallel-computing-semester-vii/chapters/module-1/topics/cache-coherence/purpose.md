# Learning Objectives
After studying this topic, you should be able to:
1. Define cache coherence and explain why it is necessary in multiprocessor systems
2. Differentiate between write-through and write-back caching strategies
3. Compare and contrast snooping-based and directory-based coherence protocols
4. Explain the MESI protocol states and transitions with examples
5. Identify false sharing scenarios and propose solutions
6. Analyze the performance implications of cache coherence overhead
