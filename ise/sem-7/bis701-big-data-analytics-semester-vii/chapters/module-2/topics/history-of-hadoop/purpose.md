# Why Study History of AI?

## Big Picture Foundation
Understanding AI history provides crucial context for current developments. It explains why certain approaches succeeded while others failed, and helps avoid repeating past mistakes.

## Exam Relevance
| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Frequency | Common in AI exams, especially foundational courses |
| Marks | 6-10 marks (timelines, key figures, milestones) |
| Question Types | Timeline questions, significance of events, comparative essays |

## Practical Value
- **Perspective:** Understand why current AI looks the way it does
- **Avoiding Hype:** Recognize patterns of over-promising
- **Research Direction:** Learn from past failures
- **Industry Awareness:** Understand commercial AI cycles

## Learning Outcomes
After this topic, you should be able to:
1. Identify key milestones in AI history
2. Explain the causes and effects of AI winters
3. Describe contributions of major AI pioneers
4. Relate historical developments to current AI trends
5. Critically evaluate AI progress claims
