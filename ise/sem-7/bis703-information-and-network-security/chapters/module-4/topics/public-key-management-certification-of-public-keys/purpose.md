**Why this topic matters:**
Public-Key Management is crucial in ensuring the security and authenticity of online transactions, communication, and data exchange. The topic matters because it addresses the vulnerability of traditional encryption methods and provides a secure way to manage and verify public keys, safeguarding sensitive information in the digital age.

**Real-world applications:**
This topic has numerous real-world applications, including secure online banking, e-commerce, digital signatures, and virtual private networks (VPNs). It also forms the basis for various cryptographic protocols, such as SSL/TLS and PGP, which ensure secure communication over the internet.

**Connection to other concepts:**
Public-Key Management is closely related to other security concepts, such as Authentication, Authorization, and Accounting (AAA), and Cryptography. It also intersects with network security measures, like firewalls and intrusion detection systems, as well as with identity management and digital identity verification.
