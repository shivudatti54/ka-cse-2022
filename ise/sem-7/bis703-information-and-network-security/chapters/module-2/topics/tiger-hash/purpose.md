**Topic Explanation**

**1. Why this topic matters:**
Tiger Hash is a widely used hashing algorithm that plays a crucial role in information and network security, particularly in password storage and data protection. Learning Tiger Hash is essential to understand the security fundamentals of password hashing and its impact on protecting sensitive data.

**2. Real-world applications:**
Tiger Hash has real-world applications in various industries, including finance, healthcare, and government, where secure password storage and data protection are critical. It is widely used in password managers, authentication systems, and data encryption protocols to ensure the confidentiality and integrity of sensitive information.

**3. Connection to other concepts:**
Tiger Hash connects to other concepts in information and network security, such as cryptography, hash functions, and password storage. Understanding Tiger Hash is also essential to comprehend other hashing algorithms, such as Blowfish and MD5, and to appreciate the importance of password hashing in securing online transactions and protecting against cyber threats.
