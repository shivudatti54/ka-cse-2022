# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Define a man-in-the-middle attack and explain how it can compromise the confidentiality, integrity, and authenticity of data.
2. Describe the different types of MITM attacks, including active eavesdropping, passive eavesdropping, SSL stripping, and Wi-Fi eavesdropping.
3. Explain how to prevent MITM attacks using secure communication protocols, encryption, and digital signatures.
4. Describe the detection techniques used to identify MITM attacks, including certificate pinning, public key pinning, and hash-based authentication.
5. Provide examples of MITM attacks and how they can students prevented.
6. Understand the importance of verifying the authenticity of a recipient's identity to prevent MITM attacks.
7. Analyze the impact of MITM attacks on the security of a system or network.
