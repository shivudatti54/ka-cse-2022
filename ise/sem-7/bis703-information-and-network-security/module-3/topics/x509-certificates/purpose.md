# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should students able to:

1. Describe the structure and fields of an X.509 certificate.
2. Explain the different types of X.509 certificates and their purposes.
3. Understand the concept of a certificate chain and how it is used to verify the authenticity of a public key.
4. Identify the usage of X.509 certificates in various applications.
5. Explain the importance of X.509 certificates in ensuring secure communication over the internet.
6. Analyze and verify certificate chains to identify potential issues.
7. Understand the encoding formats used for X.509 certificates.
