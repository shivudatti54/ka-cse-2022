# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Explain the origin of Hadoop and its connection to the Nutch web search project

2. Describe how Google's research papers (GFS and MapReduce) influenced Hadoop's architecture

3. Identify the key contributors (Doug Cutting and Mike Cafarella) and their roles in Hadoop's creation

4. Trace the timeline of Hadoop's evolution from a personal project to an enterprise platform

5. Understand why Hadoop was developed as an alternative to traditional RDBMS for large-scale data processing

6. Recognize the significance of Yahoo's contributions in Hadoop's early development

7. Explain the importance of Hadoop becoming an Apache Top-Level Project in 2008

8. Appreciate the expansion of the Hadoop ecosystem and its components
