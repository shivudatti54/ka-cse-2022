# Operations: Insert-Delete-Display

=====================================

## Introduction

---

- Operations on Singly Linked List:
  - Insertion
  - Deletion
  - Display

## Insertion

---

- **Insertion by Node**: Insert a new node at a specific position in the linked list.
  - Time complexity: O(1) if inserting at the beginning, O(n) otherwise
  - Space complexity: O(1)
- **Insertion at the End**: Insert a new node at the end of the linked list.
  - Time complexity: O(1)
  - Space complexity: O(1)

## Deletion

---

- **Deletion by Node**: Delete a node at a specific position in the linked list.
  - Time complexity: O(1) if deleting at the beginning, O(n) otherwise
  - Space complexity: O(1)
- **Deletion at the End**: Delete the last node in the linked list.
  - Time complexity: O(1)
  - Space complexity: O(1)

## Display

---

- **Traverse the Linked List**: Print the elements of the linked list.
  - Time complexity: O(n)
  - Space complexity: O(1)

## Theorems and Formulas

---

- **Degree of a Node**: The degree of a node is the number of edges incident to it.
- **Degree Formula**: `d(n) = |E| - |V| + 1`, where `d(n)` is the degree of node `n`, `|E|` is the number of edges, and `|V|` is the number of vertices.
