**Introduction to Queues: Why it Matters, Real-World Applications, and Connections to Other Concepts**

**Why this topic matters:** Understanding queues is essential in computer science as it allows for efficient management of tasks, jobs, and requests in various applications, enabling better resource allocation and minimizing delays.

**Real-world applications:** Queues are widely used in real-world scenarios, such as waiting lines in banks, hospitals, and shopping malls, job scheduling in operating systems, and network protocols like TCP/IP.

**How it connects to other concepts:** Queues are closely related to other data structures like stacks, and linked lists, as they can be implemented using these data structures. Additionally, understanding queues is crucial for studying algorithms and data structures like Heaps, Trees, and Graphs, as queues are often used in conjunction with these data structures.
