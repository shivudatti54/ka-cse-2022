Module: Module 5
Topic: Books

**Learning Purpose:**

This topic is foundational because books are the primary vessels of knowledge, encapsulating human thought, history, and imagination. Understanding the evolution, structure, and impact of books is crucial for appreciating how information is curated, preserved, and disseminated across generations. Students will learn about the anatomy of a book, including its physical and digital components, the publishing process, and the role of books in shaping culture and society. This connects directly to broader concepts in information science, literacy, media studies, and the history of technology. Real-world applications include careers in publishing, librarianship, content creation, and digital archiving, empowering students to engage critically with written knowledge and contribute to its future evolution.
