# Learning Objectives
After studying this topic, you should be able to:
1. Explain the biological inspiration behind artificial neural networks and how artificial neurons mimic biological neurons
2. Describe the structure and function of a perceptron, including its mathematical formulation
3. Compare and contrast different activation functions and their appropriate applications
4. Understand and explain the forward propagation and backpropagation processes in neural networks
5. Identify different neural network architectures and their suitable application domains
6. Analyze the advantages, limitations, and challenges of neural network approaches
