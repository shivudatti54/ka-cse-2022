# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1.  Define the term "State of the Art" (SOTA) in the context of Artificial Intelligence.
2.  Identify the primary criterion (benchmark performance) used to designate an AI model as SOTA.
3.  List and describe at least three major domains of AI (e.g., NLP, Computer Vision, RL) and name a key SOTA achievement in each.
4.  Explain at least two key technological or resource drivers that have enabled recent advances in SOTA AI.
5.  Critically discuss at least three significant limitations or challenges facing current SOTA AI systems.
6.  Differentiate between the learning methodologies of a SOTA reinforcement learning system and a SOTA language model.
