Learning Chapter 2 on "Decentralization using blockchain" is crucial as it explains the fundamental concept of decentralization, which is essential for understanding the underlying technology behind blockchain. This topic matters because it provides a comprehensive understanding of how blockchain enables peer-to-peer transactions and data exchange without intermediaries. Real-world applications of decentralization include cryptocurrency transactions, supply chain management, and secure voting systems. This concept is also closely related to "Methods of decentralization" and "Blockchain and full ecosystem" as it provides a foundational understanding of how blockchain works within a larger ecosystem.
