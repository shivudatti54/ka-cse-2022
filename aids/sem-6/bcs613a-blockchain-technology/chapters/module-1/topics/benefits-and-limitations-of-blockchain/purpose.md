# Learning Objectives
After studying this topic, you should be able to:
1. Define and explain the six core features of blockchain technology: Decentralization, Immutability, Transparency, Security, Consensus, and Distributed Ledger.
2. Analyze real-world scenarios and identify suitable blockchain applications, justifying the choice based on its inherent features.
3. Differentiate between the three tiers of blockchain (Public, Consortium, Private) and recommend an appropriate tier for a given use case.
4. Evaluate the benefits and limitations of implementing a blockchain solution compared to a traditional centralized database system.
5. Explain how consensus mechanisms provide a solution to the Byzantine Generals problem in a distributed network.
6. Describe the role of cryptography (hashing, digital signatures) in enabling key blockchain features like security and immutability.
