# Learning Objectives
After studying this topic, you should be able to:
1.  Define and differentiate between the three main tiers of blockchain: Public, Private, and Consortium.
2.  Explain the key characteristics, advantages, and disadvantages of each blockchain tier.
3.  Describe the concept of the Blockchain Trilemma and its implications for blockchain design.
4.  Distinguish between Layer 1 (base protocol) and Layer 2 (scaling solution) blockchains.
5.  Identify common Layer 2 scaling techniques, such as Rollups and State Channels, and explain their basic function.
6.  Evaluate and suggest an appropriate blockchain tier (Public, Private, Consortium) for a given use case or scenario.
