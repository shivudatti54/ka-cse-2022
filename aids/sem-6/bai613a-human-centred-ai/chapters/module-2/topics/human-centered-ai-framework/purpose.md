# Learning Purpose: Human-Centred AI Framework

**1. Why is this topic important?**
Understanding the Human-Centred AI (HCAI) framework is crucial because it provides the foundational methodology for designing AI systems that augment human capabilities, prioritize user well-being, and operate ethically. It moves the focus from purely technical performance to creating trustworthy, responsible, and beneficial technology that serves human needs.

**2. What will students learn?**
Students will learn the core principles, components, and processes of an HCAI framework. This includes how to integrate human feedback throughout the AI lifecycle, conduct iterative prototyping and evaluation with users, and apply design thinking to ensure systems are usable, equitable, and transparent.

**3. How does it connect to other concepts?**
This topic directly builds upon the ethical principles (Module 1) by providing the practical tools to implement them. It connects to subsequent modules on metrics and evaluation (e.g., assessing trust and user experience) and is essential for understanding how to govern and audit AI systems responsibly.

**4. Real-world applications**
This framework is applied when designing collaborative robotics in manufacturing, developing accessible healthcare diagnostics tools, creating explainable loan approval systems in finance, and building any AI application where human-AI interaction and trust are paramount to success.
