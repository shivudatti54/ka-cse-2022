# **HCAI Forward: Assessing Trustworthiness, Caring for and Learning from Older Adults**

### Chapter 22: Assessing Trustworthiness

- **Trustworthiness Assessment Framework**: A multi-faceted approach to evaluate the trustworthiness of HCAI systems.
  - Technical trustworthiness: evaluating the system's technical capabilities and reliability.
  - Social trustworthiness: assessing the system's ability to understand and respond to social cues.
  - Ethical trustworthiness: evaluating the system's adherence to ethical principles and values.
- **Trustworthiness Metrics**: Developing metrics to measure trustworthiness, such as:
  - Accuracy metrics (e.g., precision, recall, F1-score)
  - User experience metrics (e.g., user satisfaction, engagement)

### Chapter 24: Caring for Older Adults

- **Human-Centred AI for Care**: Designing HCAI systems that prioritize human well-being and dignity.
  - User-centered design principles
  - Empathy and understanding of older adults' needs and preferences
- **Caregiving and Support**: Developing HCAI systems that provide caregiving and support services, such as:
  - Telecare and telehealth services
  - Personalized care planning and management

### Chapter 25: Learning from Older Adults

- **Knowledge and Expertise**: Leveraging older adults' knowledge and expertise to improve HCAI systems.
  - Intergenerational collaboration and knowledge transfer
  - Co-creation of AI systems with older adults
- **Empowerment and Participation**: Empowering older adults to participate in the development and use of HCAI systems.
  - User empowerment and autonomy
  - Inclusive design principles

### Chapter 26: Future Directions

- **Future Research Directions**: Exploring future research directions for HCAI, including:
  - Ethical considerations and governance
  - Human-centred design principles and methods
  - Impact evaluation and assessment
- **Policy and Regulatory Frameworks**: Developing policy and regulatory frameworks to support the development and deployment of HCAI systems for older adults.
