### Learning Purpose: Suggested Learning Resources

**1. Why is this topic important?**
Identifying and evaluating high-quality resources is a critical meta-skill for any professional, especially in a rapidly evolving field like Human-Centred AI (HCAI). This topic provides the tools to navigate the vast information landscape effectively, ensuring continuous learning beyond the classroom and fostering a habit of evidence-based practice.

**2. What will students learn?**
Students will learn to curate a personalized toolkit of authoritative resources, including key academic journals, foundational books, influential researchers, and industry blogs. They will develop the ability to critically assess the credibility, relevance, and bias of different sources to stay current with emerging HCAI research, methodologies, and ethical debates.

**3. How does it connect to other concepts?**
This topic directly supports all other modules. It provides the pathways to deepen understanding of core concepts like ethical frameworks (Module 2), participatory design (Module 3), and evaluation methods (Module 4). It empowers students to independently explore topics introduced throughout the course and connect them to the latest real-world developments and scholarly discourse.

**4. Real-world applications**
This skill is essential for HCAI practitioners to maintain professional competence, inform their design decisions with current research, anticipate industry trends, and engage in meaningful discourse with peers. It enables lifelong learning, ensuring their work remains ethically grounded and technically relevant in a fast-paced field.
