# Learning Objectives
After studying this topic, you should be able to:
1.  Define stemming and explain its critical role in improving recall within an Information Retrieval (IR) system.
2.  Describe the operational mechanism of a rule-based stemmer like the Porter Stemmer, including the concept of phases and context-sensitive rules.
3.  Differentiate clearly between stemming and lemmatization, highlighting the trade-offs in terms of speed, accuracy, and computational requirements.
4.  Identify and describe different types of research corpora (e.g., raw, annotated, parallel, comparable) and their primary applications in NLP.
5.  Explain the unique challenges involved in developing stemmers and corpora for Indian languages, citing their agglutinative nature and morphological richness.
6.  Illustrate how stemmers and corpora integrate into a standard IR pipeline, from document indexing to query processing.
