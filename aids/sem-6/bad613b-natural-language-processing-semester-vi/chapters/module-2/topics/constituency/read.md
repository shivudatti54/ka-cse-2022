S  -> NP VP         (A sentence is a Noun Phrase followed by a Verb Phrase)
NP -> Det N         (A Noun Phrase is a Determiner followed by a Noun)
NP -> Det Adj N     (A Noun Phrase can also have an Adjective)
VP -> V NP          (A Verb Phrase is a Verb followed by a Noun Phrase)
VP -> V PP          (Or a Verb followed by a Prepositional Phrase)
PP -> P NP          (A Prepositional Phrase is a Preposition followed by a Noun Phrase)

Det -> 'the' | 'a'
N   -> 'fox' | 'dog' | 'student' | 'code'
Adj -> 'quick' | 'brown' | 'lazy'
V   -> 'jumps' | 'wrote' | 'completed'
P   -> 'over' | 'in'
