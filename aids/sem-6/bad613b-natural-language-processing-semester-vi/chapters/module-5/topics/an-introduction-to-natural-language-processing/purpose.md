# Learning Objectives
After studying this topic, you should be able to:
1. Define Natural Language Processing and explain its significance in artificial intelligence
2. Identify and provide examples of different types of linguistic ambiguity
3. Describe the historical development and origins of NLP as a field
4. Explain the unique challenges posed by Indian languages in NLP applications
5. Understand and calculate probabilities in n-gram language models
6. Compare and contrast different grammatical frameworks used in NLP, including the Paninian Framework
