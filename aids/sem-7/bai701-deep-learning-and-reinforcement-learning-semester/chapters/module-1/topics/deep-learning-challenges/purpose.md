# Learning Objectives
After studying this topic, you should be able to:
1. Explain the limitations of traditional NLP approaches and how deep learning addresses them
2. Describe the concept of word embeddings and their advantages over one-hot encoding
3. Compare and contrast the CBOW and Skip-gram architectures in word2vec
4. Explain the training techniques of negative sampling and hierarchical softmax
5. Interpret semantic relationships and analogies using word vector arithmetic
6. Understand visualization techniques for word embeddings and their applications
