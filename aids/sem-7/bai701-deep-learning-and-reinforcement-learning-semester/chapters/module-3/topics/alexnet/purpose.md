# Learning Objectives
After studying this topic, you should be able to:
1.  Describe the historical context and significance of both the LeNet-5 and AlexNet architectures.
2.  Identify and explain the function of each layer type (convolution, pooling, fully connected) in both networks.
3.  Calculate the output dimensions of a convolutional or pooling layer given its parameters.
4.  List and explain the key innovations introduced by AlexNet (ReLU, dropout, GPU training, etc.) and their purpose.
5.  Compare and contrast the LeNet-5 and AlexNet architectures in terms of depth, components, and scale.
6.  Articulate the impact these models had on the field of deep learning and computer vision.
