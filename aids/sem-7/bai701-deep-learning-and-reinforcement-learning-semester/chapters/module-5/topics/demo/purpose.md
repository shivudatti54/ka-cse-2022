### Learning Purpose: Demo of Deep Learning & Reinforcement Learning

**1. Why is this topic important?**
A hands-on demonstration is crucial as it bridges the gap between theoretical concepts and practical implementation. It transforms abstract algorithms into tangible results, solidifying understanding and showcasing the real power and challenges of working with these models.

**2. What will students learn?**
Students will learn how to set up a deep learning or reinforcement learning environment (e.g., using TensorFlow/PyTorch or OpenAI Gym). They will observe the complete workflow: from defining a network/agent and a task, through training and iteration, to evaluating the final model's performance and analyzing its behavior.

**3. How does it connect to other concepts?**
This demo integrates and applies concepts from previous modules, such as neural network architectures (CNNs/RNNs), gradient descent, loss functions, and the fundamentals of RL (like policies, rewards, and value functions). It shows how these components work in unison to solve a problem.

**4. Real-world applications**
The demo will likely showcase applications like image classification with CNNs, natural language processing with RNNs, or teaching an RL agent to play a game/solve a control task. This mirrors real-world uses in autonomous vehicles, personalized recommendations, robotics, and game AI, providing a clear line of sight to industry applications.
