### Learning Purpose: Calculating Sample Means

**1. Why is this topic important?**
The sample mean is a fundamental concept in statistics and machine learning, serving as the most basic estimator for the population mean. It is the cornerstone of exploratory data analysis, providing the first insight into the central tendency of a dataset. Understanding its calculation and properties is crucial before advancing to more complex statistical models and inferential techniques.

**2. What will students learn?**
Students will learn to computationally calculate the sample mean from a given dataset using Python or R. This includes understanding the mathematical formula (Σx_i / n), implementing it efficiently with library functions (e.g., `pandas.DataFrame.mean()`), and interpreting the result in the context of the data.

**3. How does it connect to other concepts?**
This topic is directly foundational for subsequent modules. The sample mean is a key component in calculating variance, standard deviation, and z-scores. It is also the principle behind the Law of Large Numbers and Central Limit Theorem, and it serves as a simple baseline model for supervised learning tasks like regression.

**4. Real-world applications**
Calculating sample means is a ubiquitous first step in data science workflows across all industries. It is used to analyze average customer spend in retail, mean processing time in manufacturing, average patient recovery time in healthcare, and to establish baseline performance metrics for machine learning models before tuning.
