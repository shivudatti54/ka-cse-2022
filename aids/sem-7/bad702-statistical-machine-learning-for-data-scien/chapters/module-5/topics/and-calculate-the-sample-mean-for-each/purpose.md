**Why this topic matters:**
Learning to calculate the sample mean for each feature is essential in statistical machine learning, as it enables the identification of the central tendency of each variable in a dataset. This fundamental concept facilitates the development of discriminant analysis models, which are crucial for classification tasks in various fields. Accurate calculation of sample means enhances the accuracy of predictions and model performance.

**Real-world applications:**
In real-world applications, calculating the sample mean for each feature is crucial in data analysis, particularly in finance, marketing, and healthcare. For instance, in credit scoring models, understanding the average credit score for each demographic group helps lenders make informed decisions. In medical research, analyzing the mean blood pressure for each age group allows researchers to identify patterns and correlations.

**Connection to other concepts:**
Calculating the sample mean for each feature also connects to other statistical concepts, such as covariance and standard deviation, which are essential components of discriminant analysis. Additionally, understanding the sample mean facilitates the interpretation of other statistical measures, like the variance and standard error, which are critical in evaluating model performance and making predictions.
