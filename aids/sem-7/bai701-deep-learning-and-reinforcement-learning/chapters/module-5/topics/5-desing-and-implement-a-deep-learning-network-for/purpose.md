**Why this topic matters:**
Learning to classify textual documents with deep learning networks is crucial in today's data-driven world, as it enables efficient and accurate processing of vast amounts of text data. This skill has significant practical applications across various industries, including natural language processing, information retrieval, and sentiment analysis. Effective text classification can also inform decision-making processes in areas such as marketing, healthcare, and social media monitoring.

**Real-world applications:**
The classification of textual documents has numerous real-world applications, including spam detection in emails, sentiment analysis for customer feedback, and text categorization for news articles. Additionally, this skill is essential for natural language processing tasks, such as language translation, text summarization, and question answering. Deep learning networks can also be applied to areas like text generation, plagiarism detection, and text classification for marketing and advertising.
