# Learning Objectives
After studying this topic, you should be able to:
1.  List and describe the six primary stages of the certificate lifecycle.
2.  Identify common reasons for revoking a digital certificate before its expiration.
3.  Explain how Certificate Revocation Lists (CRLs) work and analyze their advantages and disadvantages.
4.  Explain how the Online Certificate Status Protocol (OCSP) works and analyze its advantages and disadvantages.
5.  Describe the mechanism of OCSP Stapling and articulate the problems it solves compared to standard OCSP.
6.  Compare and contrast the CRL, OCSP, and OCSP Stapling revocation mechanisms across criteria such as latency, privacy, and scalability.
