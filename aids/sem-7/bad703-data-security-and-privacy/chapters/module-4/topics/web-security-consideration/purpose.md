# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define biodiversity and explain its importance
2. Identify the main types of biodiversity and their characteristics
3. Explain the threats to biodiversity and the conservation efforts underway
4. Describe the benefits of biodiversity to humans and the environment
5. Discuss the importance of conservation and the role of humans in maintaining ecosystem services
6. Analyze case studies and examples of biodiversity and conservation efforts
