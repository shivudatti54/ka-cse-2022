### Learning Purpose: Applications of Data Hiding

**1. Why is this topic important?**
In an era of massive data exchange, protecting sensitive information is paramount. Data hiding provides a crucial layer of security beyond conventional encryption by concealing the very existence of data. This topic is vital for understanding how to protect intellectual property, ensure secure communication, and maintain privacy in a digitally connected world, making it a fundamental skill for cybersecurity and IT professionals.

**2. What will students learn?**
Students will learn the core principles and practical techniques for concealing information within various digital carriers like images, audio, and video files (steganography). They will explore methods such as digital watermarking for copyright protection and understand how data hiding is used for covert communication and to add a stealth layer to encrypted data, enhancing overall information security.

**3. How does it connect to other concepts?**
This module builds directly upon foundational knowledge of cryptography (Module 4), demonstrating how data hiding acts as a complementary stealth layer to encryption. It also connects to network security (Module 3) by illustrating methods for secure covert data transmission and to data privacy laws (Module 1/2) by providing technical means to achieve compliance through anonymization and secure storage.

**4. Real-world applications**
Key applications include copyright protection through invisible digital watermarks in media, secure covert messaging channels, adding metadata to medical images for patient privacy, and embedding tamper-proof details in legal documents to prove authenticity.
