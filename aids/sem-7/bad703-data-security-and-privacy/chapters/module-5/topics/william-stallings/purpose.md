### Learning Purpose: William Stallings

**1. Why is this topic important?**
Understanding the work of William Stallings is crucial as he is a foundational author and thought leader in data security. His textbooks and research have shaped the curriculum and best practices used by professionals worldwide. Studying his contributions provides a structured, authoritative framework for grasping complex security principles, from cryptography to network defense.

**2. What will students learn?**
Students will learn the core concepts, models, and protocols detailed in Stallings' work, such as the OSI security architecture, fundamental cryptographic algorithms, and access control mechanisms. They will analyze his systematic approach to designing and implementing secure systems.

**3. How does it connect to other concepts?**
This module directly builds upon prior knowledge of network protocols (Module 3) and risk management (Module 4). It provides the theoretical underpinning and practical standards that will be essential for understanding upcoming topics on security implementation (Module 6) and emerging threats.

**4. Real-world applications**
The principles from Stallings' writings are applied in real-world scenarios including the development of organizational security policies, the selection and configuration of firewalls and intrusion detection systems, and the implementation of encryption for securing data in transit and at rest.
