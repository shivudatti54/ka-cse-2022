# **Revision Notes: Optimization Technique (8 hours)**

## **Definition and Notions**

- Optimization technique: a method used to find the maximum or minimum value of a function.
- Vector calculus: a branch of mathematics that deals with the study of vectors and their properties.

## **Important Formulas and Theorems**

- **Gradient Theorem**: ∇f(x) = (∂f/∂x, ∂f/∂y, ..., ∂f/∂n)
- **Lagrange Multiplier**: λ(f(x) - g(x)) = 0, where λ is the Lagrange multiplier.
- **Optimization Problem**: Find the maximum or minimum value of a function subject to constraints.

## **Key Concepts**

- **Maxima and Minima**: Find the maximum or minimum value of a function.
- **Optimization Techniques**: Gradient Descent, Newton's Method, Lagrange Multiplier Method.
- **Constraint Optimization**: Optimization problems with constraints.

## **Important Topics**

- **Multivariable Optimization**: Optimization of functions of multiple variables.
- **Partial Derivatives**: Derivatives of functions with respect to one variable.
- **Hessian Matrix**: A square matrix of second partial derivatives.

## **Key Results**

- **Weierstrass Theorem**: Every continuous function on a closed and bounded set has a maximum and minimum value.
- **Lagrange's Theorem**: The maximum and minimum values of a function subject to constraints occur at critical points.

## **Revision Tips**

- Practice solving optimization problems and review the formulas and theorems.
- Understand the key concepts and important topics.
- Review the key results and definitions.
