# Learning Objectives
After studying this topic, you should be able to:
1.  Define and differentiate between physical, logical, file system, and cloud acquisition methods.
2.  Explain the concept of forensic soundness and its importance in the acquisition process.
3.  Identify the appropriate acquisition method based on a given scenario (device type, lock status, condition).
4.  List the key challenges associated with acquiring evidence from mobile devices.
5.  Describe the step-by-step process for performing a forensically sound mobile device acquisition.
6.  Understand the legal considerations and requirements for obtaining data from mobile devices and cloud services.
