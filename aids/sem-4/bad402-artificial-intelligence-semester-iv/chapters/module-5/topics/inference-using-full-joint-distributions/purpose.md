### Learning Purpose: Inference using Full Joint Distributions

**1. Why is this topic important?**
This topic is fundamental as it introduces the core probabilistic framework for reasoning under uncertainty in AI. Mastering this provides the mathematical bedrock for more advanced, efficient inference techniques and is crucial for understanding the limits and capabilities of probabilistic models.

**2. What will students learn?**
Students will learn to represent a problem domain as a full joint probability distribution. They will master the process of using this distribution to answer probabilistic queries through enumeration—summing the relevant probabilities to compute exact inferences about unknown variables given evidence.

**3. How does it connect to other concepts?**
This is the foundational application of probability theory to AI, directly connecting to Bayes' Theorem. It sets the stage for subsequent modules where this exact but computationally expensive method is scaled up using more efficient techniques like Bayesian Networks (Module 6) to avoid building the entire joint distribution explicitly.

**4. Real-world applications**
This principle underpins numerous real-world systems, including basic diagnostic systems (e.g., medical or fault diagnosis), simple risk assessment models, and any application requiring a complete and exact probabilistic analysis of a small set of well-defined variables.
