# Learning Objectives
After studying this topic, you should be able to:
1. Explain the concept of non-monotonic reasoning and how it differs from classical logic
2. Describe default reasoning and its applications in handling incomplete information
3. Understand the role and functioning of Truth Maintenance Systems
4. Explain the principles of fuzzy logic and how it represents vague concepts
5. Compare and contrast different symbolic approaches to handling uncertainty
6. Identify appropriate symbolic reasoning methods for different problem types
