# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Define logic and its importance in artificial intelligence.
2. Explain the different types of logic and their applications.
3. Use logical operators and inference rules to solve problems.
4. Describe the different uninformed search strategies and their trade-offs.
5. Apply logical reasoning to solve problems in artificial intelligence.
6. Evaluate the time and space complexity of different search strategies.
