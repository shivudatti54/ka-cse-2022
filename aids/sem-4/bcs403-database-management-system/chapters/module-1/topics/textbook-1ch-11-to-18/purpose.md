Learning "Textbook 1: Ch 1.1 to 1.8" on DATABASE MANAGEMENT SYSTEM is crucial as it provides a foundation in understanding the basic concepts, terminology, and principles of database management, which is essential for designing, implementing, and maintaining databases in various industries.
