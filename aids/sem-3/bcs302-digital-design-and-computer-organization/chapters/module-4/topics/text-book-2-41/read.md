## **4.1 Introduction to Digital Logic**

### 4.1.1 What is Digital Logic?

Digital logic is a branch of mathematics that deals with the manipulation of binary digits (bits) to represent and process information. It is the foundation of digital design and computer organization.

**Definition:** Digital logic is the study of binary systems and the use of logical operations to process and represent information.

**Key Concepts:**

- Binary system: a system that uses only two digits, 0 and 1, to represent information
- Boolean algebra: a mathematical system that deals with logical operations and their combinations
- Digital gates: basic electronic components that perform logical operations

### 4.1.2 Basic Digital Logic Gates

There are three basic digital logic gates:

- **AND Gate:** produces an output of 1 only if all inputs are 1
- **OR Gate:** produces an output of 1 if any input is 1
- **NOT Gate (Inverter):** produces an output that is the opposite of the input

**Table 1: Truth Tables for Basic Digital Logic Gates**

| Gate | Input 0 | Input 1 | Output |
| ---- | ------- | ------- | ------ |
| AND  | 0       | 0       | 0      |
| AND  | 0       | 1       | 0      |
| AND  | 1       | 0       | 0      |
| AND  | 1       | 1       | 1      |
| OR   | 0       | 0       | 0      |
| OR   | 0       | 1       | 1      |
| OR   | 1       | 0       | 1      |
| OR   | 1       | 1       | 1      |
| NOT  | 0       | 1       | 1      |
| NOT  | 1       | 0       | 0      |

### 4.1.3 Digital Circuit Design

A digital circuit is designed using digital logic gates and their combinations. The goal of digital circuit design is to create a circuit that performs a specific function, such as addition or multiplication.

**Key Concepts:**

- Circuit design: the process of designing a digital circuit using digital logic gates
- Digital circuit types: simple circuits, combinational circuits, sequential circuits
- Digital circuit analysis: the process of analyzing the behavior of a digital circuit

### 4.1.4 Digital Circuit Analysis

Digital circuit analysis involves determining the output of a digital circuit for a given set of inputs. There are two types of digital circuit analysis:

- **Combinational analysis:** determines the output of a combinational circuit for a given set of inputs
- **Sequential analysis:** determines the output of a sequential circuit for a given set of inputs

**Key Concepts:**

- Combinational analysis: the process of determining the output of a combinational circuit
- Sequential analysis: the process of determining the output of a sequential circuit
- Digital circuit simulation: the process of simulating the behavior of a digital circuit using software

### 4.1.5 Introduction to Computer Organization

Computer organization is the study of the structure and behavior of a computer system. It involves the design and analysis of digital circuits and the development of computer architecture.

**Key Concepts:**

- Computer organization: the study of the structure and behavior of a computer system
- Digital circuit design: the process of designing a digital circuit using digital logic gates
- Computer architecture: the design and organization of a computer system
