Learning about system structures and what operating systems do is crucial because it provides a foundation for understanding how computer systems manage and interact with hardware resources. This knowledge matters as it enables individuals to design, develop, and maintain efficient and effective operating systems. In real-world applications, understanding system structures is essential for creating secure, scalable, and user-friendly operating systems for various industries, such as gaming, finance, and healthcare. This topic also connects to other concepts, such as computer architecture, memory management, and programming languages, as it provides a deeper understanding of the inner workings of operating systems.
