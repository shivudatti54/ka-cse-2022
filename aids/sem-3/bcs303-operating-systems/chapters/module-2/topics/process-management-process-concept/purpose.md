**Why this topic matters:**
Learning about process management is crucial in understanding how operating systems manage computer resources, handle concurrent tasks, and ensure efficient system performance. This knowledge is essential in designing and implementing efficient systems that can handle multiple tasks simultaneously. It also lays the foundation for understanding more advanced topics in operating systems.

**Real-world applications:**
Process management has numerous real-world applications in various industries, including computer networks, embedded systems, and cloud computing. It is used in managing tasks, threads, and processes in operating systems, allowing for efficient allocation of resources and improved system performance. This knowledge is also used in developing software that can handle multiple tasks concurrently, such as web servers and databases.

**Connection to other concepts:**
Process management is closely related to other concepts in operating systems, such as memory management, thread management, and synchronization. Understanding process management helps in grasping these concepts, as they all work together to manage the execution of tasks in a system. It also lays the foundation for understanding more advanced topics in operating systems, such as job scheduling and process scheduling.
