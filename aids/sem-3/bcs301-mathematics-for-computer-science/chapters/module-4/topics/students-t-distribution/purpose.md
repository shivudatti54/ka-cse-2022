# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Explain the importance of key management in cryptographic systems
2. Compare and contrast symmetric and asymmetric key distribution methods
3. Describe the operation of Key Distribution Centers (KDCs) and their role in symmetric key management
4. Explain the Diffie-Hellman key exchange protocol and its mathematical basis
5. Analyze the structure and components of X.509 digital certificates
6. Evaluate different key distribution approaches and their appropriate applications
