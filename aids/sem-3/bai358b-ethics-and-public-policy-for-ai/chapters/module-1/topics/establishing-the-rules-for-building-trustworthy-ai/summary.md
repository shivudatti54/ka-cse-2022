# **Establishing the Rules for Building Trustworthy AI**

**Module:** An Ethical Framework for a Good AI Society
**Topic:** Establishing the Rules for Building Trustworthy AI (Textbook 1: Chapter 3)

**Key Points:**

- **Trustworthiness of AI**: Definition: An AI system is trustworthy if it behaves as intended, is transparent, accountable, and free from bias.
- **Rules for Building Trustworthy AI**:
  - **Transparency**: Provide clear explanations of AI decision-making processes.
  - **Accountability**: Assign responsibility for AI system outcomes.
  - **Fairness**: Ensure AI systems are free from bias and discriminatory practices.
  - **Explainability**: Develop techniques to interpret AI model decisions.
  - **Security**: Protect AI systems from unauthorized access and data breaches.
- **Formulas and Theorems:**
  - **Shapley Value**: Measures the contribution of each feature to the model's predictions.
  - **Lift**: Measures the improvement in performance due to feature selection.
  - **Fairness Metrics**: Includes metrics such as demographic parity, equalized odds, and calibration.
- **Recommendations:**
  - **Develop and Implement Ethics Guidelines**: Establish and enforce guidelines for AI development and deployment.
  - **Conduct Regular Audits and Testing**: Regularly test and audit AI systems for bias, fairness, and security.
  - **Foster Transparency and Explainability**: Develop techniques to explain AI model decisions and provide transparent explanations of AI system behavior.

**Important Concepts:**

- **Value Alignment**: Ensuring AI systems align with human values and ethics.
- **Robustness**: Ensuring AI systems can withstand adversarial attacks and unexpected events.
- **Human Oversight**: Ensuring human oversight and review of AI system decision-making processes.
