### Learning Purpose for Pie Charts in Data Analytics with R

Upon completion of this topic, students will be able to:
*   **Explain** the appropriate and inappropriate scenarios for using pie charts based on principles of effective data visualization.
*   **Construct** a pie chart in R using the `ggplot2` library, customizing elements such as colors, labels, and titles.
*   **Critique** the use of pie charts in real-world data visualizations, identifying their strengths and weaknesses in communicating information.
*   **Create** alternative visualizations (e.g., bar charts) in R that more effectively represent the data when a pie chart is unsuitable.
