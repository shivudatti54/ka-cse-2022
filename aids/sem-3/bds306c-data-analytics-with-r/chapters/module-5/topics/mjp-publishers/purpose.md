# Learning Purpose: MJP Publishers Case Study

**1. Why is this topic important?**
This topic is crucial as it provides a comprehensive, end-to-end case study that mirrors a real-world business analytics scenario. It moves beyond isolated techniques to demonstrate how to integrate the entire data analysis workflow—from data import and wrangling to visualization and communication—to solve a complex business problem, building essential job-ready skills.

**2. What will students learn?**
Students will learn to apply their R programming skills to a complete business problem. They will perform data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), and create statistical models to analyze sales performance and customer behavior for MJP Publishers. The module focuses on deriving actionable insights and effectively presenting findings to inform business strategy.

**3. How does it connect to other concepts?**
This module synthesizes concepts from all previous modules. It requires the application of data import (`readr`), manipulation (`dplyr`, `tidyr`), visualization (`ggplot2`), and statistical modeling skills in a single, cohesive project. It serves as a capstone experience, reinforcing how these individual techniques work together in practice.

**4. Real-world applications**
The skills practiced are directly transferable to roles like Data Analyst, Business Intelligence Analyst, and Marketing Analyst. Students will be equipped to tackle similar problems in e-commerce, retail, and marketing, analyzing sales trends, customer segmentation, and product performance to drive data-driven decision-making.
