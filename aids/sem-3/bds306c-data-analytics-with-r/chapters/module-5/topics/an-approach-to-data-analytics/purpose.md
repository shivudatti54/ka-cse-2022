### Learning Purpose: An Approach to Data Analytics

**1. Why is this topic important?**
This topic is crucial because it provides the foundational framework for the entire data analytics lifecycle. Understanding a structured approach ensures analyses are methodical, reproducible, and yield actionable, trustworthy results, moving beyond ad-hoc scripting to professional-grade work.

**2. What will students learn?**
Students will learn a standardized workflow for tackling data problems, such as the OSEMN (Obtain, Scrub, Explore, Model, iNterpret) framework. This includes defining a clear analytical goal, data acquisition and cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA) with visualization, model building, and effectively communicating insights.

**3. How does it connect to other concepts?**
This module synthesizes all previous technical skills (e.g., data wrangling with `dplyr`, visualization with `ggplot2`) into a cohesive, end-to-end process. It sets the stage for subsequent modules on advanced modeling and reporting by establishing the necessary context and structure for those techniques.

**4. Real-world applications**
This systematic approach is directly applicable to domains like business intelligence (e.g., analyzing customer churn), scientific research (e.g., processing experimental data), and public policy (e.g., evaluating program effectiveness), ensuring findings are robust and defensible.
