**Important Formulas and Definitions**

- **Mean**: $\mu = \frac{\sum x_i}{n}$
- **Median**: $M = \frac{x_{n/2} + x_{(n+1)/2}}{2}$ (for even n)
- **Mode**: $M = \text{most frequent value}$
- **Variance**: $\sigma^2 = \frac{\sum (x_i - \mu)^2}{n}$
- **Standard Deviation**: $\sigma = \sqrt{\frac{\sum (x_i - \mu)^2}{n}}$

**Key Theorems**

- **Central Limit Theorem**: $\bar{X} \xrightarrow{d} N(\mu, \frac{\sigma^2}{n})$
- **Law of Large Numbers**: $\frac{\bar{X}_n}{n} \xrightarrow{p} \mu$
