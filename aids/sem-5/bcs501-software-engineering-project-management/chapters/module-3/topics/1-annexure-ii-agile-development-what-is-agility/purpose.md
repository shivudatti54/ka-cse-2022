Learning about Agile Development, specifically Annexure II, is crucial as it provides a framework for managing complex projects and delivering value to stakeholders in a rapidly changing environment. This topic matters because it equips project managers and team members with the skills to adapt to uncertainty, prioritize tasks, and ensure flexibility in project planning and execution. Real-world applications include software development, IT service management, and product management in various industries. This knowledge connects to other concepts in software engineering and project management, such as Scrum, Kanban, and Waterfall methodologies.
