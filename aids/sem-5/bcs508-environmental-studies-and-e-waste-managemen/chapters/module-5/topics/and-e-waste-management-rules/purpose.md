# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1.  List and explain the five tiers of the waste hierarchy in their correct order of preference.
2.  Compare and contrast the advantages and disadvantages of landfilling, incineration, recycling, and composting.
3.  Describe the key engineering features of a modern sanitary landfill and explain their purpose.
4.  Differentiate between Waste-to-Energy recovery and simple incineration without energy recovery.
5.  Explain the role of policy instruments (like EPR) and public participation in achieving effective waste management.
6.  Define Integrated Waste Management (IWM) and argue for its importance over reliance on a single method.
