# Sustainability: 17 SDG Targets and Possible Actions

## **Introduction**

The United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) provide a framework for achieving a more sustainable and equitable world. The 17 SDGs are interconnected and interdependent, and each goal has several targets that aim to address specific global challenges. This study material focuses on the 17 SDG targets related to sustainability, with a focus on environmental studies and e-waste management.

## **What is Sustainability?**

Sustainability is the ability of a system, process, or activity to endure over time without degrading the environment or resource base. It involves meeting the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs.

## **17 SDG Targets Related to Sustainability**

### 1. No Poverty (SDG 1)

- Target 1.1: End poverty in all its forms everywhere
- Target 1.2: Achieve full and productive employment and decent work for all
- Possible actions:
  - Implement policies to reduce poverty and inequality
  - Invest in education and job training programs
  - Support microfinance initiatives to promote economic growth

### 2. Zero Hunger (SDG 2)

- Target 2.1: End hunger, achieve food security and improved nutrition, and promote sustainable agriculture
- Target 2.2: Implement sustainable agriculture practices to reduce greenhouse gas emissions
- Possible actions:
  - Promote agroecology and sustainable agriculture practices
  - Support small-scale farmers through access to markets and finance
  - Implement programs to reduce food waste and improve nutrition

### 3. Good Health and Well-being (SDG 3)

- Target 3.1: Ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages
- Target 3.2: Achieve universal health coverage
- Possible actions:
  - Implement policies to improve access to healthcare services
  - Promote healthy lifestyles and preventable diseases
  - Support research and development of new medical technologies

### 4. Quality Education (SDG 4)

- Target 4.1: Ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning
- Target 4.2: Improve the quality of basic education
- Possible actions:
  - Implement policies to improve access to education, particularly for marginalized groups
  - Promote inclusive education and support students with disabilities
  - Invest in teacher training and development

### 5. Gender Equality (SDG 5)

- Target 5.1: Achieve gender equality and empower all women and girls
- Target 5.2: Implement policies to promote women's participation in politics and decision-making
- Possible actions:
  - Implement policies to promote gender equality in education and employment
  - Support women's entrepreneurship and small business development
  - Implement programs to prevent violence against women and girls

### 6. Clean Water and Sanitation (SDG 6)

- Target 6.1: Ensure universal and equitable access to safe drinking water and sanitation
- Target 6.2: Implement sustainable water resources management
- Possible actions:
  - Promote water conservation and efficient use
  - Implement policies to improve access to sanitation and hygiene
  - Support water and sanitation infrastructure development

### 7. Affordable and Clean Energy (SDG 7)

- Target 7.1: Ensure access to affordable, reliable, sustainable and modern energy
- Target 7.2: Increase the share of renewable energy in the global energy mix
- Possible actions:
  - Promote renewable energy sources, such as solar and wind power
  - Implement energy-efficient technologies and practices
  - Support energy access and poverty reduction programs

### 8. Decent Work and Economic Growth (SDG 8)

- Target 8.1: Achieve sustainable economic growth and promote full and productive employment
- Target 8.2: Implement policies to promote sustainable tourism and reduce inequality
- Possible actions:
  - Implement policies to promote fair labor practices and reduce exploitation
  - Support entrepreneurship and small business development
  - Invest in education and job training programs

### 9. Industry, Innovation and Infrastructure (SDG 9)

- Target 9.1: Build resilient infrastructure, promote sustainable industries and consumption patterns
- Target 9.2: Implement policies to promote sustainable transportation and reduce emissions
- Possible actions:
  - Promote sustainable transportation and reduce emissions
  - Implement policies to reduce waste and pollution
  - Support research and development of new technologies

### 10. Reduce Inequalities (SDG 10)

- Target 10.1: Reduce inequality within and among countries
- Target 10.2: Implement policies to promote social mobility and reduce poverty
- Possible actions:
  - Implement policies to promote social mobility and reduce poverty
  - Support education and job training programs
  - Implement programs to address discrimination and inequality

### 11. Cities and Human Settlements (SDG 11)

- Target 11.1: Make cities and human settlements inclusive, safe, resilient and sustainable
- Target 11.2: Implement policies to promote sustainable urban planning and reduce emissions
- Possible actions:
  - Implement policies to promote sustainable urban planning and reduce emissions
  - Support affordable housing and urban development
  - Promote community engagement and participation in urban planning

### 12. Responsible Consumption and Production (SDG 12)

- Target 12.1: Achieve sustainable consumption and production patterns
- Target 12.2: Implement policies to promote sustainable agriculture and reduce food waste
- Possible actions:
  - Implement policies to reduce waste and pollution
  - Promote sustainable agriculture practices
  - Support education and awareness-raising campaigns

### 13. Climate Action (SDG 13)

- Target 13.1: Take urgent action to combat climate change
- Target 13.2: Implement policies to promote renewable energy and reduce emissions
- Possible actions:
  - Implement policies to reduce emissions and promote renewable energy
  - Support climate change adaptation and resilience efforts
  - Invest in research and development of new climate technologies

### 14. Life Below Water (SDG 14)

- Target 14.1: Conserve and sustainably use the world's oceans, seas and marine resources for sustainable development
- Target 14.2: Implement policies to reduce marine pollution and protect marine ecosystems
- Possible actions:
  - Implement policies to reduce marine pollution and protect marine ecosystems
  - Support sustainable fishing practices and reduce overfishing
  - Promote marine conservation and protected areas

### 15. Life on Land (SDG 15)

- Target 15.1: Protect, restore and promote sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems, forests, agriculture, and wildlife
- Target 15.2: Implement policies to reduce deforestation and promote sustainable land use
- Possible actions:
  - Implement policies to reduce deforestation and promote sustainable land use
  - Support reforestation and afforestation efforts
  - Promote sustainable agriculture practices and reduce pollution

### 16. Peace, Justice and Strong Institutions (SDG 16)

- Target 16.1: Promote peaceful and inclusive societies for sustainable development
- Target 16.2: Implement policies to promote access to justice and human rights
- Possible actions:
  - Implement policies to promote peaceful and inclusive societies
  - Support access to justice and human rights
  - Promote education and awareness-raising campaigns

### 17. Partnerships for Goals (SDG 17)

- Target 17.1: Strengthen the means of implementation and revitalize the global partnership for sustainable development
- Target 17.2: Implement policies to promote sustainable development partnerships
- Possible actions:
  - Implement policies to promote sustainable development partnerships
  - Support international cooperation and collaboration
  - Promote education and awareness-raising campaigns

## Conclusion

The 17 SDG targets related to sustainability are interconnected and interdependent, and each target has several possible actions that can be taken to achieve the goal. By implementing these actions, we can work towards creating a more sustainable and equitable world for all.
