# Learning Purpose: Continuous Internal Evaluation

**1. Why is this topic important?**
Continuous Internal Evaluation (CIE) is crucial because it moves beyond a single, high-stakes final exam to assess student progress. This ongoing process provides timely feedback, identifies learning gaps early, and allows for instructional adjustments, fostering a more supportive and effective learning environment for mastering complex data analysis skills.

**2. What will students learn?**
Students will learn how their performance is measured through various CIE components like quizzes, assignments, mini-projects, and presentations. They will understand the criteria for assessment and how to utilize feedback for improvement. This empowers them to take ownership of their learning journey in EDA.

**3. How does it connect to other concepts?**
CIE directly connects to all prior EDA modules (e.g., data cleaning, visualization, statistical summaries). It assesses the practical application of these concepts. Success in CIE tasks demonstrates a student's ability to synthesize techniques from across the course to analyze real datasets comprehensively.

**4. Real-world applications**
The iterative process of performing analysis, receiving feedback, and refining work mirrors the agile, collaborative nature of a professional data analyst or scientist. CIE prepares students for a career where projects are constantly reviewed and improved upon, ensuring robust, reliable, and impactful data-driven insights.
