### Learning Purpose: E-Waste Management in New Delhi
#### 1. Importance of the Topic
E-Waste management is crucial due to the rapid increase in electronic waste, posing significant environmental and health risks.

#### 2. Student Learning Outcomes
Students will learn about the current state of e-waste in New Delhi, management practices, and the impact of improper disposal on the environment and human health.

#### 3. Connection to Other Concepts
This topic connects to broader environmental studies concepts, including waste management, pollution, and sustainability, providing a comprehensive understanding of urban environmental challenges.

#### 4. Real-World Applications
Understanding e-waste management in New Delhi has real-world implications for developing effective policies, practices, and technologies to mitigate the environmental and health impacts of e-waste, contributing to a more sustainable future.
