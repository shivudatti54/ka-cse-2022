# Learning Objectives
After studying this topic, you should be able to:
1. Classify different types of forests based on climate, vegetation, and management approaches
2. Explain the ecological, economic, and socio-cultural functions of forest resources
3. Analyze the major threats to forest resources and their environmental consequences
4. Evaluate various forest conservation and management strategies at local, national, and international levels
5. Describe the role of community participation and indigenous knowledge in sustainable forest management
6. Apply concepts of sustainable forest management to real-world conservation challenges
