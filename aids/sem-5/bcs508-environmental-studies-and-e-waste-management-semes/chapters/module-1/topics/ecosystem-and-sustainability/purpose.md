# Learning Objectives
After studying this topic, you should be able to:
1. Define "carbon footprint" and "carbon dioxide equivalent (CO₂e)" and explain their significance in environmental studies.
2. Differentiate between Scope 1, Scope 2, and Scope 3 emissions with clear examples for each category.
3. Identify the major greenhouse gases, their primary anthropogenic sources, and their relative Global Warming Potential (GWP).
4. Analyze the direct link between human activities, increased carbon footprint, and the observed effects of climate change.
5. Evaluate and propose mitigation strategies to reduce carbon footprint at individual, corporate, and policy levels.
6. Critically assess the role of technology and innovation in achieving sustainability goals related to carbon emissions.
