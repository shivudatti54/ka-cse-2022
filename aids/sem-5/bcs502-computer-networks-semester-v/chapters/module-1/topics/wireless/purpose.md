# Learning Objectives
After studying this topic, you should be able to:
1. Identify and explain common wireless attack techniques including eavesdropping, evil twin, and deauthentication attacks
2. Analyze the vulnerabilities in WEP, WPA, and WPA2 security protocols that enable various attacks
3. Describe the process of WEP and WPA-PSK cracking attacks step by step
4. Explain how KRACK (Key Reinstallation Attack) works against WPA2 networks
5. Differentiate between passive and active wireless attacks with examples
6. Recommend appropriate defensive measures for each type of wireless attack
