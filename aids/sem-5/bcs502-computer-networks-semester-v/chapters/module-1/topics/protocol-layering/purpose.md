# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1.  Understand the concept of protocol layering and its importance in computer networks.
2.  Describe the TCP/IP model and its four layers.
3.  Describe the OSI model and its seven layers.
4.  Compare and contrast the TCP/IP and OSI models.
5.  Identify the functions of each layer in the TCP/IP and OSI models.
6.  Explain the key concepts of protocol layering, including protocol and layering.
