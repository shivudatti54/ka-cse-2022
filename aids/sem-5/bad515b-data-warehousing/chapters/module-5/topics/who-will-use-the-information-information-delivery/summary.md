# Who Will Use The Information? Information Delivery

==============================================

## Overview

---

- The purpose of information delivery is to make data accessible to authorized users.
- Effective information delivery is crucial for successful data warehousing.

## Key Points

---

- **Information Users**:
  - Users who will use the information
  - Can be internal (e.g., managers, analysts) or external (e.g., customers, partners)
- **Information Delivery Models**:
  - Push-based (e.g., sales, marketing)
  - Pull-based (e.g., reporting, analytics)
- **Factors Affecting Information Delivery**:
  - User needs and expectations
  - Information quality
  - Technical capabilities
- **Benefits of Effective Information Delivery**:
  - Improved decision-making
  - Increased productivity
  - Better stakeholder satisfaction

## Definitions

---

- **Information Quality**: The degree to which data is accurate, complete, and relevant.
- **Data Accessibility**: The ability to retrieve and use data by authorized users.

## Theorems/ Formulas

---

- **Foggy Data Model**:
  - A data model that does not account for the needs of users.
  - Can lead to poor data quality and ineffective information delivery.
- **Information Quality Framework**:
  - A structured approach to evaluating and improving information quality.
  - Typically includes criteria such as accuracy, completeness, and relevance.

## Best Practices

---

- Conduct user research to understand information needs and expectations.
- Ensure data quality and integrity.
- Provide multiple channels for information delivery (e.g., web, mobile, print).

## Revision Tips

---

- Focus on key points and benefits of effective information delivery.
