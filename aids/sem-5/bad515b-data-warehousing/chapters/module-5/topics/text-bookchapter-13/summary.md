# **Text Book: Chapter 13 Revision Notes**

## **Data Quality: A Key To Success**

### Why Data Quality is Critical

- Ensures accuracy and reliability of data
- Prevents errors and inconsistencies
- Supports informed decision-making
- Enhances data-driven business strategy
- Reduces data-related risks and liabilities

### Data Quality Challenges

- Definition and measurement of data quality
- Establishing data quality standards
- Ensuring data quality across the organization

### Quality Attributes of Data

---

- Accuracy
- Completeness
- Consistency
- Relevance
- Timeliness

### Data Quality Metrics

---

- Data quality score (DQS)
- Data quality index (DQI)
- Data quality rating (DQR)

### Theoretical Background

---

- **Data Quality Framework** (DQF): a structured approach to managing data quality
- **Data Quality Model** (DQM): a theoretical framework for understanding data quality

### Important Formulas and Definitions

---

- **Data Quality Index (DQI)**: measures the overall quality of data
- **Data Quality Score (DQS)**: measures the accuracy and completeness of data
- **Information Quality (IQ)**: the quality of information as it pertains to its correctness, usefulness, and reliability.

### Key Theorems

---

- **The Law of Total Quality**: the quality of the whole is a function of the sum of the qualities of its parts.
- **The Law of Data Quality**: the quality of the data is a function of the quality of the data sources.

### Quick Revision Tips

---

- Review the definitions and formulas of data quality attributes, metrics, and models
- Practice calculating data quality scores and indices
- Familiarize yourself with the laws of total quality and data quality.
