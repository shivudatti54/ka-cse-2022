Learning "Textbook-2: Chap-5 (5.1 to 5.4)" on the topic of Image Restoration and Reconstruction is crucial as it deals with the degradation and restoration of images, which is a fundamental aspect of Computer Vision. This topic matters as it enables the development of algorithms and techniques that can recover distorted or degraded images, which has significant implications in various fields such as medical imaging, surveillance, and photography. Real-world applications of this topic include image denoising, deblurring, and super-resolution, which are essential in various industries. This topic also connects to other concepts in Computer Vision, such as image processing, feature extraction, and object recognition.
