# Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1. Explain the fundamental concept of Fourier Transform and its importance in image processing
2. Differentiate between spatial domain and frequency domain representations of images
3. Apply Fourier Transform to images and interpret the resulting frequency spectrum
4. Implement frequency domain filtering operations (low-pass, high-pass, band-pass)
5. Utilize the convolution theorem for efficient filtering operations
6. Analyze the computational advantages of FFT over direct DFT implementation
