# Learning Objectives
After studying this topic, you should be able to:
1. State the Pumping Lemma for context-free languages precisely
2. Understand the intuition behind why the Pumping Lemma holds for all context-free languages
3. Apply the Pumping Lemma to prove that specific languages are not context-free
4. Identify appropriate strings to use when applying the Pumping Lemma
5. Recognize the limitations of the Pumping Lemma in language classification
6. Differentiate between the Pumping Lemmas for regular and context-free languages
