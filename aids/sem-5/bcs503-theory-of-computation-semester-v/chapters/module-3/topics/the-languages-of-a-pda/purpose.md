# Learning Objectives
After studying this topic, you should be able to:
1. Define a Pushdown Automaton (PDA) and explain its purpose.
2. Describe how a PDA works and how it recognizes context-free languages.
3. Construct a PDA to recognize a given context-free language.
4. Convert a PDA to a Context-Free Grammar (CFG) and explain the result.
5. Explain the difference between a PDA and a Deterministic Pushdown Automaton (DPDA).
6. Recognize the languages that can be recognized by a PDA and a DPDA.
