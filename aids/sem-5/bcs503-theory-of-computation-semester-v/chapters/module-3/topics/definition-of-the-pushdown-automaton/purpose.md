# Learning Objectives
After studying this topic, you should be able to:
1. Define a Pushdown Automaton and its key components.
2. Explain how a PDA works and how it recognizes a given language.
3. Compare and contrast PDAs with other types of automata.
4. Identify the purpose of the stack, transition function, and final states in a PDA.
5. Recognize the type of language that is recognized by a PDA.
6. Apply the concepts of PDAs to solve problems and analyze languages.
