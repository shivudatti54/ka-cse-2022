# Learning Objectives
After studying this topic, you should be able to:

1.  Define and explain the basic concepts of finite automata, including DFA and NFA.
2.  Define and explain the basic concepts of regular expressions, including union, concatenation, and Kleene closure.
3.  Convert regular expressions to finite automata and vice versa.
4.  Solve problems involving finite automata and regular expressions.
5.  Explain the relationship between finite automata and regular expressions.
6.  Identify the advantages and disadvantages of using finite automata over regular expressions.
