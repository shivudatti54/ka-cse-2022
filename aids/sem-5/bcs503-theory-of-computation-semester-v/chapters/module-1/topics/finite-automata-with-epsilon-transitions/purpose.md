# Learning Objectives
After studying this topic, you should be able to:
1. Define and explain the concept of epsilon-transitions in finite automata.
2. Identify the differences between DFA and NFA.
3. Convert an NFA to a DFA using the subset construction method.
4. Simplify the transition diagram of a finite automaton using epsilon-transitions.
5. Explain the purpose and application of epsilon-transitions in finite automata.
6. Analyze and design finite automata with epsilon-transitions to recognize specific languages.
