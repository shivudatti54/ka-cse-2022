# Learning Objectives
After studying this topic, you should be able to:
1. Define resource sharing and explain its importance in distributed systems
2. Identify and describe different types of transparency in distributed systems
3. Explain the challenges associated with resource sharing in distributed environments
4. Describe the architecture and components of Remote Procedure Call (RPC) systems
5. Compare and contrast RPC with Remote Method Invocation (RMI)
6. Analyze request-reply protocols and their implementation considerations
7. Apply resource sharing concepts to real-world distributed systems scenarios
