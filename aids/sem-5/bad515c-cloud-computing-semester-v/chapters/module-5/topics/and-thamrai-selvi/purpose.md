### Learning Purpose: Module 5 - Cloud Management and Governance

**1. Why is this topic important?**
This topic is critical because managing and governing cloud resources is fundamental to ensuring security, cost-effectiveness, and operational efficiency. Without proper management frameworks, organizations risk security breaches, uncontrolled spending ("cloud waste"), and compliance failures.

**2. What will students learn?**
Students will learn the core principles and best practices for cloud management, including cost management strategies, resource monitoring, identity and access management (IAM), and establishing governance frameworks. This includes understanding tools like AWS CloudWatch, Azure Cost Management, and compliance standards.

**3. How does it connect to other concepts?**
This module builds directly upon earlier knowledge of cloud service models (IaaS, PaaS, SaaS) and deployment strategies. It provides the operational "how-to" for implementing the architectures and services covered in previous modules, linking technical deployment to business and security objectives.

**4. Real-world applications**
These skills are directly applicable to roles such as Cloud Architect or DevOps Engineer. Students will be equipped to design policies for automated resource scaling, implement security controls to protect data, create budgets and alerts to control costs, and ensure an organization's cloud environment is audit-ready.
