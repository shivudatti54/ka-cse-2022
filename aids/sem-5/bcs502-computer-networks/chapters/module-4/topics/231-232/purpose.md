Learning "23.1- 23.2" on the transport layer is crucial as it enables students to understand the fundamental principles of data transmission and reception in computer networks, ensuring reliable and efficient communication between devices. This topic matters because it lays the groundwork for the development of higher-level protocols such as TCP/IP, which govern data transfer over the internet. In real-world applications, this knowledge is essential for designing and implementing robust network systems, such as online banking and e-commerce platforms. This topic also builds upon concepts learned in the introduction to the transport layer and user topics, further solidifying students' understanding of computer networks.
