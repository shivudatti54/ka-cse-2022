# Sparse Matrices
This content enables students to understand, implement, and analyze linked-list-based sparse matrix representations. Students will learn the multilist (orthogonal list) structure, understand the trade-offs between row-major and column-major representations, and develop proficiency in implementing core operations (insertion, deletion, traversal, search, transpose, addition) with formal time and space complexity analysis. The content prepares students for application-based problem-solving and algorithmic analysis expected at the students/students/students level.
